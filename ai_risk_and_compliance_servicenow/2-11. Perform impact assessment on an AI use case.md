# Perform Impact Assessment on an AI Use Case

## Overview

Perform an impact assessment of an AI use case to identify risks such as copyright issues, bias, privacy breaches, misinformation, or surveillance. This enables improved oversight and risk management.

## Before You Begin

**Role required:**
- AI Asset Owner, or
- `sn_grc_ai_gov.ai_risk_and_compliance_business_user`

## Procedure

1. Navigate to **All > AI Control Tower > AI assets**.
2. Open the required AI system from the list.
3. In the **Assess** stage of the AI Asset Life cycle, select the task for the impact assessment.
4. Select **Take assessment**.
5. **Optional:** To reassign the impact assessment to another user, select **Reassign** and do the following:
   1. In the **Reassign task** dialog box, fill in the fields as appropriate.

      | Field | Description |
      |-------|-------------|
      | Assignee | Name of the user to whom you want to reassign the task. |
      | Additional comments | Additional information stating the reason for reassignment. |

   2. Select **Reassign**.
6. **Optional:** To initiate a sidebar discussion, select the **more** icon, and select **Discuss**.
   1. In the **Start a Sidebar discussion** dialog box, fill in the fields as appropriate.

      | Field | Description |
      |-------|-------------|
      | Subject | Option to provide a title for the sidebar discussion. |
      | Add participants | Options to add participants for the sidebar discussion. **Note:** You can include any users with access to this record in this discussion. |
      | Include a brief message for participants | Brief summary about the feedback to be discussed in the sidebar discussion. |

   2. Select **Start discussion**.
7. To initiate the assessment, select **Start**.
8. Review any instructions or reference information.
   - You can see additional question guidance text for your review as you complete the questions.
9. Answer all relevant questions in the questionnaire.
   - Your responses are automatically saved, and based on them, relevant risk statements and control objectives are associated with the assessment.
   - After the AI Risk and Compliance Analyst marks the assessment state as **Closed complete**, risks and controls are generated from these risk statements and control objectives respectively and are mapped to the AI asset.

   > **Note:** The system automatically maps risks and controls only when you use the default assessment questionnaire.
10. Select **Submit**.

## How Risks and Controls Are Produced

- As you answer questions, your responses are automatically saved.
- Based on the responses, relevant **risk statements** and **control objectives** are associated with the assessment.
- Once an AI Risk and Compliance Analyst marks the assessment state as **Closed complete**:
  - **Risks** are generated from the risk statements.
  - **Controls** are generated from the control objectives.
  - Both are mapped to the AI asset.
- Automatic mapping of risks and controls happens **only** when the default assessment questionnaire is used.

## What to Do Next

- An AI Risk and Compliance Analyst evaluates the completed assessment along with the suggested list of risks and controls derived from the submitted information.
- Proceed to **Initiate risk assessment on AI asset**.
