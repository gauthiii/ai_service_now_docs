# Configure email-based intake for AI Risk and Compliance

Configure email-based intake so that incoming email messages create intake records in AI Risk and Compliance.

## Overview

You can submit AI-related cases or inquiries by sending emails to configured addresses. When the system receives an email, it automatically processes the message by extracting key information — such as the subject, body, and sender — and creates the corresponding **AI Case** or **Inquiry**. This ensures submissions are captured consistently and made available for review and action.

Email-based intake requires two things working together:

- **Inbound email processing** enabled at the instance level.
- **Intake configuration** defined in AI Case Management.

Together, these determine how the system processes incoming emails and which intake types the system creates.

## Before you begin

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Roles required | `admin` and `sn_ai_case_mgmt.ai_case_admin` |

## How inbound email creates intake records

- Emails are sent to configured group email addresses (and the instance email address).
- The system extracts the subject, body, and sender from each incoming message.
- Based on the intake configuration, the system creates the corresponding AI Case or Inquiry record.

## Email properties

These instance-level properties must be enabled for inbound email processing:

| Property name | System property | Setting |
|---------------|-----------------|---------|
| Email receiving enabled | `glide.email.smtp.active` | Yes |
| Automatically create users for incoming emails from trusted domains | `glide.pop3readerjob.create_caller` | Yes |

## Procedure

1. Navigate to **All > System Properties > Email Properties**.
2. Enable inbound email processing on the instance by selecting **Yes** for the **Email receiving enabled** (`glide.email.smtp.active`) and **Automatically create users for incoming emails from trusted domains** (`glide.pop3readerjob.create_caller`) properties.
   - This enables inbound email processing so the instance can receive and evaluate incoming email messages.
3. Navigate to **All > AI Case Management > Case Type**.
4. Select the **Inbound Email Configuration** related list and enter the group email address configured for inbound email.
   - Configuring the **Group email** field on the AI case intake type record sets the email address used for case intake.
5. Navigate to **All > AI Case Management > Inquiry Type**.
6. Select the **Inbound Email Configuration** related list and enter the group email address configured for inbound email.
   - Configuring the **Group email** field on the AI inquiry intake type record sets the email address used for inquiry intake.

## Result

Emails sent to the configured group email address and the instance email address create corresponding intake records in AI Risk and Compliance.
