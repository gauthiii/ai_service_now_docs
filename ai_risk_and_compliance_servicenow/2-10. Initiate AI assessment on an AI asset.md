# Initiate AI Assessment on an AI Asset

Initiate an AI assessment for the whole AI asset.

## Role Required

- **AI Asset Owner** or **AI Risk and Compliance Analyst**
  `[sn_grc_ai_gov.ai_risk_and_compliance_analyst]`

## Before You Begin

Ensure you have the required role listed above. The assessment template you intend to use must already be published. For information about publishing, see *Publish the assessment templates*.

## Procedure

1. Navigate to **All > AI Risk and Compliance > AI Risk and Compliance Workspace**.
2. Select the list icon.
3. From the List, open the AI asset for which you want to initiate the AI assessment.
4. From the **More actions** menu, select **Initiate assessment**.
5. On the **Send AI assessment** dialog box, fill in the fields.

   ### Send AI Assessment Form

   | Field | Description |
   |-------|-------------|
   | **Assessment template** | The assessment template you use to perform the AI assessment. You can see the list of all published assessment templates in this drop-down list. For information about publishing, see *Publish the assessment templates*. |
   | **Related entity** | Entity that needs to be analyzed for impact assessment. This field is automatically set to the AI asset's related entity. |
   | **Assigned to** | Person to whom the assessment is assigned. This field is auto-populated with the business owner responsible for owning and managing the AI asset. |
   | **Due date** | Date by which the assessment needs to be completed. |
   | **Title** | Title of the AI assessment. |
   | **Description** | Description of the AI assessment. |

6. Select **Send**.
