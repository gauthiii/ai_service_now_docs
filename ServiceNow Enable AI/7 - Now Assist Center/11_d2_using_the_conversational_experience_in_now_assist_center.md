### Using the conversational experience in Now Assist Center

Use the Now Assist panel to perform Now Assist administration and setup tasks through a
conversational interface directly in Now Assist Center.
The Now Assist panel appears pinned along the right side of the browser by default, and is
accessible from every page in the workspace. You can interact with your AI companion by typing
questions and instructions in plain language.
