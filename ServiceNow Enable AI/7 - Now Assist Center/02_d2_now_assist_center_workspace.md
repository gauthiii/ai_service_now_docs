### Now Assist Center workspace

Use the Now Assist Center workspace to set up, monitor, and manage your Now Assist solutions.

### Overview of the Now Assist Center workspace

The Now Assist Center workspace provides features that enable you to set up of your AI
solutions in a unified experience without switching between separate Now Assist applications.
With the Now Assist Center workspace, you can:
- Access your integrated Now Assist applications and features using the side navigation bar.
- Use the home page features to set up Now Assist solutions on your instance, find and activate
automation opportunities, and track Now Assist performance.
- Use the conversational interface in the Now Assist panel as your AI assistant to perform
common setup, building, and question answering tasks.

### Access the Now Assist Center workspace

To access the Now Assist Center workspace, navigate to All > Now Assist Center or
Workspaces > Now Assist Center.
The first time you access Now Assist Center, a welcome message displays prompting you to get
started by installing essential Now Assist plugins. If you already have these plugins installed,
the message simply introduces the benefits of Now Assist Center. Select Let’s get started to
continue to the home page.

### Home page

The Now Assist Center home page provides features to quickly set up your Now Assist
implementation, find automation opportunities, track performance of Now Assist solutions, and
access related Now Assist applications.
Your home page is tailored to suit your specific environment, role, and business objectives. The
appearance your home page and its sections display accordingly. The entitlements, Impact
subscription, plugin installations, and data associated with your instance may affect your
experience on the home page.
The following sections may appear on your home page based on your instance.

### Actionable use cases

quick-start use cases. Each use case is tailored to your environment and proposed to help you
implement AI adoption, best practices, and administrative setup on the platform.

Actionable use cases section
Select Activate on the card to begin, and a conversation will open in the Now Assist panel. In the
Now Assist panel, you can use natural language to have your AI companion implement the use
case.
After it is implemented, the card disappears and the new solution appears under the Recently
activated AI section of the home page.
For more information, see Activating actionable use cases from Now Assist Center.

### Automation opportunities

This section shows the most relevant opportunities to automate actions or workflows in your
instance using AI. The opportunities appear as actionable cards, each showing the results of an
assessment performed by AI Agent Advisor.
Automation opportunities section
AI Agent Advisor analyzes your instance data to identify the leading AI opportunities, estimates
the potential time savings for each opportunity, and finds the AI agents needed to support the
automation.
Select View all to see a list of all the opportunities identified by AI Agent Advisor.
For more information, see Using AI Agent Advisor in Now Assist Center.

### AI readiness

The Now Assist AI readiness section shows the readiness assessments for your environment.
Each assessment helps to determine whether your organization's instance is ready to implement
generative AI and agentic AI features in Now Assist. Explore actionable insights to enhance your
data, applications, configurations, and workflows, improving the quality of your AI outcomes.
AI readiness section
Select Start AI readiness assessment to run the assessment of your instance. After the
assessment is complete, the results display in a card showing your overall AI readiness status.
Overall AI readiness assessment
Select View Assessment to see the assessment on a separate workspace tab.
For more information, see Using Now Assist Readiness Evaluation in Now Assist Center.

### Now Assist panel

The Now Assist panel is the conversational interface where you can interact with the AI
companion to perform setup, solution building, and question answering tasks.
The AI companion in the Now Assist panel uses generative AI to accomplish Now Assist setup
and configuration tasks on your instance based on your requests. Describe what you want to
accomplish and the AI companion guides you through the process, executes actions with your
confirmation, and answers questions about your instance and the Now Assist products.
Use the Now Assist panel to perform the following types of actions:
- Perform common administrator functions
- Implement the setup and configuration of AI solutions
- Answer questions and find help documentation
- Activate and set up base system skills
- Enable or disable native and machine translation
- Configure language support for supported language models
- View privacy policy details and guidance
- View data sharing details and guidance
- View data overflow processing details and guidance
- View policy and model providers with management guidance
- View active and deprecated AI model versions
- Discover and assign AI agents and agentic workflows that implement use cases
