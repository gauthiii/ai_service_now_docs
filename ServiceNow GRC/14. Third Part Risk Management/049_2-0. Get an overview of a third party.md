Information on the tabs for a third party
On most reports, you can select an item to view the underlying data.
Risk overview tab
The Risk overview tab displays third-party name and process information, summary
reports, risk intelligence scores, and tracking data for issues and tasks. See Viewing
summarized risk information for a third party.
Details tab
The Details tab displays overview and process tracking information and offers
actions for managing the third party or engagement. See Viewing general
information on a third party
Subsidiaries tab
The Subsidiaries tab displays overview information on subsidiaries of the third
party. See Viewing information about third-party subsidiaries
Downstream suppliers tab
The Downstream suppliers tab displays overview information on fourth parties that
the third party engages with. See Viewing information on fourth parties.
Risk intelligence scores tab
For DD requests, risk intelligence scores appear in a list. For an individual third
party, a card displays the most recent score or rating and a link for each risk
intelligence report. See Viewing risk intelligence scores.
Note: When you select the More tab, you can view additional tabs that will show other
types of relevant information such as, associated assessments, issues, tasks, risk
components, entities, entity types, and more.

Information on the Risk overview tab
A: Name and process information
This section provides quick access to tier and contact information.
B: Third-party summary
Risk rating and scoring rules are described in Set up risk rating scales for scoring.
Third-party risk components are external assessments, engagements, and
subsidiaries. The component risk ratings are aggregated and are displayed as the
Risk rating value on the Third party's scorecard.
C: Risk intelligence providers
Risk intelligence scores are described in Set up risk rating scales for scoring and
Viewing risk intelligence scores.
D: Tracking
•Assessments: Counts of assessments in various process statuses. Select a
number to view details.
•Issues: Issues are described in Create an issue for a third party or engagement
and Manage issues.
•Tasks: Tasks are described in Create a task for a third party or engagement and
Manage a task for a third party or engagement.
E: Overview
This section provides quick access to basic information about the third party.
