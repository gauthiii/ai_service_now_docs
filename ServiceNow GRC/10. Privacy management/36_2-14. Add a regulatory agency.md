Procedure
1.Open Employee Center.
2. Navigate to GRC tasks > Tasks > My items > Processing activities.
This opens a list of processing activities where you're added as a key stakeholder.
3. Open the processing activity you want to edit.
4.Select Request edit access.
A confirmation message appears confirming that an email has been sent to the privacy
analyst.
Result
The privacy analyst who owns the processing activity receives an email and reviews the request.
The analyst updates your processing activity privilege to Edit processing activity and respond
to privacy assessments, and assigns the processing activity to you. For steps to assign a
processing activity, see Assign a processing activity to a key stakeholder.
Note: If the processing activity is in the Review or Monitor state, the analyst must move
the processing activity back to the Discover state before assigning it to you.
After it's assigned, you receive an email with a link to the processing activity.
What to do next
Edit the processing activity related lists directly from the Employee Center by navigating to GRC
tasks > Tasks > My pending tasks > Processing activities. Select Assign back to privacy team
when done to request a review of the updated processing activity.
Note: Your edit access is removed when you assign a processing activity back to the
privacy team.

Add a regulatory agency
Add a regulatory agency in the Privacy Workspace to identify the relevant regulatory authorities
that are responsible for overseeing the industries or sectors within each jurisdiction. The
jurisdictions consolidate all the regulatory communications via emails and implement the
notification rules for data privacy or security breaches for the reported privacy cases.
Before you begin
Role required: sn_privacy.analyst
Procedure
1.Navigate to All > Privacy Management > Privacy Workspace > Lists > Regulatory information
> Agencies.
2. Select New.
3. On the form, fill in the fields.
For a description of the fields, see Regulatory agency form .

Regulatory agency form
Use the Create Regulatory Agency form in the Privacy Workspace to create a regulatory agency.
The regulatory agency is a new record type that handles regulatory changes.
Create Regulatory Agency form

Name Name of the regulatory agency. For

Regulation Authority

•Legislative

agencies or businesses engage in similar
