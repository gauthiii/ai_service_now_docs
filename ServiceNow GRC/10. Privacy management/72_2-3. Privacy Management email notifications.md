Privacy Management email notifications
Reminders enable relevant users to perform their assessments and complete the tasks
associated with them.
By default, the Privacy Management application includes the following types of email
notifications.
•Email notifications to the entity owner for privacy screening assessment.
•Email notifications to the entity owner for privacy impact assessment.
•Email notification received for Entity owner for when Privacy Assessment is cancelled when
Assessment is in Open state.
•Email notification received for Entity owner for when Privacy Assessment is cancelled when
Assessment is in Work-in-progress state.
