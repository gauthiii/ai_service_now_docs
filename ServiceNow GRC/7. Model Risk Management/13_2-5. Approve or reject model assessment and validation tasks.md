### Approve or reject model assessment and validation tasks

Approve or reject model assessment and validation tasks
Approve or reject the model assessment and validation tasks either from the Employee Center or
Model Risk Workspace.
Before you begin
Role required: sn_model_risk_mgmt.task_approver
Procedure
1.To approve or reject model assessment and validation tasks from the GRC tasks page,
navigate to All > Self-Service > Employee Center and do the following:
a.Under My active items, select GRC tasks.
b.Select Approvals from the My to-dos
card.
c.To approve or reject model assessment tasks, select Model assessment tasks and open the
task.
d. To approve or reject model validation tasks, select Model validation tasks and open the
task.
2. To approve or reject model assessment and validation tasks from the Model Risk Workspace,
navigate to All > Model Risk Workspace and do the following:
a.Select the tasks icon .
b.To approve or reject model assessment tasks, select MRM Assessments and open the task.
c.To approve or reject model validation tasks, select MRM Validations and open the task.
3. Select Approve.
