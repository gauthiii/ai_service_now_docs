Reassign model assessments and validations
Reassign an model risk assessment or validation to another assignee from the stakeholder list of
the model risk record when the task is in the In-progress state. It ensures that the task continues
without delay if the original assignee is unavailable.
Before you begin
Role required: sn_model_risk_mgmt.model_governance or
sn_model_risk_mgmt.risk_coordinator
Model validators [sn_model_risk_mgmt.model_validator] can also reassign tasks, but only those
tasks that are assigned to them.
Procedure
1.Navigate to All > Model Risk Workspace.
2. Select the list icon .
3. Navigate to the right location based on the type of task.
a.To reassign an assessment task, navigate to Assessment tasks > In-progress.
b.To reassign a validation task, navigate to Validation tasks > In-progress.
4.Select the task that you want to reassign.
5. Select Reassign.
6.In the Reassign model task dialog, enter the assignee name and reason for reassignment.
