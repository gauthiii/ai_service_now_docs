CAM overview
The CAM application applies a standardized approach to automating NIST's Risk Management
Framework (RMF).

RMF workflow supported by CAM
RMF was mandated by the U.S. Federal government to provide the necessary resiliency to
support the economic and national security interests of the United States. CAM employs the
seven steps defined by the RMF to allow you to make better-informed decisions about your
security posture.
RMF consists of the seven steps illustrated here.

> A circular flow diagram titled "RMF security life cycle" showing six interconnected steps arranged around a central teal circle labeled "System Life Cycle".
> 
> **Top center:** Dark border rectangle with icon — **Prepare** (Information System). Caption: "Define the system boundary, assign roles, identify common controls, and prepare for the RMF process." Arrow points clockwise to Categorize.
> 
> **Top right:** Box with layered squares icon — **Categorize** (Information System). Caption: "Define criticality/sensitivity of information system according to potential worse case, adverse impact to mission/business." Arrow points down to Select.
> 
> **Right:** Box with checklist icon — **Select** (System Controls). Caption: "Select baseline controls; apply tailoring guidance and supplement controls as needed based on risk assessments." Arrow points down to Implement.
> 
> **Bottom right:** Box with gear/dial icon — **Implement** (System Controls). Caption: "Implement controls within enterprise architecture using sound systems engineering practices; apply configuration settings." Arrow points left to Assess.
> 
> **Bottom left:** Box with magnifier icon — **Assess** (System Controls). Caption: "Determine control effectiveness (that is, controls implemented correctly, operating as intended, meeting requirements for information system)." Arrow points up to Authorize.
> 
> **Left:** Box with shield/lock icon — **Authorize** (Information System). Caption: "Determine risk to organizational operations and assets, individuals, other organizations, and the Nation; if acceptable, authorize operation." Arrow points up to Monitor.
> 
> **Top left:** Box with circular arrows icon — **Monitor** (System Controls). Caption: "Continuously track changes to the information system that may affect security controls and reassess control effectiveness." Arrow points right back to Categorize.
> 
> Color scheme: Dark grey/black borders for step boxes, teal for the central circle, dark teal arrows showing clockwise flow.
