Reporting a compliance case in GRC: Compliance Case Management
You can report a compliance case or an event that needs the attention of the compliance team
by using the GRC: Compliance Case Management application.
As a business user, you can report a case by using one the following options:
•Report a compliance case from the Employee Center
•Create a compliance case in the Compliance Workspace
•Report a compliance case from the Anonymous Reporting Center
Report a compliance case from the Employee Center
Report any violation of the organization's policies or guidelines by using the Employee Center
application. By reporting a case, you reduce and avert the adverse regulatory actions that could
impact the company's financials, reputation, and growth.

3. In the Browse Risk & Compliance section, select Report a compliance case.

Role required: sn_comp_case.compliance_case_business_user

About this task
ARC enables you to report suspected misconduct safely and confidentially, encouraging early
disclosure of compliance issues that might otherwise go unreported. You can report a case
anytime, without sharing your name or contact details. Investigators only see the report details,
not who submitted it.
Note: When submitting an anonymous report, don't include personal data about yourself
or other individuals that could directly or indirectly reveal an identity. For example, names,
email addresses, or phone numbers.
Anonymous reports accept standard text characters. Avoid including executable code, scripts,
or unsupported special characters, because they may be removed automatically during
submission. For more information, refer to Overview of Anonymous Reporting Center.
Procedure
1.Navigate to All > Self-Service > Employee Center.
2. On the Employee Center landing page, select Risk and compliance.
3. Select the Visit Anonymous Report Center card.
You're signed out of your employee account to maintain anonymity.
4.Select Continue.
5. On the ARC landing page, select Submit a report anonymously.
6.Select Report a compliance case.
7.On the form, fill in the fields.
For a description of the field values on the Report a Compliance Case form, see Anonymous
compliance case form.
8.Enter the CAPTCHA verification code on the screen.
9.Select Submit.
10.To save a PDF copy of your report, select Download report copy.
Note: Save the report securely. The Report key and Report number are required to
access updates or comments related to an anonymous report.
Result
A compliance case is created and routed to the compliance case team for review. Your identity
remains anonymous and is recorded as a guest. If the assigned case analyst needs more details,
they may add comments to the report.
To respond to their comments, refer to Follow up on a case anonymously.

Report a compliance case through email
Report a compliance issue by sending an email to your organization's compliance mailbox. This
creates a compliance case automatically in Compliance Case Management.

Role required: none
You must know:
•The email address designated for reporting a compliance incident (for example
compliance@company.com).
•The instance email ID for your ServiceNow environment (for example,
<instancename>@service-now.com).

Procedure
1.Compose the email on Outlook.
a.In the To field, enter the designated compliance request email address and the instance
email ID.
The instance name should be in the format: <instancename>@service-now.com
b.Optional: Add recipients in CC if you want them added to the case watchlist.
2. Use Outlook’s Importance setting to mark the priority (for example, High or Low).
This priority will map to the Priority field in the created case.
3. Add email content.
a.In the Subject, provide a clear subject line that will help classify the case easily.
b.In the Body, include information about the compliance incident.
4.Add any relevant attachments.
Attachments are stored in the case record for reference.
5. Select Send.
Result
The email triggers an inbound email action in the Compliance Case Management application
and creates a compliance case.
Field mapping after email submission:
•Subject → Title field
The subject in the email is automatically captured in the Title field of the compliance case.
•Body Content → Description field in the case
The text in the email body is automatically captured in the Description field of the compliance
case.
•Importance → Priority field
The importance level set in the email (for example, High) is mapped to the Priority field in the
case.
•Source → Email
The source of the case is recorded as Email, indicating that the case was created from an
inbound email.
•CC Recipients → Watchlist
Any recipients included in the CC field of the email are added to the case watchlist, allowing
them to receive updates.
For a compliance admin, the case will be visible in Compliance workspace > List > Cases >
Unassigned cases.
Any reply sent by the admin appears in the Email related list of the compliance case.
