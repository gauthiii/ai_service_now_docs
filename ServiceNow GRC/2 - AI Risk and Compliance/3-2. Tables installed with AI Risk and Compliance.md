# Tables installed with AI Risk and Compliance

Tables are added with the activation of the AI Risk and Compliance application.

The following table provides a description of the tables installed.

## Tables installed

| Table | Technical name | Description |
|-------|----------------|-------------|
| AI system | `sn_grc_ai_gov_ai_system` | Captures AI use cases submitted via the Employee Center, serving as a central repository for all submitted requests. |
| AI system entity | `sn_grc_ai_gov_ai_system_entity_map` | Maintains entity information for AI systems created through the intake form, including linked details about associated AI models and datasets. |
| AI system task | `sn_grc_ai_gov_ai_system_task` | Tracks AI impact assessments and system-related tasks, such as Legal Review, Architecture Review, Security Review, and other required evaluations. |
| AI case | `sn_ai_case_mgmt_ai_case` | Consolidates AI-related cases and inquiries, providing a comprehensive view of all AI engagements and support requests. |
