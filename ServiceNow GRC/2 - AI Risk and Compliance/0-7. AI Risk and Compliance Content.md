# AI Risk and Compliance Content

## Overview

The **AI Risk and Compliance Content** application is a centralized repository of frameworks, citations, risk statements, and control objectives. Using this application accelerates the adoption of AI Risk and Compliance frameworks.

It is part of the broader **Exploring AI Risk and Compliance** chapter of the ServiceNow documentation and serves as the content backbone for AI Risk and Compliance capabilities.

## What the Content Is

The application acts as a single, centralized source for the building blocks needed to adopt AI Risk and Compliance frameworks:

- **Frameworks** – Standardized AI risk and compliance frameworks and authority documents.
- **Citations** – References associated with a particular framework.
- **Risk statements** – Risk definitions drawn from the authority documents.
- **Control objectives** – Objectives associated with selected citations.

## Installation and Access

| Step | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Source | Install **AI Risk and Compliance Content** from the **ServiceNow Store**. |
| Launch | After installation, launch the application by selecting the **AI Risk and Compliance Content** icon on the **AI Risk and Compliance Workspace**. |

## Frameworks and Regulations

The application contains the following frameworks and authority documents:

- **EU Artificial Intelligence Act**
- **NIST Risk Management Framework**

> **Note:** The list of frameworks supported isn't exhaustive and may evolve in future releases.

## Benefits of the Unified Content Management Application

The Unified content management application provides the following benefits:

- Provides the ability to **choose the framework** you want to use and activate.
- Helps you **choose the citations** that are associated with a particular framework and install them in the **active state** in your instance.
- Enables you to **choose control objectives**, which are associated with the selected citations.
- Helps you **select the risk statements** from the authority documents.

## How It's Used / Configured

The application is built around the **Unified content management** approach, which gives you granular, layered control over what you activate in your instance:

1. **Select a framework** – Choose the framework you want to use and activate (for example, the EU Artificial Intelligence Act or the NIST Risk Management Framework).
2. **Choose citations** – Pick the citations associated with the chosen framework and install them in the active state in your instance.
3. **Choose control objectives** – Select the control objectives that are associated with the selected citations.
4. **Select risk statements** – Select the risk statements from the authority documents.
