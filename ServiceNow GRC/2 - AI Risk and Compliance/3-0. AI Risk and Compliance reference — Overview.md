# AI Risk and Compliance reference — Overview

This chapter provides **reference topics** with additional information about the artifacts that are installed with the **AI Risk and Compliance** application.

Reference topics provide additional information such as the **tables** and **roles** that are installed with the AI Risk and Compliance application.

## What this chapter covers

- **Roles installed with AI Risk and Compliance** — the essential roles installed to perform the respective day-to-day responsibilities, including their technical role names, descriptions, and contained roles. (See `3-1`.)
- **Tables installed with AI Risk and Compliance** — the tables installed with the application, including their labels, technical table names, and descriptions. (See `3-2`.)
