# Activate or update NIST Risk Management Framework using the Unified content management

## Overview

Activate or update the NIST Risk Management Framework and select the citations for the framework. Activation installs the framework and the selected citations, control objectives, and risk statements on your instance.

## Before you begin

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Role required | `sn_grc_ai_gov.ai_risk_and_compliance_manager` |

## Procedure

1. Navigate to **All > AI Risk and Compliance Workspace**.
2. Select the **AI Risk and Compliance Content** icon on the AI Risk and Compliance Workspace.
3. Select the **Activate** button on the NIST Risk Management Framework card.
   - Make sure you have selected the correct version of the NIST Risk Management Framework.
4. Read the disclaimer statement and select **Agree**.
5. Choose the required citations from the list and select **Next**.
   - You can include citations that are marked as **Ready**.
6. Choose the **Control objectives** and select **Next**.
   - The Control objectives are populated based on the selected Citations in the previous step.
7. Choose the **Risk statements** and select **Next**.
   - You can include risk statements that are marked as **Ready**.
8. Review and confirm the selected citations, control objectives, and risk statements.
9. Select **Submit**.

## Updating the framework

10. To modify the NIST Risk Management Framework, select the **Update** button.
    - The **Update** button appears only when the NIST Risk Management Framework is activated.
11. Repeat the steps from step 5.

## Result

The framework and the selected citations, control objectives, and risk statements are installed on your instance.
