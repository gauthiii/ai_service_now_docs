# Tasks Landing Page for AI Risk and Compliance

## Overview

The **Tasks page** in the AI Risk and Compliance Workspace provides a **single-pane view** of:

- Your pending tasks
- Your group's tasks
- The tasks that are on your watchlist

You can also **update tasks directly from the dashboard**, without having to navigate elsewhere.

## Access and Roles

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Workspace | AI Risk and Compliance Workspace |
| Required role | `sn_grc_ai_gov.ai_risk_and_compliance_analyst` |
| View | A user with this role can view the Tasks workspace |

A sample Tasks page is presented to a logged-in user holding the analyst role.

## Tabs on the Tasks Page

The Tasks page displays the following tabs:

- **My pending tasks**
- **My group's tasks**
- **My items**
- **Watchlist**

### My pending tasks

Helps you access all the cases and tasks that have been **assigned to you**. This section includes:

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| AI asset tasks | All the AI asset tasks assigned to me. |
| AI assets | Any AI assets assigned to me. |
| Bulk risk assessments | Any bulk risk assessments assigned to me. |
| Cases | All the AI cases assigned to me to work on. |
| Inquiries | AI inquiries pending with me for review. For example, as an AI case analyst you've created an inquiry and assigned it to an inquiry owner to work on it. The inquiry owner completed the inquiry and submitted it to you for review. All such inquiries are available in this section. |
| Issues | All the issues originating from AI cases are visible to me. |
| Risk assessments | All the risk assessments of the AI systems are visible to me. |

### My group's tasks

Helps you access all the cases and tasks that have been **assigned to your assignment group**. This section includes:

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Cases | All the AI cases assigned to my assignment group. |
| Inquiries | All inquiries pending with my assignment group for review. |

### My items

Helps you access various items that have been **assigned to you**. This section includes:

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| AI asset tasks | All the AI asset tasks assigned to me. |
| Bulk risk assessments | Any bulk risk assessments assigned to me. |
| Inquiries | AI inquiries pending with me for review. |
| Issues | All the issues originating from AI cases are visible to me. |
| Policy exceptions | All the policy exceptions for the AI systems are visible to me. |

### Watchlist

Lets you access all the cases and tasks where **you're added to the watchlist**.

## Features

### Update tasks from the dashboard

Tasks can be updated directly from the Tasks page, giving you a single point of action for managing your work.

### Edit list (manage reporting columns)

You can **add or remove the reporting columns** under any of the tabs on the Tasks page.

To add or remove columns:

1. Navigate to the required tab and select the required category.
2. Select the **List Actions** icon and then **Edit columns**.
3. Select and move the required columns between the **Available columns** list and the **Selected columns** list (in either direction).
4. Select **OK**.

The selected columns are then added or removed accordingly.

## Summary of Uses

- Acts as a centralized, single-pane landing page for an AI Risk and Compliance analyst to monitor and act on work.
- Surfaces personal tasks (My pending tasks, My items), group-level tasks (My group's tasks), and watched items (Watchlist).
- Consolidates AI-related work items such as AI asset tasks, AI assets, bulk risk assessments, cases, inquiries, issues, risk assessments, and policy exceptions.
- Allows in-place task updates and customizable reporting columns for tailored views.
