# Using AI Risk and Compliance — Overview

This chapter explains how to use the **AI Risk and Compliance** application in day-to-day operations.

Use the AI Risk and Compliance application to **request AI systems, AI models, AI datasets, and so on**, and to **perform assessments** for them to determine their **risk posture**.

## What this chapter covers

The chapter walks through the end-to-end usage tasks, including:

- **Requesting AI assets** — AI use cases, AI models, and datasets.
- **Raising inquiries and reporting cases** — AI inquiries, reporting AI cases (from the Employee Center, in the workspace, or anonymously).
- **Managing issues** — creating and remediating AI issues.
- **Running assessments** — initiating and performing AI impact and risk assessments on AI assets and their risks.
- **Managing controls** — adding, removing, and managing controls for AI assets.
- **Bulk assessments** — creating bulk risk assessment projects and performing them in stacked and grid views.
- **Activating frameworks** — activating or updating the NIST Risk Management Framework and the EU Artificial Intelligence Act using Unified content management.

> Each task has its own detailed file in this set (see `2-1` through `2-20`).
