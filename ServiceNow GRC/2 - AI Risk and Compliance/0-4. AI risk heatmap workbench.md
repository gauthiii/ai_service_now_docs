# AI Risk Heatmap Workbench

## Overview

The **AI risk heatmap workbench** is part of the *Exploring AI Risk and Compliance* chapter of the ServiceNow documentation. It describes the AI risk heatmap, a tool for visualizing AI risk data.

## What the Risk Heatmap Is

- A **risk heatmap** is a graphical representation of your risk data.
- The individual values contained in a **matrix** are represented in **colors**, where each color denotes a specific meaning.
- It allows you to view your **AI risk assessment results** in a format that is:
  - Convenient
  - Visually attractive
  - Succinct

## How Data Is Obtained

- The data displayed on the heatmap is **obtained from the risk assessments**.

## Filters and Configuration

- The heatmap and its filters are documented further in the **"Configure risk heatmaps"** reference within the ServiceNow documentation.
- For details on understanding the heatmap and configuring its filters, refer to that section.

## Uses

- View AI risk assessment results visually in a single matrix.
- Quickly interpret risk levels through color-coded values.
- Present risk data in a concise, easy-to-read format.
