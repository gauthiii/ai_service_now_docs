# Publish the Assessment Templates

When creating a new AI system or updating an existing one, you must publish the assessment templates **before** initiating the required assessments (such as the impact assessment and conformity assessment). An assessment template must be published so that the corresponding assessments can be initiated.

## What Are Assessment Templates?

Two assessment templates are provided by default. They are available in the **Draft** state by default, and must be published before they can be used to run assessments.

| Template | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **AI impact assessment** | Ensures that your AI system is both effective and respectful of individuals' rights. By identifying and addressing potential impacts on privacy, non-discrimination, and other human rights, it helps ensure your AI system complies with global regulations and frameworks, such as the **EU AI Act** and the **NIST AI Risk and Compliance Framework**. This helps the system operate ethically and responsibly in accordance with organizational AI policies. |
| **AI impact assessment for EU AI Act conformity assessment** | A structured process used to evaluate whether an AI system complies with the **EU Artificial Intelligence Act (AI Act)**, especially focusing on its risk classification and ensuring compliance with fundamental rights, safety, and transparency requirements. The purpose is to ensure that high-risk AI systems do not pose harm to users, society, or the environment, and meet regulatory obligations according to the AI Act. |

## Why Publishing Is Required

- Both templates ship in the **Draft** state by default.
- Assessments cannot be initiated until their template is published.
- After a template is published, its state changes from **Draft** to **Published**.

## Before You Begin

| Requirement | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| **Role required** | `sn_grc_ai_gov.ai_risk_and_compliance_manager` |

A user with the `sn_grc_ai_gov.ai_risk_and_compliance_manager` role can publish the assessments.

## Procedure

1. Navigate to **All > Assessment Workspace**.
2. Select and open the following templates one at a time:
   - **AI impact assessment**
   - **AI impact assessment for EU AI Act conformity**
3. Select **Publish**.

## Result

The templates are published and ready to be used for assessments. The state of the templates changes to **Published**.
