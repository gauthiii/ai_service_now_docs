# Activate or update EU Artificial Intelligence Act using the Unified content management

## Overview

Activate or update the EU Artificial Intelligence Act and select the citations for the framework. This installs the framework and the selected citations on your instance.

## Before you begin

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Role required | `sn_grc_ai_gov.ai_risk_and_compliance_manager` |

## Procedure

1. Navigate to **All > AI Risk and Compliance Workspace**.
2. Select the **Unified content management** icon on the AI Risk and Compliance Workspace.
3. Select the **Activate** button on the EU Artificial Intelligence Act Framework card.
   - Make sure you have selected the correct version of the EU Artificial Intelligence Act.
4. Read the disclaimer statement and select **Agree**.
5. Choose the required citations from the list and select **Next**.
   - You can include citations that are marked as **Ready**.
6. Review and confirm the selected citations.
7. Select **Submit**.
8. To modify the EU Artificial Intelligence Act, select the **Update** button.
   - The **Update** button appears only when the EU Artificial Intelligence Act is activated.
9. Repeat the steps from step 5.

## Results

The EU Artificial Intelligence Act framework and the selected citations are installed on your instance.
