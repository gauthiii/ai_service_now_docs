Policy Exception Overview Performance Analytics dashboard
The Policy Exception Overview Performance Analytics dashboard provides views into the
number, severity, and source of policy exceptions. It also shows exempted controls.

•Audit Administrator (sn_policy.admin): To pinpoint areas of concern quickly.
•Audit Analyst (sn_policy.user): To prioritize which risks or tasks to focus on based on criticality
to the organization.
