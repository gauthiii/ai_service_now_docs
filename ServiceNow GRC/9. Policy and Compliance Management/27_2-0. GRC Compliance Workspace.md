Performing compliance tasks in Compliance Workspace
Starting with Version 13.0.0, a new workspace has been introduced for the GRC: Policy and
Compliance Management application. The new workspace provides you with an improved User
Experience (UX) and a simplified user journey.
Note: Compliance Workspace has a dependency on GRC: Policy and Compliance
Management application and the latter is automatically installed when the workspace
plugin is installed.
To know more about Compliance Workspace, see GRC Compliance Workspace. For persona-
based Compliance Workspace pages, see:
•Compliance Home page for the Compliance Manager
•Compliance Home page for the Compliance Analyst
•Compliance Home page for the IT compliance manager
GRC Compliance Workspace
Compliance Workspace is a unified interface where you can manage all your tasks related to
policies, control objectives, controls, and policy exceptions.

Roles in the Compliance Workspace
The targeted users of the Compliance Workspace are the corporate compliance managers, the
corporate IT compliance managers, and the corporate compliance analysts.
To access or view the Compliance Workspace, you need one of the following roles:
•IT Compliance Manager role: sn_compliance_ws.it_compliance_manager
•Corporate Compliance Manager role: sn_compliance_ws.corporate_compliance_manager
•Corporate Compliance Analyst role: sn_compliance_ws.corporate_compliance_analyst
For more information on the roles, the responsibilities, and the tasks that the users can
accomplish in the Compliance Workspace, see
•Compliance Home page for the compliance manager.
•Compliance Home page for the compliance analyst.
•Compliance Home page for the IT compliance manager.

Compliance Home page for the Compliance Manager
The compliance manager home page in the Compliance Workspace gives a complete overview
of the compliance posture of the organization. The workspace helps the compliance manager
to centrally manage internal standards, policies, and control processes that match the external
regulatory standards.
Compliance manager most often reports to a compliance director and ensures that the company
complies with organizational and industrial regulations and standards.
The user persona may vary across organizations, however, a corporate compliance manager,
IT compliance manager, compliance manager, or compliance analyst, depending on the
organizational structure, can be tasked with ensuring that the organization is complying with
policies and regulations.

Responsibilities of a compliance manager
The compliance manager must
•Ensure that all policies and regulations are being followed.
•Create and maintain policies up to the level of defining and applying controls.
•Approve and track policy exceptions.
•Manage the team appropriately.

Other GRC plugins for an overall view
As the compliance manager requires an overall view of all compliance tasks, they can view the
privacy management and regulatory change management application domains as well in the
compliance manager home page. However, you require the following plugins to be installed for
certain widgets to be displayed on the home page.
•GRC: Audit Management for the control tests widget in the Control assurance section.

the Tracking section. The logged in user must also have the RCM manager role
(sn_grc_reg_change.manager).
•GRC: Privacy Management for the Domain compliance status section. The logged in user must
also have a privacy manager role (sn_privacy.manager).
Compliance Home page for the Compliance Analyst
The compliance analyst home page in the Compliance Workspace enables the compliance
analyst to track compliance activities and helps compliance managers to ensure that the
organization is compliant with various regulations and policies.
Responsibilities of a compliance analyst
The compliance analysts oversee specific areas of compliance including specific policies,
regulations, and related compliance activities. Therefore, the filter option help them to filter the
compliance records based on the required criteria.
•Analyze and update the existing policies and related documents.
•Implement policies and controls when assigned.
•Monitor the performance of controls.
•Schedule and follow up attestations.

The compliance analyst Home page provides a personalized experience for a corporate
compliance analyst to manage their work.
Overview
Provides a summary of the overall compliance stance of the organization.
Authority documents
The widget displays the overall count of authority documents, summing
up the counts of compliant and non-compliant authority documents.
The section also gives you details of the compliance score percentage.
Clicking the name of the authority document takes you to a detailed
overview of the document showing its compliance status and tracking
details.
Policies
In addition to the count of compliant and non-compliant policies, list
of least compliant policies and compliance score, this section also
provides the number of high risk exceptions for each listed policy.
Entities
Provides the total count of active entities, and a list of least compliant
entities along with their entity class. Click the name of an entity to know
its compliance status and tracking details.
Control Assurance
Provides an overview of testing and monitoring controls. By default, the audit tasks
are filtered and displayed based on the Assigned to me condition of the logged in
user. Whereas, the analyst can filter the indicators and attestations based on the
entities that they manage.
Tracking
Provides a summary of important and open tasks and exceptions and helps the
analyst to manage policy exceptions. Since the analyst is required to understand
the policy exceptions created on the policies that is assigned to them, they can
apply the filter on the required policies and view the exceptions created on these
policies. Similarly, the analyst can select specific policies to view its campaign
details.
Note: To track the issues the compliance analyst can access the issue
details from the "Issues Overview" section.
Quick links
Enables the analyst to create new records such as control objectives, controls,
acknowledgement campaigns, and policy exceptions.
Compliance Home page for the IT compliance manager
The Compliance Workspace provides an exclusive home page for the IT compliance manager to
view the IT-related risk and compliance data. The workspace helps the IT compliance manager
to centrally manage internal standards, policies, and control processes that are exclusively IT-
related to comply with the external regulatory standards.

Tracking
Tracks all the IT-related policies that are in queue for the IT compliance manager's

acknowledgment campaigns that are active and the number of overdue responses
are also displayed.

IT Compliance List overview
As you log in as an IT compliance manager, all the data in the List view of the Compliance
Workspace are filtered to display those records that fall within the IT risk and compliance
functional domain. Therefore, you get to view those records that are related to your user role and
complete the IT-related tasks without filtering them manually.
