To enable the Analytics and Reporting solution for GRC: Policy and Compliance Management,
perform these steps.
1.Visit the ServiceNow Store and obtain an entitlement to the Performance Analytics - GRC:
Policy and Compliance Management application and associated applications. See Install a
ServiceNow Product on the ServiceNow Store.
2. On the instance, navigate to System Applications > All Applications > All.
3. Locate and install the Performance Analytics - GRC: Policy and Compliance Management
application.
Important:
Starting with version 18.1.0 of the Policy and Compliance Management application, the
Analytics and Reporting solutions for Policy and Compliance are available in the Next
Experience UI Framework.
Compliance Overview Performance Analytics dashboard
The Compliance Overview dashboard provides an executive view into compliance requirements,
overall compliance, and compliance breakdowns so areas of concern can be identified quickly.
Compliance Overview dashboard
Important: Starting with version 18.1.1 of the Policy and Compliance Management
application, the Compliance Overview dashboard is available in the Next Experience UI
Framework.
If you are on Vancouver or Washington DC, you can view the dashboard in the Next
Experience UI Framework.

The following roles and tasks are associated with this solution:
•Audit Manager (sn_compliance.manager): To have clear visibility into the overall state and
volume of vulnerabilities within the organization.
•Audit Administrator (sn_compliance.admin): To pinpoint areas of concern quickly.
•Audit Analyst (sn_compliance.user): To prioritize which risks or tasks to focus on based on
criticality to the organization.
