Prior to performing application-specific setup, you need to prepare the system. This involves

Assigning Policy and Compliance Management roles to your users
Before you can successfully implement or use the Policy and Compliance Management
application, you must assign roles to your users.
Before you begin
Role required: admin
Procedure
1.Navigate to All > User Administration > Users.
2. Click the name of a user.
3. Click the Roles tab.
4.Click Edit.
5. Move the roles you want to assign to the user from the Collection side to the Roles List, then
click Save.
6.Repeat these steps for each of your users.
For a comprehensive list of compliance users, see Roles installed with GRC: Policy and
Compliance Management.
