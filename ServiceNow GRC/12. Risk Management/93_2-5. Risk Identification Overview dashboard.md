Risk Identification Overview dashboard
The risk identification dashboard is useful for risk managers and helps them to keep a track of
various records or objects which are in transit in the risk identification workflow.
This dashboard offers various insights for the users. These insights enable risk managers to
decide the required action and helps them to prioritize the risk identification records that need
attention. For example, if there are questionnaires past their due date, the risk managers may
want to follow up with the person who was assigned the questionnaire.
