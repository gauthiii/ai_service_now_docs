Create and run a scripted risk indicator in the Risk Workspace
Create and run a scripted risk indicator to identify the possibility of a future adverse impact on
your organization. A scripted indicator enables you to write your own script to run the indicators.
Indicators are an early warning system and they enable you to take preventative action on the
risks.
Before you begin
Role required: sn_risk.manager or sn_risk.admin
