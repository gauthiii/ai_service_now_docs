Issue management in the Risk Workspace
The issues landing page in the Risk Workspace provides logged-in managers and users with
all the information they need to manage issues on a single page. The landing page features
actionable insights, quick action buttons, filters, and access to open issue triages.
The issues landing page is available in the following workspaces:
•Compliance Workspace
•Audit Workspace
•Privacy Workspace
•Risk Workspace
Open the Issues overview landing page
On each of the listed workspaces, you can access the issues by selecting the Issue overview
icon ( ).
