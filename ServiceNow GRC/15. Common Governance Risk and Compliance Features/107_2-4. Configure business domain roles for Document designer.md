AI reporting assistant
The AI reporting assistant is a Document designer feature that uses prompts to generate report
from ServiceNow data directly in a Word document.
Using the Microsoft Word reporting agent, the assistant queries your ServiceNow data and
inserts the output into your Word report as stories, tables, charts, or data points. Once installed,
the assistant is accessible from the Document designer plugin in Microsoft Word.

How the AI Reporting Assistant works
When you enter a prompt, the Microsoft Word reporting agent queries your ServiceNow data and
generates the output in the requested format as stories, tables, charts, or data points. You can
also select from prebuilt prompt suggestions displayed in the assistant panel. Before the content
is inserted into your Word document, you can review the generated plan and choose to Approve,
Make changes, or Cancel.
AI-generated content can be tracked per conversation. When you start a new conversation and
add content for the first time, you're prompted to enable tracking. When tracking is enabled, all
content inserted during that conversation is recorded and visible in the AI Generated section of
the Manage content page.

Manage content
The Manage content page is accessible from the Document designer add-in in Microsoft Word.
The AI generated section appears at the top of the page when AI-generated content is present
in the document.
AI generated section
The AI generated section displays cards for each piece of content inserted using the AI
reporting assistant during a tracked conversation. Each card includes the following:
•Title: the name of the AI-generated content, generated by the Microsoft Word reporting agent.
Titles are limited to 255 characters. To view the full title, hover over the card.
•Content type: the type of content inserted, displayed as part of the title for multi-source
content. For example: Chart Name, Table Name, Story Name, or Data Name.
•Link: the source table of the AI-generated content, aligned to the right of the card.
