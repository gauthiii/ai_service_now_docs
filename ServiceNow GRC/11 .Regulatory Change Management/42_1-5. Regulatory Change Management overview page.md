Procedure
1.Navigate to All > Regulatory Change Management > Compliance Workspace.
2. Select the list icon .
3. To generate a compliance case report, select All cases.
4.To generate a compliance request report, select All requests.
5. Open the compliance case or compliance request that you want to export as a PDF report.
6.Select the More actions icon.
7.Select Export PDF.
The Export PDF button appears only when a document template is specified in the record
type.
8.Select the template to generate a PDF report.
9.Select Export.
A PDF report is generated and is available in the Attachments section of the record to
download.
10.To download a local copy of the PDF report, select the More actions icon on the PDF
report, and select Download.
Regulatory Change Management overview page
The Regulatory Change Management overview page provides a high-level view of the regulatory
activities in your organization. If you have the sn_grc_reg_change.manager role, you can view
this page in the Compliance Workspace.
Note: The Overview Dashboard in the Classic UI is no longer available from the Yokohama
release.
The prerequisites for displaying the Regulatory Change Management overview page in the
Compliance Workspace are:
•Download the GRC: Regulatory Change Management application from the ServiceNow Store in
your instance.
•Log in as a user with the sn_grc_reg_change.manager role into the Regulatory Change
Management application.
Use the to access the landing page.
The Regulatory Change Management application managers with the
sn_grc_reg_change.manager user role assign the regulatory alerts and monitor the associated
regulatory change tasks, import document tasks, and action tasks.
The Regulatory Change Management application in the Compliance Workspace is designed for
the users with the following roles:
•Regulatory Change Management application managers with the sn_grc_reg_change.manager
role: Only the users with the sn_grc_reg_change.manager role can view the Home page of the
Regulatory Change Management application in the Compliance Workspace.
•Regulatory Change Management application users with the sn_grc_reg_change.user role:
Users with the sn_grc_reg_change.user role can view the tasks assigned to them in the
Regulatory Change Management application, but they cannot view the Regulatory Change
Management application landing page in the Compliance Workspace.

Overview page view
The overview page consists of the data visualization widgets that display the regulatory activity
across your organization. The data visualization widgets are selectable widgets that use color
coding to distinguish the states of the alerts and the tasks. You can click each widget to view all
the associated regulatory alerts or tasks. The color scheme for each donut chart or bar graph is
displayed in each subsection.

Tasks page in the Compliance Workspace
The Tasks page within the Compliance Workspace provides a centralized, task-centric interface
for managing all activities related to regulatory events, source documents, and compliance
workflows. It helps you stay organized, ensures timely and accountable action, and promotes
overall regulatory readiness.
Each task relates to a step in the regulatory response process, such as impact assessment,
policy review, control updates, or evidence collection. Tasks can be created both manually and
automatically. Automatic task creation depends on workflow settings linked to regulatory alerts or
change requests.
This interface supports individual and team-level accountability, promotes timely completion of
tasks, and enables compliance with internal and external regulatory obligations. You can filter,
sort, and act on tasks directly within this interface, making it an essential tool for compliance
officers, analysts, and business stakeholders.
The key features of the Tasks page are as follows:
•Segment task responsibilities across individual and group levels.
•Filter tasks and view personal contributions or monitored items.
•Manage compliance through a task-centric interface.
•Integrate seamlessly with alerts, change requests, and compliance activities.
The page includes four distinct tabs, each serving a specific purpose to enhance task visibility
and management.
My pending tasks
Shows all active or outstanding tasks assigned directly to the logged-in user. This view reinforces
individual accountability by clearly identifying required actions.

My group’s tasks
Shows tasks assigned to groups or teams that the user belongs to, even if the user isn't the direct
assignee. This data is useful for teams with shared responsibilities or collaborative workflows.
The group's tasks view:
•Promotes visibility across shared or collective responsibilities.
•Supports collaboration and effective task delegation among team members.
•Assists managers and leads to monitor and coordinate group tasks.
My items
Lists all tasks or items created, requested, or owned by me, even if not directly assigned. It
ensures ownership continuity throughout the compliance life-cycle.
The item view:
•Includes regulatory change requests, impact assessments, and policy reviews initiated by me.
•Provides full visibility into the user’s regulatory contributions.
•Monitors progress and outcomes for user-initiated items.
Watchlist
Shows tasks that the user has manually chosen to monitor. It's ideal for users involved in passive
oversight of key compliance activities.
The watchlist view:
•Allows tracking of high-priority or cross-functional tasks without direct ownership.
•Supports monitoring of relevant tasks across multiple workstreams.
•Keeps supervisors, coordinators, and compliance officers informed of relevant updates.
The Tasks page helps you manage duties efficiently, ensuring proactive compliance with
regulations within set timelines.
