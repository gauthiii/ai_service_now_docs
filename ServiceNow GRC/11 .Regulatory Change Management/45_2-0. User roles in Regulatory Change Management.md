User roles in Regulatory Change Management
Stakeholders in the Regulatory Change Management (RCM) application have different roles and
responsibilities.
The Regulatory Change Management application roles are installed with the activation of
the GRC: Regulatory Change Management application. The customization or extension of
configuration is not allowed. Only the users with GRC Admin roles can do the configurations
setup. The application involves the following types of roles.
