Tasks page in the Compliance Workspace
The Tasks page within the Compliance Workspace provides a centralized, task-centric interface
for managing all activities related to regulatory events, source documents, and compliance
workflows. It helps you stay organized, ensures timely and accountable action, and promotes
overall regulatory readiness.
Each task relates to a step in the regulatory response process, such as impact assessment,
policy review, control updates, or evidence collection. Tasks can be created both manually and
automatically. Automatic task creation depends on workflow settings linked to regulatory alerts or
change requests.
This interface supports individual and team-level accountability, promotes timely completion of
tasks, and enables compliance with internal and external regulatory obligations. You can filter,
sort, and act on tasks directly within this interface, making it an essential tool for compliance
officers, analysts, and business stakeholders.
The key features of the Tasks page are as follows:
•Segment task responsibilities across individual and group levels.
•Filter tasks and view personal contributions or monitored items.
•Manage compliance through a task-centric interface.
•Integrate seamlessly with alerts, change requests, and compliance activities.
The page includes four distinct tabs, each serving a specific purpose to enhance task visibility
and management.
My pending tasks
Shows all active or outstanding tasks assigned directly to the logged-in user. This view reinforces
individual accountability by clearly identifying required actions.

My group’s tasks
Shows tasks assigned to groups or teams that the user belongs to, even if the user isn't the direct
assignee. This data is useful for teams with shared responsibilities or collaborative workflows.
The group's tasks view:
•Promotes visibility across shared or collective responsibilities.
•Supports collaboration and effective task delegation among team members.
•Assists managers and leads to monitor and coordinate group tasks.
My items
Lists all tasks or items created, requested, or owned by me, even if not directly assigned. It
ensures ownership continuity throughout the compliance life-cycle.
The item view:
•Includes regulatory change requests, impact assessments, and policy reviews initiated by me.
•Provides full visibility into the user’s regulatory contributions.
•Monitors progress and outcomes for user-initiated items.
Watchlist
Shows tasks that the user has manually chosen to monitor. It's ideal for users involved in passive
oversight of key compliance activities.
The watchlist view:
•Allows tracking of high-priority or cross-functional tasks without direct ownership.
•Supports monitoring of relevant tasks across multiple workstreams.
•Keeps supervisors, coordinators, and compliance officers informed of relevant updates.
The Tasks page helps you manage duties efficiently, ensuring proactive compliance with
regulations within set timelines.
