Assign Operational Resilience roles to your users
Assign the appropriate roles to the users of the Operational Resilience application.
Before you begin
Role required: sn_oper_res.admin
About this task
To use the Operational Resilience application successfully, you must assign relevant roles to
your users.
Procedure
1.Navigate to All > User Administration > Users.
2. Click the name of a user.
3. Click the Roles tab.
4.Click Edit.
5. Move the roles that you want to assign to the user from the Collection side to the Roles List and
click Save.
