## What Is This?

This is the first step in the procedure to configure a trace collector in AI Control Tower. It covers how to navigate to the trace configuration area and begin adding a new trace collector. These steps apply to all supported cloud providers unless otherwise noted.

---

## Step 1 Access Trace Configuration

Follow these steps to access the trace configuration:

1. Navigate to the AI Control Tower workspace in your ServiceNow instance.
2. Go to Configurations > AI Evaluations > Traces.
3. Click Add.
4. Select your cloud provider connector (AWS, GCP, or Azure).

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

To begin configuring a trace collector, open the AI Control Tower workspace and drill into Configurations > AI Evaluations > Traces. Use the Add action to start a new trace configuration, then choose the cloud provider connector that matches your environment (AWS, GCP, or Azure). After completing this access step, proceed to fill in the trace configuration fields (Step 2).
