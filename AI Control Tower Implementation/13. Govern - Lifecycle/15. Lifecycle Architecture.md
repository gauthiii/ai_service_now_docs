# Lifecycle Architecture

> ServiceNow AI Control Tower (AICT) — Implementation Guide
> Section: Govern - Lifecycle → Lifecycle Architecture
> Source: AI-Control-Tower.pdf, p.173

---

## What Is This?

Lifecycle Architecture describes the underlying records and roles that come into existence when an AI Use Case (AI System) request is submitted, before the governance lifecycle is kicked off. It identifies the responsible roles and the system-of-record objects the platform automatically creates so that an AI System can be governed and overseen throughout its lifecycle.

---

## Roles

- **AI Asset Owner** (`sn_ai_asset_mgmt.ai_asset_owner`)
- **AI Steward** (`sn_ai_governance.ai_steward`)

---

## Records Created on Submission

Submitting an AI Use Case (AI System) request performs the following prior to kicking-off the lifecycle:

- Creates an **AI System Digital Asset** (`alm_ai_system_digital_asset`) — Represents the AI System "asset" in the platform
- Creates an **AI System Product Model** (`cmdb_ai_system_component_product_model`) — Is a product model of the AI System asset

---

## Records Created as Part of AI Asset Governance

As part of establishing the AI Asset Governance, when the AI Control Tower SKU is installed, the below system of record is setup as well.

- Creates an **AI Asset Governance Details** (`sn_ai_governance_asset_governance_details`) — Is a system of record that powers the governance of the AI system including overseeing the lifecycle of the AI System

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing the AI System lifecycle, ensure the AI Control Tower SKU is installed so that the AI Asset Governance Details system of record is provisioned. Confirm the AI Asset Owner and AI Steward roles are assigned to the appropriate users. Upon submission of an AI Use Case (AI System) request, verify that the AI System Digital Asset and AI System Product Model records are auto-created, and that the AI Asset Governance Details record is established to power and oversee the lifecycle before the lifecycle is kicked off.
