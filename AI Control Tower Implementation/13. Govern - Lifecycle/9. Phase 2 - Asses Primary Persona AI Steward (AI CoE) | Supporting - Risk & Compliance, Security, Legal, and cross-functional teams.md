## What Is This?

Phase 2 (Assess) is the governance lifecycle stage where a registered AI asset undergoes structured evaluation before development begins. The AI Steward (AI CoE) is the primary persona, coordinating assessment activities, while Risk & Compliance, Security, Legal, and cross-functional teams provide supporting input. It fits between the New/registration stage and the Build & Test phase in the AI Control Tower governance lifecycle.

---

## Phase 2: Assess

During the Assess phase, the AI asset undergoes structured evaluation across four key dimensions:

- Performance and technical viability
- Business value and expected impact
- Risk posture
- Regulatory and policy compliance

The AI Steward coordinates all assessment activities, ensuring alignment with enterprise principles including privacy, ethics, transparency, and the protection of fundamental rights. AI Control Tower supports this work by enabling identification and tracking of risks and the definition and mapping of required controls.

Upon completion, the AI Steward reviews findings and approves advancement to the Build & Test phase.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

An implementer applies this phase after an AI asset has been registered (moved past the New stage). The AI Steward drives the assessment, evaluating the asset across performance/technical viability, business value/impact, risk posture, and regulatory/policy compliance, while engaging supporting teams (Risk & Compliance, Security, Legal, cross-functional). Use AI Control Tower to identify and track risks and to define and map required controls, ensuring alignment with enterprise principles (privacy, ethics, transparency, protection of fundamental rights). The phase is gated: the AI Steward must review findings and approve advancement before the asset proceeds to Build & Test.
