## What Is This?

The Intake / New / Onboard step is Phase 1 of the AI Asset Lifecycle in AI Control Tower. It is the entry point through which new AI assets — AI systems, models, datasets, and prompts — are submitted into governance. This phase is driven by the AI Product Owner persona and captures the key details needed to begin responsible development, deployment, and oversight.

---

## Phase 1: Intake / Onboard

**Primary Persona: AI Product Owners**

AI Product Owners initiate the AI asset lifecycle by submitting AI use case requests through the Employee Center. These requests capture key details related to the AI models, datasets, and intended business use.

This phase is the first of the distinct stages in the AI asset lifecycle that ensure responsible development, deployment, and ongoing oversight — covering any AI asset type from initial onboarding through retirement. Each phase has defined activities, responsible personas, and governance checkpoints.

## How to Use This in Implementation

To apply this in implementation, AI Product Owners use the Employee Center to submit AI use case requests, providing details about the AI models, datasets, and intended business use. These submissions create the AI asset record in the AI Asset Repository and trigger the downstream governance activities (impact assessment, risk classification, control assessment) carried out by the AI Steward and AI Risk & Compliance Manager. Implementers should ensure the Employee Center intake form captures the required asset details and that the GOTH playbook tasks are configured to match the organization's AI governance process.
