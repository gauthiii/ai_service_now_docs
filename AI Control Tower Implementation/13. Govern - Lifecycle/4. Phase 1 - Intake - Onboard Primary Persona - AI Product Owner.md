## What Is This?

Phase 1 (Intake / New / Onboard) is the first stage of the AI asset lifecycle within AI Control Tower. It is owned by the AI Product Owner persona, who initiates the lifecycle for any AI asset. This phase captures the initial AI use case request and the key details needed to begin governance, deployment, and oversight of the asset.

---

## Intake / New / Onboard

**Primary Persona: AI Product Owners**

AI Product Owners initiate the AI asset lifecycle by submitting AI use case requests through the Employee Center. These requests capture key details related to the AI models, datasets, and intended business use.

---

## Context: The AI Asset Lifecycle

The AI Control Tower lifecycle outlines the governance and management process for AI assets within your organization. This workflow is divided into distinct stages that ensure responsible development, deployment, and oversight of AI assets.

The AI asset lifecycle defines the series of phases required to manage any AI asset — including AI systems, models, datasets, and prompts — from initial intake through retirement. Each phase has defined activities, responsible personas, and governance checkpoints to ensure responsible development, deployment, and ongoing oversight.

## How to Use This in Implementation

To implement Phase 1, enable AI Product Owners to submit AI use case requests through the **Employee Center**. Ensure the request intake form captures the key details for the AI models, datasets, and intended business use, since these feed the downstream governance phases (Build & Validate, Deploy/Monitor/Assess Value). The request flows into the AI Asset Repository (via ServiceNow AI Agent Studio), where it becomes available to the AI Steward and AI Risk & Compliance Manager for assessment. Note from the lifecycle diagram that certain assessment/risk tasks are configured as part of the GRC playbook by customers based on their own AI governance process.
