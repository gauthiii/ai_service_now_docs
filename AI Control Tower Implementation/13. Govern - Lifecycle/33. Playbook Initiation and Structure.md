## What Is This?

This subtopic describes how the AI Asset Lifecycle Playbook is initiated within AI Control Tower and how its content is structured. The playbook is triggered when an AI Steward starts a review of an AI asset, and it organizes the lifecycle into standard stages. It fits into the Govern - Lifecycle phase of implementation, providing the guided, step-by-step experience that drives an AI asset through governance.

---

## Playbook Context

ServiceNow Playbooks are interactive, guided experiences built on the Now Experience UI framework and configured via UI Builder. They are used across ServiceNow products — including CSM, HR, Field Service, and Risk & Compliance — to guide users through predefined, step-by-step processes for complex tasks.

AI Control Tower implements the AI Asset Lifecycle Playbook, which is associated with the AI Asset Approval Request `[sn_ai_governance_assessment_request]` at the time a lifecycle record is created.

- The Approval Request is filtered with `Type = "AI Governance Lifecycle"`
- It maintains a relationship with the AI Asset Governance Details record `[sn_ai_governance_asset_governance_details]`
- This relationship enables the Playbook to surface within the AI Control Tower workspace on the Governance Details record

---

## Playbook Initiation and Structure

The lifecycle playbook is triggered when an AI Steward selects **Start Review** on an AI asset. This action:

- Initiates the lifecycle process
- Activates the Lifecycle tab on the AI Asset record
- Loads the Playbook within the workspace

The Playbook is organized into three standard lifecycle stages — **Assess**, **Build & Test**, and **Deploy** — each containing defined phases, associated lifecycle tasks, and stage-specific governance activities.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

To apply this in implementation, ensure the AI Asset Lifecycle Playbook is correctly associated with the AI Asset Approval Request (`sn_ai_governance_assessment_request`) filtered on `Type = "AI Governance Lifecycle"` and related to the AI Asset Governance Details record (`sn_ai_governance_asset_governance_details`) so the Playbook surfaces in the AICT workspace. The AI Steward triggers the playbook via **Start Review**, which activates the Lifecycle tab and loads the three-stage structure (Assess, Build & Test, Deploy). Implementers should plan governance phases and lifecycle tasks around these three standard stages.
