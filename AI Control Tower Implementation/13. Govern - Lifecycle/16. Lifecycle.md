## What Is This?

This subtopic explains how lifecycle governance and risk/compliance execution are handled within the AI Control Tower (AICT) architecture through distinct but interconnected records. It clarifies the separation of concerns between AI Control Tower (lifecycle governance and orchestration) and AI Risk and Compliance / GRC (risk, compliance, and control execution). This alignment is foundational to governing an AI System across its lifecycle phases.

---

## AI Control Tower and AI Risk & Compliance Object Alignment

Within the AI Control Tower architecture, lifecycle governance and risk/compliance execution are handled through distinct but interconnected records.

- **The AI Asset Governance Details record**

  ```
  [sn_ai_governance_asset_governance_details]
  ```

  serves as the primary lifecycle governance record within AI Control Tower. It provides AI Stewards with:
  - End-to-end lifecycle visibility
  - Governance state tracking
  - Oversight across all lifecycle phases

- **The AI System record**

  ```
  [sn_grc_ai_gov_ai_system]
  ```

  is the operational record leveraged by AI Risk and Compliance (GRC) to:
  - Execute risk assessments
  - Manage compliance workflows
  - Track regulatory alignment

Together, these records ensure separation of concerns:

- **AICT:** Lifecycle governance and orchestration
- **GRC:** Risk, compliance, and control execution

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing AI System governance, recognize that two distinct records work together. The AI Asset Governance Details record (`sn_ai_governance_asset_governance_details`) is the lifecycle governance record AI Stewards use within AI Control Tower for end-to-end visibility and governance state tracking across lifecycle phases. The AI System record (`sn_grc_ai_gov_ai_system`) is the operational record GRC uses to execute risk assessments, manage compliance workflows, and track regulatory alignment. Keep these responsibilities separated: orchestrate the lifecycle through AICT while delegating risk, compliance, and control execution to GRC.
