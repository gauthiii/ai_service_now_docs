## What Is This?

"Assess" is a lifecycle phase in the AI governance lifecycle within AI Control Tower. When an AI Governance Details record enters the Assess phase, a series of AI Risk and Compliance (AIRC) flows are triggered to perform impact assessment, collaboration, analyst assignment, and risk assessment. This subtopic documents the flows that run while an AI asset is in the Assess phase.

---

## Flow: AIRC flow once AI Gov Details is in assess phase

This flow runs once the AI Governance Details record transitions into the Assess phase.

| Step | Action / Task | Details / Task Type |
|------|---------------|---------------------|
| 1 | Flow initiation | Triggers once AI Governance Details enter *Assess* phase |
| 1.1 | Transition to Assess phase | Trigger point: AI Governance Details record moves to Assess |
| 1.2 | Create AI system *Impact Assessment* task | AI asset approval task of type *Impact Assessment* |
| 1.3 | Assign AI Impact Assessment task | Task created for AI Risk and Compliance managers |
| 1.4 | Complete and review Impact Assessment task | AI Asset Owner completes it; AI Risk & Compliance Manager reviews and closes |
| 1.5 | Sync Impact Assessments tasks | AICT and AIRC assessments tasks kept in sync |
| 2 | Create Collaboration with legal team for AI use case task | Create AI approval task of type *AI Governance Task* |
| 3 | Create Collaboration for security clearance on AI use case development task | Create AI approval task of type *AI Governance Task* |
| 4 | Create Architecture review for AI use case task | Create AI approval task of type *AI Governance Task* |

## Flow: Assign analyst to AI System

This flow runs when the AI system impact assessment is closed, assigning a risk analyst to the AI system.

| Step | Action / Task | Details / Task Type |
|------|---------------|---------------------|
| 1 | Flow initiation | When AI system impact assessment is closed |
| 1.1 | Update AI system record (i.e assign analyst field to the user who closed the task) | Assign risk analyst on AI system to the user who closed the task |

## How to Use This in Implementation

When an AI Governance Details record reaches the Assess phase, these AIRC flows automate the governance work: an Impact Assessment task is created and assigned to AI Risk and Compliance managers, completed by the AI Asset Owner and reviewed/closed by the AI Risk & Compliance Manager, with AICT and AIRC assessment tasks kept in sync. Additional AI Governance Tasks are created for legal collaboration, security clearance, and architecture review. Once the impact assessment is closed, the Assign analyst flow updates the AI system record to assign the risk analyst to the user who closed the task. Implementers should ensure the AICT and AIRC assessment synchronization is configured so the two stay aligned.
