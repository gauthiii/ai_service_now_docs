## What Is This?

Phase 3 (Build & Test) is the stage in the AI governance lifecycle where the AI asset is developed, integrated, and validated. The primary personas for this phase are the AI Product Owners and the AI Risk & Compliance Teams. It follows the Assess phase and precedes the Deploy & Operate phase, embedding risk and compliance activities throughout development and requiring a pre-deployment review before progression.

---

## Phase 3: Build & Test

This phase covers the development, integration, and validation of the AI asset. Core activities include:

- Design and development of models and algorithms
- Integration with required data sources
- Functional and performance testing for reliability and accuracy

Risk and compliance activities are embedded throughout — addressing identified risks and implementing recommended controls during development. Prior to deployment, a pre-deployment review validates:

- Compliance with external frameworks (e.g., NIST AI RMF, EU AI Act)
- Alignment with internal policies (e.g., AI SDLC policies)
- Resolution of open issues and closure of any policy exceptions

The AI Steward reviews readiness and approves progression to the Deploy phase.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Implementers reaching this phase should ensure development covers model/algorithm design, data source integration, and functional/performance testing. Throughout the build, embed risk and compliance work by addressing identified risks and implementing recommended controls. Before deployment, complete a pre-deployment review confirming external framework compliance (NIST AI RMF, EU AI Act), alignment with internal policies (AI SDLC), and closure of open issues and policy exceptions. Progression to the Deploy phase requires the AI Steward's readiness review and approval.
