## What Is This?

Build & Test is a phase in the AI asset governance lifecycle within AI Control Tower. AI assets advance into this phase after AI Stewards review progress and approve advancement from the Assess phase. This phase covers the building and testing of the AI use case before further governance progression.

---

## Build & Test Phase

The lifecycle begins when an AI Product Owner submits an AI use case request through the Employee Center, capturing key details about the AI models, datasets, and intended business use. AI assets may enter the inventory through two paths.

> Note: On page 170 the "Build & Test" heading appears at the bottom of the page and only the introductory sentence above is shown. The two intake paths and subsequent Build & Test detail continue on the following page(s) beyond this page range.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

An implementer reaches Build & Test only after the Assess phase is completed and AI Stewards have approved advancement. Entry into the lifecycle/inventory is driven by an AI Product Owner submitting an AI use case request through the Employee Center, which captures details about the AI models, datasets, and intended business use. Ensure the Employee Center intake is configured so that AI use case requests can be submitted and routed into the inventory.
