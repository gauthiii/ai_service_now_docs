---

## What Is This?

The Lifecycle Task Framework is the layer of discrete tasks that drive lifecycle execution within an AI Asset Approval Request. AI Asset Approval Tasks (`sn_ai_governance_assessment_task`) are created within the Approval Request to represent and track individual governance and compliance activities across lifecycle phases. It provides structured tracking and accountability for governance work spanning all AI asset types.

---

## AI Asset Approval Tasks

AI Asset Approval Tasks `[sn_ai_governance_assessment_task]` are created within the Approval Request to drive lifecycle execution. These tasks:

- Represent discrete governance and compliance activities across the Assess, Build & Test, and Deploy phases
- Enable structured tracking and accountability
- Apply across all AI asset types: AI Systems, AI Models, Datasets, Prompts, and Inputs and Outputs

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

The Lifecycle Task Framework operationalizes governance work inside an AI Asset Approval Request. Implementers create AI Asset Approval Tasks (`sn_ai_governance_assessment_task`) within the Approval Request to track each discrete governance and compliance activity tied to a lifecycle phase (Assess, Build & Test, Deploy). Because tasks apply uniformly across all AI asset types (AI Systems, AI Models, Datasets, Prompts, and Inputs and Outputs), they provide a consistent mechanism for accountability and progress tracking. This depends on the Approval Request already referencing the AI Asset Governance Details record and being scoped with `Type = "AI Governance Lifecycle"`.
