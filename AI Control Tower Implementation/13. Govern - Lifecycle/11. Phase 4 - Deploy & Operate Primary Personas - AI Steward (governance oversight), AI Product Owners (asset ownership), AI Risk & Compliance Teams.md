## What Is This?

Phase 4 (Deploy & Operate) is the final stage in the AI Control Tower governance lifecycle, following Phase 3 (Build & Test). In this phase, the AI asset is operationalized within business environments. The primary personas are the AI Steward (governance oversight), AI Product Owners (asset ownership), and AI Risk & Compliance Teams.

---

## Phase 4: Deploy & Operate

**Primary Personas:** AI Steward (governance oversight), AI Product Owners (asset ownership), AI Risk & Compliance Teams

In this phase, the AI asset is operationalized within business environments. Deployment may follow a phased or limited release strategy or proceed as a full-scale enterprise rollout.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

An implementer reaches this phase after the AI Steward has reviewed readiness and approved progression from the Build & Test phase. To apply Phase 4, plan the operationalization of the approved AI asset and choose a deployment strategy — either a phased/limited release or a full-scale enterprise rollout — with the AI Steward providing governance oversight, AI Product Owners holding asset ownership, and AI Risk & Compliance Teams involved throughout.
