## What Is This?

This subtopic introduces how AI Control Tower handles approval requests and lifecycle execution by separating lifecycle governance from risk and compliance execution. It explains that AI Control Tower relies on two distinct but interconnected records to achieve this separation. This sits within the Govern - Lifecycle phase of an AI Control Tower implementation.

---

## Object Alignment: AI Control Tower and AI Risk & Compliance

AI Control Tower uses two distinct but interconnected records to separate lifecycle governance from risk and compliance execution.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing approval requests and lifecycle execution, an implementer should recognize that lifecycle governance and risk/compliance execution are kept as separate concerns handled by two interconnected records. This subtopic is introductory; the detailed alignment of these two records (the AI Asset Governance Details record and the AI System record) is described in the related Lifecycle object alignment content.
