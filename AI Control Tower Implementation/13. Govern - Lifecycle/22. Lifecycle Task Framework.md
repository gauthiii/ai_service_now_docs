## What Is This?

The Lifecycle Task Framework is the layer in AI Control Tower where AI Asset Approval Tasks are created within an Approval Request to drive lifecycle execution. These tasks represent discrete governance and compliance activities across the AI lifecycle phases and provide structured tracking and accountability. They sit beneath the Approval Request, which itself operationalizes the lifecycle defined in the Governance Details record.

---

## Lifecycle Task Framework

AI Asset Approval Tasks (`sn_ai_governance_assessment_task`) are created within the Approval Request to drive lifecycle execution. These tasks:

- Represent discrete governance and compliance activities across the Assess, Build & Test, and Deploy phases
- Enable structured tracking and accountability
- Apply across all AI asset types: AI Systems, AI Models, Datasets, Prompts, and Inputs and Outputs

### Context: Position Within the Execution Layer

The Lifecycle Task Framework operates under the AI Asset Approval Request (`sn_ai_governance_assessment_request`), which is the execution layer that operationalizes the lifecycle defined in the Governance Details record. The Approval Request drives progression across all lifecycle phases, and the Approval Tasks are the underlying units of work that carry out that progression.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

To implement lifecycle execution, create AI Asset Approval Tasks (`sn_ai_governance_assessment_task`) within an AI Asset Approval Request. Each task should map to a discrete governance or compliance activity in the Assess, Build & Test, or Deploy phase, ensuring structured tracking and accountability. Because tasks apply across all AI asset types (AI Systems, AI Models, Datasets, Prompts, and Inputs and Outputs), use them consistently to drive lifecycle progression as orchestrated by the parent Approval Request.
