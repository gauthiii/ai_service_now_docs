## What Is This?

Assess is a phase in the AI asset governance lifecycle within AI Control Tower. During this phase, AI assets that have entered the "New" stage undergo structured evaluation across key dimensions before they are allowed to advance. It is led by the AI Steward (AI CoE) with support from cross-functional teams, and it ensures alignment with enterprise principles prior to building and testing the asset.

---

## Assess Phase

**Primary Persona:** AI Steward (AI CoE)
**Supporting Stakeholders:** Risk & Compliance, Security, Legal, and other cross-functional teams

During the Assess phase, AI assets undergo structured evaluation across key dimensions:

- Performance and technical viability
- Business value and expected impact
- Risk posture
- Regulatory and policy compliance

The AI Steward (CoE) coordinates assessment activities, ensuring alignment with enterprise principles such as:

- Privacy
- Ethics
- Transparency
- Protection of fundamental rights

AI Control Tower enables:

- Identification and tracking of risks
- Definition and mapping of required controls

Following completion of assessments, AI Stewards review progress and approve advancement to the Build & Test phase.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

To apply the Assess phase, an AI Steward (AI CoE) coordinates a structured evaluation of each registered AI asset across performance and technical viability, business value and expected impact, risk posture, and regulatory and policy compliance, engaging Risk & Compliance, Security, Legal, and other cross-functional teams as supporting stakeholders. Use AI Control Tower to identify and track risks and to define and map required controls, ensuring alignment with enterprise principles (privacy, ethics, transparency, and protection of fundamental rights). The prerequisite is that the asset has already been registered and advanced from the "New" stage; once assessments are complete, the AI Steward reviews progress and approves advancement to the Build & Test phase.
