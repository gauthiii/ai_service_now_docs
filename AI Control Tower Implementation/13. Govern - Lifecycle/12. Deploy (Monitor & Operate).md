## What Is This?

Deploy (Monitor & Operate) is the final stage of the AI governance lifecycle in AI Control Tower, covering what happens after an AI system goes live. At this stage, continuous monitoring tracks ongoing performance, user adoption, and emerging risk and compliance posture, while governance roles maintain oversight and value realization is measured. It closes the end-to-end lifecycle that moves through New, Assess, Build and Test, and Deploy.

---

## Post-Deployment Monitoring and Oversight

Post-deployment, continuous monitoring tracks performance and effectiveness, user adoption, and emerging risks and compliance posture. AI Stewards maintain ongoing governance oversight while AI Product Owners ensure sustained lifecycle management and adherence to governance standards.

AI Control Tower also supports value realization tracking, enabling measurement of AI adoption and business impact over time.

## How to Use This in Implementation

For the Deploy (Monitor & Operate) stage, implementers should ensure continuous monitoring is in place after go-live to track performance/effectiveness, user adoption, and emerging risk and compliance posture. Assign AI Stewards for ongoing governance oversight and AI Product Owners for sustained lifecycle management and adherence to governance standards. Enable value realization tracking to measure AI adoption and business impact over time, and reference the AI Risk and Compliance and AI Value capabilities. The lifecycle itself is implemented via the AI Asset Lifecycle Playbook, which orchestrates flows/subflows and provides the workspace UI layer; before publishing, incorporate Onboarding and Offboarding Lifecycle Tasks from other workstreams.
