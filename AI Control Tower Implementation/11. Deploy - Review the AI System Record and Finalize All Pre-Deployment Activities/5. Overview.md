## What Is This?

This page covers the final Deploy-phase activities for an AI system record in AI Control Tower, including the Deploy task list and the configuration guidance for modifying the Deploy phase flow and sub-flows. It shows how the Deploy stage appears in the AI Asset Onboarding lifecycle for an AI Agent record and how pre-deployment tasks are completed before the system goes live.

## How to Use This in Implementation

During the Deploy phase, complete the remaining deployment task(s) (for example, TASK0022725 "Deploy Asset") and use "Mark as complete" to finalize pre-deployment activities once the record is "Approved for deployment." To customize the Deploy-phase flow or its sub-flows, refer to the Govern – Lifecycle topic, and always create a copy version of any system-defined flow or sub-flow before making changes to preserve upgrade safety.
