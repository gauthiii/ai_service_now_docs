## What Is This?

This subtopic introduces the Deploy phase of the AI system lifecycle in AI Control Tower. It is the point at which all compliance activities are finalized and an AI system is transitioned into the deployed state. It marks the move from pre-deployment review into active, monitored production governance.

---

## Overview

Finalize all compliance activities, confirm that risks and issues have been addressed, and move an AI system into the deployed state with active monitoring for ongoing governance.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Before performing the Deploy phase, an implementer should ensure all preceding lifecycle activities — particularly pre-deployment review and residual risk assessment — are complete and that outstanding risks and issues have been resolved. Once these compliance activities are confirmed, the AI system can be moved into the deployed state, after which it remains under active monitoring for ongoing governance.
