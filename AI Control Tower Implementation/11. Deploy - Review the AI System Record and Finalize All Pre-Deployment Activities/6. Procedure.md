## What Is This?

This procedure describes the bulk actions an implementer performs from the AI asset inventory in the AI Risk and Compliance Workspace to finalize pre-deployment activities for AI systems. It covers initiating AI assessments and risk assessments across multiple AI assets at once, including bulk risk assessment creation. These actions are used to ensure AI systems have the required assessments completed before deployment.

---

## Procedure

### A. Bulk Action: AI Assessment

From the **AI asset inventory → AI systems** list in the AI Risk and Compliance Workspace, select one or more AI systems, then choose the **AI assessment** bulk action (from the action menu next to **Edit**) to send AI assessments.

**Send AI assessment** is a two-step wizard:

**Step 1 — Related entities (Select related entities):** All the related entities for the selected AI assets are listed here. An assessment will be initiated for each selected entity. The table groups entities under each AI asset.

| AI asset | Name | Business owner | Risk classification |
|---|---|---|---|
| ServiceNow Regulatory Document Automation 2025 | ServiceNow Regulatory Document Automation 2025 | Val Oborne | High (Score: 9) |
| ServiceNow AI Based Credit Scoring for Loan Approvals 6.4 | ServiceNow AI Based Credit Scoring for Loan Approvals 6.4 | Mary Cruse | (blank) |

Click **Next** (or **Cancel**).

**Step 2 — Assessment details:** Initiated assessments would be assigned to the respective product owner. Fields:

- **Assessment template** (required) — e.g., "High-risk AI assessment questionnaire"
- **Due Date** (required) — e.g., 2026-03-13
- **Assessment title** (required) — e.g., "High Risk AI Asset"
- **Add related entity name as suffix** (checkbox)
- **Description** — e.g., "High Risk AI Asset"

Click **Send assessment** (or **Previous** / **Cancel**).

### B. Bulk Action: Risk Assessment

From the AI systems list, select one or more AI systems and choose the **Risk classification** bulk action (shown in the action menu near **AI assessment**) to send a regulatory risk classification assessment.

**Send regulatory risk classification assessment** is a two-step wizard:

**Step 1 — Related entities (Select related entities):** All the related entities for the selected AI assets are listed here. An assessment will be initiated for each selected entity.

| AI asset | Name | Analyst | Risk classification |
|---|---|---|---|
| ServiceNow AI Based Credit Scoring for Loan Approvals 1.0 | ServiceNow AI Based Credit Scoring for Loan Approvals 1.0 | Josh Warner | High (Score: 9) |
| ServiceNow Email Assistant AI Agent for Corporate 1.0 | ServiceNow Email Assistant AI Agent for Corporate 1.0 | Josh Warner | High (Score: 8) |

Click **Next** (or **Cancel**).

**Step 2 — Assessment details:** Risk assessments would be assigned to the respective asset analyst. Fields:

- **Risk assessment methodology** (required) — e.g., "Risk classification for AI system"
- **Due Date** — e.g., 2026-03-10
- **Assessor** (required) — e.g., Josh Warner
- **Assign risk assessment to asset analyst** (checkbox)
- **Approver**

Click **Send assessment** (or **Previous** / **Cancel**).

After sending, an information banner appears: "Assessments are being generated for your selected entity records. If an entity already has an open assessment with the chosen Risk assessment methodology, a new assessment will not be generated for that entity."

### C. Bulk Risk Assessment

From an AI asset record (e.g., **ServiceNow Email Assistant AI Agent for Corporate 1.0**), open the **AI assessment** action menu, which lists: **Risk classification**, **Risk assessment**, and **Bulk risk assessment**. Select **Bulk risk assessment**.

**Create bulk risk assessment** dialog: "Select the related entity and RAM to create a bulk risk assessment for the AI system." Fields:

- **Related entity** (required) — e.g., "ServiceNow Email Assistant AI Agent for Corporate 1.0"
- **Risk assessment methodology (RAM)** (required) — e.g., "Risk assessment for AI inventory"

Click **Create** (or **Cancel**).

This creates a **Risk Assessment Project** (e.g., RAP0001002) titled, for example, "Risk assessment for 'ServiceNow Email Assistant AI Agent for Corporate 1.0' - March 2026". The project follows a four-stage workflow: **Define → Scope risk → Assess → Approve**.

**Define stage** sections:

- **Context** (Complete)
- **Details** — **Name** (required) and **Description** fields. Buttons: Save / Next.
- **Stakeholders** — fields:
  - **Owner** (required) — e.g., Josh Warner
  - **Assessor type** — e.g., Users
  - **Assessors** (required) — e.g., Josh Warner
  - **Watchlist type** — e.g., Entity stakeholder
  - **Watchlist users from entity**

**Scope risk stage — Risks:** Risks are mapped from the assessable entity. You can remove risks from this project without deleting them from the assessable entity. Options include **Create from risk statements**, **New**, and **Add risk**.

**Assess stage — Assessments:** Assess the identified risks together by selecting one of the views: **Stacked view** or **Grid view**. Click **Start assessment**. The assessment grid shows, per risk, an **Inherent assessment** (Impact, Likelihood, Inherent risk, Final computed score) and a **Control assessment** (Applicable, Associated Controls, AI-System control eff..., Con...). Example rows show "Unintended Consequences" and "Model Performance Degradation (Mode...)" with Impact and Likelihood set to "--None--", Applicable = "Yes", and AI-System control effectiveness = "Ineffective".

## How to Use This in Implementation

To finalize pre-deployment activities, work from the AI asset inventory in the AI Risk and Compliance Workspace. Use the **AI assessment** bulk action to send assessment questionnaires (choosing an assessment template, due date, title, and description) to product owners of selected AI systems, and the **Risk classification** bulk action to send regulatory risk classification assessments to asset analysts (choosing a risk assessment methodology, due date, and assessor). For deeper, multi-risk evaluation of a single AI system, use **Bulk risk assessment** from the AI asset record to create a Risk Assessment Project, then progress it through Define, Scope risk, Assess, and Approve. Note the gotcha called out by the system: if an entity already has an open assessment with the chosen risk assessment methodology, a new assessment will not be generated for that entity.
