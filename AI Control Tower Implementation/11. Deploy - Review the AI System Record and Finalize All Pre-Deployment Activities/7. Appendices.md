## What Is This?

The Appendices provide supplementary reference material to support the AI Control Tower implementation. They point implementers to ServiceNow learning courses and certification programs for Integrated Risk Management (IRM), as well as end-user documentation and reference resources. This section is intended as a curated list of further reading and training rather than procedural implementation steps.

---

## A. Learning Courses

ServiceNow offers comprehensive certification and training programs for those looking to become proficient in Integrated Risk Management (IRM). These programs are designed to equip professionals with the necessary skills to implement and manage IRM effectively within their organizations.

Here are some fundamental certification and training options available:

1. GRC: Integrated Risk Management (IRM) Fundamentals
2. GRC: Integrated Risk Management (IRM) Implementation
3. GRC Risk - Process Guide
4. IRM Workspace Overview Video
5. GRC Issue Management Process Guide: Provides detailed guidance on the way that ServiceNow intends the process to be, for GRC Issue Management
6. GRC Product Architecture Blueprint: Describes the inherent functionality of the Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC) and outlines the technical components in the form of a diagram

## How to Use This in Implementation

Use these appendices as a directory of further learning and reference resources after completing the core AI Control Tower deployment activities. Implementers and GRC/IRM practitioners can enroll in the listed ServiceNow certification courses (IRM Fundamentals, IRM Implementation) and consult the process guides and architecture blueprint to deepen their understanding. The End-User Documentation links and reference demo data support onboarding and hands-on familiarization. No configuration steps are defined here; the value is in directing teams to authoritative ServiceNow training and documentation.
