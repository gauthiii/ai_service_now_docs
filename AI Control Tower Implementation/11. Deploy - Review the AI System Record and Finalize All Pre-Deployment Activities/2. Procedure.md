## What Is This?

This subtopic describes the procedure step that initiates the Deploy lifecycle phase for an AI system in AI Control Tower. After all pre-deployment activities are reviewed and finalized, a lifecycle task is generated to prompt the responsible owner to deploy the asset. It fits at the transition point where an AI system moves out of the Build and test phase toward the deployed state with active monitoring.

---

## Procedure

Lifecycle Task – Deploy Asset assigned to Product Owner as a reminder notice.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When an AI system is ready for deployment, AI Control Tower creates a "Deploy Asset" lifecycle task and assigns it to the Product Owner. This task acts as a reminder notice to carry out deployment. The implementer should ensure the Product Owner role is correctly populated on the AI system record so the reminder is routed to the right person, and that all prior pre-deployment activities (risks and issues addressed, compliance confirmed) are complete before acting on this task.
