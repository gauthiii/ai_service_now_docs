## What Is This?

The AI Case Manager is one of the AI governance personas defined for the ServiceNow AI Control Tower. This persona has full visibility into all AI cases and inquiries across the organization and is responsible for overseeing how those cases are resolved. It sits within the AI Case Management workflow, providing oversight above the AI Case Analyst and AI Case Submitter roles.

---

## Persona Description

The AI Case Manager has full visibility into all AI cases and inquiries across the organization. They oversee case resolution, monitor case volume and severity, and ensure timely investigation and closure.

### Responsibilities

- Full visibility into all AI cases and inquiries across the organization
- Oversee case resolution
- Monitor case volume and severity
- Ensure timely investigation and closure

### Example Titles

Compliance Case Manager, AI Governance Manager, AI Operations Manager, Head of AI Case Management

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When mapping personas to ServiceNow roles for AI Case Management, assign the AI Case Manager persona to individuals who need organization-wide oversight of AI cases rather than hands-on investigation of individual cases (which belongs to the AI Case Analyst). Use the example titles to identify candidates within the organization, then map this persona to the corresponding ServiceNow role per the Persona-to-Role Mapping section that follows.
