## What Is This?

AI Case Admin is one of the personas defined for AI governance and case management within the ServiceNow AI Control Tower. This persona holds full administrative access to case management functions and is responsible for configuring the AI Case Management module. It fits into implementation as the technical administrator role that sets up the case management capabilities used by other case-related personas.

---

## Role Description

The AI Case Admin has full administrative access to case management functions, including configuration of case types, state models, and assessment templates. This role is typically held by a platform administrator responsible for configuring the AI Case Management module.

## Example Titles

- ServiceNow Platform Administrator
- AI Governance Platform Admin
- GRC System Administrator

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

During implementation, assign the AI Case Admin persona to the platform administrator who will configure the AI Case Management module. This person sets up the foundational elements—case types, state models, and assessment templates—that enable the AI Case Submitter, AI Case Analyst, and AI Case Manager personas to perform their work. Because this is an administrative configuration role rather than an operational case-handling role, it should be granted to a trusted platform or GRC system administrator with the appropriate platform access.
