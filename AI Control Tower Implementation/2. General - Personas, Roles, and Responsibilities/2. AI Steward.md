## What Is This?

The AI Steward is one of the three core personas that AI Control Tower (AICT) is designed around, corresponding to the primary organizational roles responsible for AI governance. This persona is accountable for the platform's data assets and AI capabilities, ensuring data quality, integrity, and ethical use. It maps to one or more out-of-the-box system roles in the platform and sits at the center of AI governance within AICT implementation.

---

## Role Description

The AI Steward persona is accountable for (SC1) the platform's data assets and AI capabilities, ensuring data quality, integrity, and ethical use. The steward implements governance policies to maintain compliance, security, and responsible AI practices across ServiceNow applications and workflows. They oversee AI governance, aligning AI initiatives with organizational values, legal standards, and risk management practices. The role includes monitoring AI performance, bias, and compliance, fostering transparency, and promoting fairness.

They collaborate across departments to implement best practices, educate teams on AI usage, and guide decision-making about AI adoption. The AI Steward acts as a bridge between technical teams, leadership, and external stakeholders to ensure AI serves both business goals and societal good.

## Example Titles

- Chief AI Officer (CAIO)
- VP of AI Strategy
- Head of AI Governance
- AI Program Director
- Director of Data & AI
- AI Centre of Excellence Lead

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing AICT, map the AI Steward persona to the corresponding out-of-the-box system role(s) in the platform so that the accountable stakeholder has the appropriate access to oversee data assets and AI capabilities. Use this persona to assign responsibility for governance policy implementation, AI performance and bias monitoring, compliance, and cross-departmental education. As a core persona, the AI Steward should be educated on responsible AI principles and engaged early to bridge technical teams, leadership, and external stakeholders.
