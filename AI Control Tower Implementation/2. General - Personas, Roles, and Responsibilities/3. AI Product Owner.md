## What Is This?

The AI Product Owner is one of the three core personas in AI Control Tower (AICT) that correspond to the primary organizational roles responsible for AI governance. Each core persona maps to one or more out-of-the-box system roles in the platform. The AI Product Owner drives the development and delivery of AI-powered products, aligning them with business goals and user needs.

---

## Role Description

The AI Product Owner drives the development and delivery of AI-powered products, aligning them with business goals and user needs. They define product vision, prioritize features, and manage the AI product backlog. Working closely with data scientists, engineers, and stakeholders, they translate business requirements into technical deliverables.

They ensure the AI solution is viable, valuable, and ethical, balancing innovation with compliance. Throughout the product lifecycle, they monitor performance, gather feedback, and adjust priorities to optimize outcomes and deliver measurable value.

## Example Titles

- AI Product Manager
- ML Product Owner
- AI Solutions Lead
- Technical Product Manager – AI
- AI Use Case Owner
- AI Applications Manager

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When mapping your organization's stakeholders to AICT, identify who plays the AI Product Owner role (often holding titles such as AI Product Manager or AI Use Case Owner) and assign them the corresponding out-of-the-box system role in the platform. This persona is responsible for product vision, backlog prioritization, and translating business requirements into technical deliverables, so ensure they are educated on responsible AI principles and have access to performance monitoring and feedback mechanisms throughout the product lifecycle.
