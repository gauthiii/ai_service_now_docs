## What Is This?

The AI Risk / Compliance Manager is one of the three core personas that AICT is designed around, corresponding to a primary organizational role responsible for AI governance. This persona focuses on identifying, assessing, and mitigating risks related to AI use in the business and ensuring AI systems comply with laws, regulations, and internal policies. Each core persona maps to one or more out-of-the-box system roles in the platform.

---

## Role Description

The AI Risk and Compliance Manager identifies, assesses, and mitigates risks related to AI use in the business. They ensure AI systems comply with laws, regulations, and internal policies, addressing issues such as bias, privacy, and transparency. Within the AI Centre of Excellence, they develop frameworks, standards, and controls for safe AI deployment across teams.

They collaborate with legal, IT, and business units to monitor AI governance, conduct audits, and manage incidents. Their role ensures responsible AI use, minimizes liability, and builds trust in AI solutions organization-wide.

## Example Titles

- Chief Risk Officer (CRO)
- Chief Compliance Officer (CCO)
- VP of AI Risk
- Director of AI Compliance
- GRC Manager – AI
- AI Ethics & Compliance Lead
- Head of Responsible AI

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When mapping organizational stakeholders to AICT, identify which individuals fill the AI Risk / Compliance Manager persona (using the example titles as a guide) and map them to the corresponding out-of-the-box system role(s) in the platform. Ensure this persona is engaged to develop the frameworks, standards, and controls for safe AI deployment, and to coordinate with legal, IT, and business units for governance monitoring, audits, and incident management. As one of the three core personas (alongside AI Steward and AI Product Owner), this role is central to establishing responsible AI governance during implementation.
