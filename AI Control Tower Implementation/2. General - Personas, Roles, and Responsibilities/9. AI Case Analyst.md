## What Is This?

The AI Case Analyst is one of the personas defined for AI governance and case management within the ServiceNow AI Control Tower. This persona is responsible for reviewing and investigating AI cases and inquiries that are assigned to them. It fits into the case management workflow between the AI Case Submitter (who initiates cases) and higher-level oversight roles such as the AI Case Manager and AI Case Admin.

---

## Persona Description

The AI Case Analyst reviews and investigates AI cases and inquiries assigned to them. They identify impacted areas such as policies, regulations, and enterprise-wide compliance risks, and manage issues related to those areas to address root causes.

**Example titles:** Compliance Case Analyst, AI Incident Investigator, GRC Analyst, AI Compliance Specialist

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing AI case management, map individuals who investigate and resolve assigned AI cases to the AI Case Analyst persona. Their scope is limited to the cases and inquiries assigned to them, focusing on identifying impacted policies, regulations, and compliance risks, and managing the related issues to address root causes. This persona works alongside the AI Case Submitter (case initiation), AI Case Manager (full case visibility and oversight), and AI Case Admin (administrative configuration of the AI Case Management module).
