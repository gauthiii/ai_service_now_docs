## What Is This?

The AI Governance Stakeholder is one of the personas defined in the ServiceNow AI Control Tower implementation. It represents business users and subject matter experts who participate in AI governance activities without managing AI assets or leading risk assessments. This persona fits into the broader Personas, Roles, and Responsibilities model used to assign appropriate access and responsibilities across an AI governance program.

---

## Persona Description

AI Governance Stakeholders are business users, subject matter experts, or team members who participate in AI governance activities but do not manage AI assets or lead risk assessments. They may submit AI cases, complete assigned tasks such as control attestations, or require read-only visibility into AI systems and assessments for oversight or reporting purposes.

**Example titles:** Business Unit Leader, Department Head, Legal Counsel, Privacy Officer, Internal Auditor, Data Governance Lead, Enterprise Architect, Security Analyst

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing the AI Control Tower personas, assign the AI Governance Stakeholder persona to business users and subject matter experts who need to participate in governance but should not have asset-management or risk-assessment-lead capabilities. Provision them with the ability to submit AI cases and complete assigned tasks (such as control attestations), and grant read-only visibility into AI systems and assessments for oversight or reporting. This persona is distinct from analyst/manager roles that perform hands-on risk and compliance work, so scope access accordingly.
