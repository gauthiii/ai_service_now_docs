## What Is This?

The AI Case Submitter is a persona within the AICT personas, roles, and responsibilities model. It represents the entry point into the AI case management process — the employees or business users who report AI-related cases or raise inquiries. This persona initiates case intake but does not investigate or manage cases.

---

## Persona Definition

AI Case Submitters are employees or business users who report AI-related cases or raise inquiries through the Employee Center. They do not investigate or manage cases but initiate the case management process by submitting relevant details and supporting documentation.

**Example titles:** Any employee or business user who interacts with AI systems and needs to report an issue, concern, or question

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing AI case management, treat the AI Case Submitter as the front-end intake role. Provision broad access through the Employee Center so any employee or business user interacting with AI systems can submit cases or inquiries with supporting documentation. Submitters do not need investigation or management privileges; downstream personas such as the AI Case Analyst, AI Case Manager, and AI Case Admin handle review, oversight, and configuration. Ensure the Employee Center intake forms capture the relevant details and documentation needed to start the case management process.
