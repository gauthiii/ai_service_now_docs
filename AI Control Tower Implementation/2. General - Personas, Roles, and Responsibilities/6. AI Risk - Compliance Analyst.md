## What Is This?

The AI Risk / Compliance Analyst is one of the supporting personas in AI Control Tower (AICT). It is a hands-on, narrowly scoped role that operates under the direction of a core persona to perform day-to-day risk and compliance work on assigned AI systems. It fits into AICT's persona model as the execution-level counterpart to the AI Risk / Compliance Manager.

---

## Role Description

The AI Risk and Compliance Analyst performs hands-on risk and compliance work on assigned AI systems. Unlike the Manager role, the Analyst's access is limited to records specifically assigned to them. They execute impact assessments, manage lifecycle tasks, perform risk assessments, and complete control attestations within their assigned scope.

### Example Titles

- GRC Analyst
- IT Risk Analyst
- AI Compliance Analyst
- Technology Risk Analyst
- AI Audit Analyst

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When mapping organizational roles to AICT system roles, assign the AI Risk / Compliance Analyst persona to individuals who carry out operational risk and compliance tasks rather than oversee them. Because their access is scoped only to records assigned to them, configure permissions so that they can execute impact assessments, lifecycle tasks, risk assessments, and control attestations within their assigned scope. This persona is a supporting role that typically operates under the direction of a core persona (such as the AI Risk / Compliance Manager).
