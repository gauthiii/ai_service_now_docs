## What Is This?

This subtopic lists the key value benefits delivered by the AI Risk & Compliance Content capability in ServiceNow. It explains what an implementer gains from using the application's downloadable AI Regulations and Frameworks content, which can be linked to internal control objectives or risk statements and used to run audits or assessments. It sits within the Govern - Risk and Controls area, following the Overview of the AI Risk & Compliance Content.

---

## Key Value Benefits

- Provides the ability to choose the framework you want to use and activate.
- Helps you to choose the citations that are associated with a particular framework and install them in the active state in your instance.
- Enables you to choose control objectives, which are associated with the selected citations.
- Helps you to select the risk statements.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

An implementer applies these benefits by selecting and activating the relevant regulatory framework (for example, the EU AI Act or NIST AI RMF), installing the associated citations into the active state in the instance, and then choosing the control objectives tied to those citations and the corresponding risk statements. These capabilities support linking regulatory content to internal control objectives or risk statements for auditing and assessment.
