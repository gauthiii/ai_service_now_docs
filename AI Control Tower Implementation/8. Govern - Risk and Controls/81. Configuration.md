## What Is This?

This subtopic covers the configuration step for the AI Risk & Compliance Content application within the Govern - Risk and Controls area. After installing and activating regulatory frameworks (EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF), configuration is where you bring your own control objectives into the application in bulk. It fits at the point in implementation where you map internal control objectives to the installed regulations or frameworks.

---

## Configuration

A. Import: Users can import control objectives in bulk using an Excel spreadsheet by following the step-by-step instructions provided in the community post (link).

### Note

**Regulatory Support Statement**

ServiceNow's Risk products are built to help our customers address regulatory requirements under various jurisdictions across the globe. ServiceNow aims to provide software updates for new or updated major regulations and requirements within twelve to eighteen months of the regulation's publication.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Before configuring, complete the install and activation steps for the desired framework (EU AI Act and/or NIST AI RMF). To configure, use the bulk import option: prepare an Excel spreadsheet of your control objectives and follow the step-by-step instructions in the referenced community post to import them. Keep in mind the regulatory support statement — ServiceNow aims to deliver updates for new or updated major regulations within twelve to eighteen months of publication, so plan for content updates accordingly.
