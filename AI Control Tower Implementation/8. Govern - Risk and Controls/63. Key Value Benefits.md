## What Is This?

"Key Value Benefits" summarizes the core value that specific Govern capabilities deliver within the Risk and Controls area of the AI Control Tower. On this page, the benefits are stated for two capabilities: Indicators (Control Indicators) and Issues. They capture, in brief, why an implementer would adopt these capabilities as part of ongoing risk and control governance.

---

## Key Value Benefits — Indicators

In the context of Indicators (metrics used to monitor risk exposure or control performance over time, including Control Indicators across the control lifecycle):

- They support ongoing oversight and early warning.

---

## Key Value Benefits — Issues

In the context of Issues (identified control failures, compliance gaps, or unacceptable risk levels managed through the ServiceNow Issue Management process):

- They drive remediation and accountability.

Example: Failure to document training data lineage for a high-risk AI model.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing the Govern – Risk and Controls capabilities, use these benefit statements to justify and scope adoption:

- Enable Indicators to provide ongoing oversight and early warning on control performance and risk exposure. Indicators are most logically created and used once a control reaches the Monitor stage, though ServiceNow also allows creating control indicators during the Draft stage.
- Enable Issues so that identified control failures, compliance gaps, or unacceptable risk levels drive remediation and accountability. A representative trigger is a failure to document training data lineage for a high-risk AI model.
