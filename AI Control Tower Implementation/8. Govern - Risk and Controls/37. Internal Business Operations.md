## What Is This?

Internal business operations describe the internal business objectives that define what an organization wants to achieve by using AI. Within the Govern - Risk and Controls area, this subtopic frames how AI is applied to support business goals, policies, and control measures responsibly and safely. It helps align AI initiatives with company values, risk tolerance, and legal responsibilities.

---

## Internal Business Objectives

Internal business objectives describe what the organization wants to achieve by using AI. These goals may include:

- Improving efficiency
- Reducing costs
- Increasing accuracy
- Enhancing customer experience
- Supporting better decision-making

This section explains how AI is used to support business goals, policies and control measures while ensuring it is done responsibly and safely. It helps align AI initiatives with company values, risk tolerance, and legal responsibilities, so AI delivers benefits without creating unintended harm or risk.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing AICT governance, use internal business objectives as the anchor for defining and prioritizing AI use cases. Translate organizational goals (efficiency, cost reduction, accuracy, customer experience, better decision-making) into measurable AI initiatives, then ensure each is governed against the organization's policies, control measures, risk tolerance, and legal responsibilities. This alignment ensures AI delivers intended benefits responsibly while avoiding unintended harm or risk.
