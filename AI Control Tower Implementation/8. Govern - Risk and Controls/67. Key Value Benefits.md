## What Is This?

"Key Value Benefits" summarizes the business value delivered by the risk and control constructs in the Govern phase — specifically Control Indicators and Issues. It captures, in brief, why these constructs matter for ongoing risk and control governance of AI within ServiceNow. It fits alongside the Overview and Procedure content for each construct.

---

## Key Value Benefits — Control Indicators

Indicators are metrics used to monitor risk exposure or control performance over time. Control Indicators support ongoing oversight and early warning.

- They support ongoing oversight and early warning.

---

## Key Value Benefits — Issues

Issues represent identified control failures, compliance gaps, or unacceptable risk levels.

- They drive remediation and accountability.

Example: Failure to document training data lineage for a high-risk AI model.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Use these benefit statements to justify and frame the implementation of Control Indicators and Issues in the Govern phase. Control Indicators are best activated when a control reaches the Monitor stage (the most logical time to start using control indicators and generating indicator tasks), delivering ongoing oversight and early warning. Issues should be implemented to drive remediation and accountability when control failures, compliance gaps, or unacceptable risk levels are identified — for example, failure to document training data lineage for a high-risk AI model.
