## What Is This?

This subtopic covers the configuration step within the Issues area of the Govern – Risk and Controls process. After issues (identified control failures, compliance gaps, or unacceptable risk levels) can be created and remediated, this configuration enables issue mapping so that issues integrate correctly with the broader risk and compliance modules. It fits into implementation as the setup that connects the Issue Management process to related governance modules.

## How to Use This in Implementation

An implementer applies this by configuring Issue Mapping (via the referenced link) as part of setting up the Issue Management process. This configuration supports the create and remediate issue procedures and ensures issues integrate seamlessly with related modules such as AI Risk Management and AI Policy and Compliance.
