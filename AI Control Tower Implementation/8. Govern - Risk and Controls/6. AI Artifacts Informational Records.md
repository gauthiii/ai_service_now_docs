## What Is This?

AI Artifacts Informational Records describe the core informational records used to govern AI within the organization. This subtopic introduces the first such artifact, the AI System / Use Case, which acts as the central inventory of all AI-enabled business use cases. It establishes the foundation for governance, risk assessment, and compliance activities across the AI Risk & Compliance solution.

---

## AI System / Use Case

### Overview

The AI System / Use Case Registry is a central inventory of all AI-enabled business use cases within the organization.

### Key Value Benefits

It provides visibility, ownership, and accountability for each AI system and serves as the starting point for governance, risk assessment, and compliance activities.

Example: An "Automated Loan Approval System" used by the Retail Banking entity to support customer credit decisions.

## How to Use This in Implementation

Implementers begin AI governance by registering each AI-enabled business use case in the AI System / Use Case Registry, which serves as the central inventory and the starting point for all downstream governance, risk, and compliance work. For each entry, capture ownership and accountability details so that visibility is maintained across the organization (for example, an "Automated Loan Approval System" owned by Retail Banking). Use the "Request an AI use case / system" procedure to create new records, then proceed to the related configuration steps to complete setup.
