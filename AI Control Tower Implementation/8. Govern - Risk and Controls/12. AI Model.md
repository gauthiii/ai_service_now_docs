## What Is This?

The AI Model Registry is a catalog of the AI models that support one or more AI systems or use cases. It provides technical transparency and governance at the model level within the Govern – Risk and Controls area of AI Control Tower. It ensures that changes to models are controlled and traceable as part of the overall AI governance lifecycle.

---

## Overview

The AI Model Registry is a catalog of the AI models that support one or more AI systems or use cases.

## Key Value Benefits

It enables technical transparency, version tracking, and model-level governance, ensuring changes to models are controlled and traceable.

Example: A machine-learning classification model used to predict loan default risk, version 2.3, deployed in production.

## How to Use This in Implementation

Implementers use the AI Model Registry to catalog each AI model backing their AI systems or use cases, capturing details such as model type, version, and deployment status (for example, version 2.3 in production). To register a model, follow the "Request an AI Model" procedure. Configuration of the registry follows the Govern – Lifecycle topic referenced in this document, so review that topic for the detailed setup and change-control steps that keep model changes traceable.
