## What Is This?

This subtopic covers the Configuration step for Authority Documents within the Govern - Risk and Controls practice. For Authority Documents, no specific configuration is required; the value is "Not Applicable." Supporting notes clarify how relationships and compliance scoring work for Authority Documents in Policy & Compliance.

Not Applicable

## How to Use This in Implementation

When working with Authority Documents, there is no dedicated configuration step to perform. Implementers should instead use the "Regulated By" form field on the Authority Document to establish the relationship with the relevant Agency. Be aware that the Compliance Score for an Authority Document (AD) is computed automatically as the average of its child control objectives, which in turn are averages of their child controls — a core Policy & Compliance behavior that influences how compliance is rolled up.
