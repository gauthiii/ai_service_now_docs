## What Is This?

This subtopic describes the key value benefits of Policies within the Govern - Risk and Controls capability of AI Control Tower. Policies are formal organizational statements that define expectations, principles, and rules for AI use. This explains why policies matter in the governance implementation.

---

## Key Value Benefits

They set the tone and governance standards for AI use.

Example: Enterprise Responsible AI Policy defining fairness, transparency, and accountability principles.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing AI governance in AI Control Tower, define policies that establish the governance standards and tone for AI use across the organization (for example, an Enterprise Responsible AI Policy covering fairness, transparency, and accountability principles). These policies provide the foundation against which control objectives are mapped and measured.
