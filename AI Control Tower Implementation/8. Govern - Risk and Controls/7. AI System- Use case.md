## What Is This?

The AI System / Use Case Registry is a central inventory of all AI-enabled business use cases within an organization. It is an AI Artifacts Informational Record that provides visibility, ownership, and accountability for each AI system. Within the Govern - Risk and Controls practice, it serves as the starting point for governance, risk assessment, and compliance activities.

---

## Overview

The AI System / Use Case Registry is a central inventory of all AI-enabled business use cases within the organization.

## Key Value Benefits

It provides visibility, ownership, and accountability for each AI system and serves as the starting point for governance, risk assessment, and compliance activities.

Example: An "Automated Loan Approval System" used by the Retail Banking entity to support customer credit decisions.

## How to Use This in Implementation

Implementers use the AI System / Use Case Registry as the foundational record for AI governance: register each AI-enabled business use case to establish a central inventory, assign ownership and accountability, and use it as the entry point for downstream governance, risk assessment, and compliance work. To onboard a new entry, follow the "Request an AI use case/ system" procedure. Configuration of the registry record is performed in the Configuration step.
