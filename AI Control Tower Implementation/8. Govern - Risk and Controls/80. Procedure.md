## What Is This?

This subtopic lists the high-level procedure for getting AI Risk & Compliance content into a ServiceNow instance. It covers installing the content pack and activating the supported regulatory frameworks (EU AI Act and NIST AI RMF) so that their citations, control objectives, and risk statements become available for governance work. It is the step that precedes detailed configuration of imported control objectives.

## How to Use This in Implementation

To make AI Risk & Compliance content usable in your instance, first install the content pack from the store. Then activate the regulatory frameworks you intend to use — EU AI Act and/or NIST AI RMF — which installs their associated citations, control objectives, and risk statements in the active state. Activating a framework is a prerequisite for linking its content to internal control objectives or risk statements and for running audits/assessments against them. After completing this procedure, proceed to configuration (for example, bulk-importing control objectives via the documented Excel import process).
