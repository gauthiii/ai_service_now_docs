## What Is This?

The Dataset entry represents the Dataset Registry, an inventory of the datasets used to train, test, or operate AI models within ServiceNow AI Control Tower. It documents where data comes from and how it is used, supporting governance over the data that underpins AI systems. It fits into the Govern - Risk and Controls area as a registry that complements the AI Model Registry.

---

## Overview

The Dataset Registry is an inventory of datasets used to train, test, or operate AI models.

## Key Value Benefits

It supports data transparency, privacy, and quality management by documenting where data comes from and how it is used.

## How to Use This in Implementation

To use the Dataset Registry, an implementer requests a dataset through the provided request flow ("Request a Dataset") to register a dataset used to train, test, or operate an AI model. Configuration of the registry follows the guidance in the Govern – Lifecycle topic of this document. Maintaining accurate dataset records helps ensure data transparency, privacy, and quality management across AI systems.
