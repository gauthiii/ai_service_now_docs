## What Is This?

Risks are recorded, assessed risk instances that are actively managed within ServiceNow AI Risk and Compliance. Unlike a Risk Statement (a pre-defined library description), a Risk is an actual instance tied to an AI system that is tracked through prioritization, treatment, and monitoring. This fits into the Govern phase as the operational record an organization manages over time.

---

## Overview

## Key Value Benefits

They enable prioritization, treatment, and monitoring.

Example: Bias risk associated with an automated credit approval model used in multiple jurisdictions.

## How to Use This in Implementation

An implementer creates a Risk to capture an actual, assessed risk instance for an AI system so it can be prioritized, treated, and monitored. Risks build on Risk Statements from the risk library—when a Risk Statement is attached to an AI System via Impact Assessment answers, it produces individual risk records that are then managed as Risks. Use the "Create Risk" procedure to register a new risk instance.
