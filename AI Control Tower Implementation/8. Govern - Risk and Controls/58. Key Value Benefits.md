## What Is This?

The "Key Value Benefits" subtopic summarizes the primary value that two governance constructs deliver within AI Risk and Compliance: Risk Statements and Risks. These short statements explain why each construct matters in the broader Govern - Risk and Controls workflow, helping implementers understand the purpose behind configuring and managing them.

---

## Key Value Benefits — Risk Statements

Within the Risk Statements area, the Key Value Benefit is:

- They ensure risks are clearly and consistently articulated.

This relates to Risk Statements, which describe a specific AI-related risk in a structured format (typically including cause, event, and impact). The AI Risk Statement Library in ServiceNow AI Risk and Compliance provides a centralized, structured collection of pre-defined AI risk statements that organizations can use to identify, assess, and manage AI risks consistently across the enterprise.

---

## Key Value Benefits — Risks

Within the Risks area, the Key Value Benefit is:

- They enable prioritization, treatment, and monitoring.

This relates to Risks, which are recorded, assessed risk instances that are actively managed.

Example: Bias risk associated with an automated credit approval model used in multiple jurisdictions.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Implementers use these benefit statements as the rationale for adopting the Risk Statement and Risk constructs. Configure pre-defined Risk Statements (via the Risk Statement Library) so that risks are articulated consistently before AI systems go through the workflow. Once Risk Statements are attached to AI Systems through Impact Assessment answers, individual Risk records are created and can then be prioritized, treated, and monitored. There are no separate configuration steps in this subtopic beyond following the related Procedures (Create Risk Statement, Create Risk).
