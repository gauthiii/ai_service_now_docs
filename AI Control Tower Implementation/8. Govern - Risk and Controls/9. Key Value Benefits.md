## What Is This?

This subtopic describes the key value benefits of the AI System / Use Case Registry within the AI Artifacts Informational Records area of the Govern - Risk and Controls practice. The AI System / Use Case Registry is a central inventory of all AI-enabled business use cases within the organization, and this section explains the governance value it delivers.

---

## Key Value Benefits

It provides visibility, ownership, and accountability for each AI system and serves as the starting point for governance, risk assessment, and compliance activities.

Example: An "Automated Loan Approval System" used by the Retail Banking entity to support customer credit decisions.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing the AI System / Use Case Registry, treat it as the central inventory and starting point for all governance, risk assessment, and compliance activities. Registering each AI-enabled business use case establishes clear visibility, ownership, and accountability per system. Use the example pattern (e.g., an "Automated Loan Approval System" owned by a Retail Banking entity) to capture the system name, owning business entity, and the business decision it supports.
