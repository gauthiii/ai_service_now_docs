## What Is This?

This Overview introduces Citations within the Govern - Risk and Controls area of ServiceNow AI Control Tower. Citations are the specific, actionable requirements or clauses contained within an authority document. They define the concrete obligations an organization must comply with when governing AI.

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing AI governance in AI Control Tower, an implementer first establishes authority documents, then breaks them down into citations — the individual, actionable clauses (for example, an EU AI Act article on human oversight or an ISO 42001 clause on AI risk assessment) that the organization must comply with. The procedure is to create each citation and then map it to the relevant controls (Citation to Control Mapping), so that compliance obligations are tied directly to the controls that enforce them.
