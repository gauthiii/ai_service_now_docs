## What Is This?

This subtopic describes the procedure for working with Citations in the Govern - Risk and Controls area of AI Control Tower. Citations are specific, actionable requirements or clauses within an authority document. This procedure covers the steps to create a citation and map it to a control.

## How to Use This in Implementation

To implement citations, an implementer first creates a citation (a specific, actionable requirement or clause within an authority document, defining what must be complied with — e.g., an EU AI Act Article requiring human oversight for high-risk AI systems, or an ISO 42001 clause on AI risk assessment), then performs Citation to Control Mapping to link the citation to the relevant control. Citations belong to an authority document, so the authority document should exist first. Refer to the linked Community Post for additional guidance.
