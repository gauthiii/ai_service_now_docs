## What Is This?

This subtopic describes the procedure steps for working with Regulatory Agencies in the ServiceNow AI Control Tower. Regulatory Agencies are external regulatory, supervisory, or standard-setting bodies that issue requirements applicable to AI systems. The procedure outlines the actions needed to register an agency and link it to its authority documents within the centralized agency library.

---

## Procedure Steps

The procedure for Regulatory Agencies consists of the following steps:

A. Add a Regulatory Agency (link)
B. Relate Authority Document (link)

### Configuration

Not Applicable

### Note

- In Authority Document, use the form field "Regulated By" to build a relation with the Agency.
- 360-degree view association is not supported.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

An implementer registers each relevant regulatory authority into the centralized agency library, then associates the corresponding authority documents to it. To build the relationship, set the "Regulated By" field on the Authority Document form so that the document points back to its issuing Agency. Note that there is no additional configuration required for this procedure, and the 360-degree view association is not supported for this relationship.
