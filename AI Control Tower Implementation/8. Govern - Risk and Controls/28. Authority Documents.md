## What Is This?

Authority Documents are official regulatory texts, standards, or guidance issued by an agency. Within AI Control Tower's Govern - Risk and Controls capability, they serve as the source of compliance obligations that AI systems must meet. They are typically related to the Regulatory Agencies that issue them.

## Overview

## How to Use This in Implementation

Use Authority Documents to establish the source of compliance obligations for AI systems, capturing official regulatory texts, standards, or guidance such as the EU Artificial Intelligence Act, ISO/IEC 42001 AI Management System, and NIST AI Risk Management Framework. Relate each Authority Document to its issuing Regulatory Agency — in the Authority Document form, the "Regulated By" field is used to build the relation with the Agency.
