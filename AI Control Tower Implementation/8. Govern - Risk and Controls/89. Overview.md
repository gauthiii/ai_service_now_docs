## What Is This?

This is the Overview of the Assessment Template capability within the AI Control Tower. It introduces the robust suite of assessment tools that evaluate AI use cases for potential risks and regulatory conformity. It sits in the Govern (Risk and Controls) stage of implementation, where assessments are triggered during onboarding and pre-deployment of an AI Asset.

---

## Assessment Template Overview

The AI Control Tower offers a robust suite of assessment tools, including the AI Impact Assessment, which evaluates AI use cases to identify potential risks such as copyright issues, bias, privacy breaches, misinformation, and surveillance. Additionally, the AI Control Tower provides AI Impact Assessments specifically designed for EU AI Act conformity.

The AI Impact assessment determines the mappings of the Risks and Controls to the AI Assets. These are outlined in the risk mapping section below. This is triggered during the assess stage of the onboarding process of the AI Asset.

The AI Impact Assessment for Conformity is to ensure that high-risk AI systems do not pose harm to users, society, or the environment, and meet regulatory obligations as per the AI Act. This is triggered via the playbook during the pre-deployment checks.

## How to Use This in Implementation

Implementers use the Assessment Template suite to drive two distinct assessment flows. Use the AI Impact Assessment during the assess stage of AI Asset onboarding to map Risks and Controls to AI Assets. Use the AI Impact Assessment for Conformity (EU AI Act) via the playbook during pre-deployment checks to validate that high-risk AI systems meet regulatory obligations. The risk mapping section (referenced below in the source) defines how Risks and Controls associate with AI Assets.
