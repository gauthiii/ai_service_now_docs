## What Is This?

This subtopic summarizes the key value benefits delivered by the Assessment Template capability within the AI Control Tower. It highlights the practical advantages that the AI Impact Assessment and related assessment tools provide to an organization's risk program. It fits at the conclusion of the Assessment Template overview in the Govern - Risk and Controls stage.

---

## Key Value Benefits

- Offers an intuitive user experience
- Ensure information is accurate, easier to analyze, and drives better decisions on your risk program
- Assessments that used to take weeks to develop can now be created in hours or days

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When adopting the AI Control Tower's Assessment Template capabilities, implementers can position these benefits to stakeholders: the assessment tooling provides an intuitive user experience, improves data accuracy and analyzability to support better risk-program decisions, and dramatically reduces the time to build assessments (from weeks to hours or days). These outcomes are realized through the AI Impact Assessment and the related assessment templates configured during the assess stage of AI asset onboarding.
