## What Is This?

This subtopic describes the procedure for initiating AI System Intake within the AI Control Tower — specifically, how to request a new AI use case. It ensures AI initiatives originate from real business needs rather than ad-hoc experimentation, and it is the entry point that registers a new AI system, model, or dataset in ServiceNow.

---

## Procedure

Requesting an AI Use Case: This ensures AI initiatives originate from real business needs rather than ad-hoc experimentation.

Request new AI Asset from Employee Portal (link)

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

To apply this procedure, an implementer directs the Business Owner or Product Owner to submit a request for a new AI asset (AI system, use case, model, or dataset) through the Employee Portal / Employee Center. This is performed at the very beginning of the process, before any review starts. The submitted service catalog request creates the AI System/Dataset or Model record in ServiceNow in Draft state and kicks off the governance workflow so that review can begin. Following this intake procedure keeps AI initiatives grounded in genuine business needs rather than ad-hoc experimentation.
