# Workspace Verification

> ServiceNow AI Control Tower (AICT) — Implementation Guide
> Section: General - Prelaunch → Workspace Verification
> Source: AI-Control-Tower.pdf, p.31

---

## What Is This?

Workspace Verification is a prelaunch checklist that confirms both AICT-related workspaces are accessible and rendering correctly for the provisioned roles before the AI asset inventory is populated. It validates that the AI Control Tower Workspace and the AI Risk & Compliance Workspace open at the expected URL/path and display their core tabs. This step fits after role provisioning and before initial inventory population.

---

## Workspace Verification Checklist

Verify that both workspaces are accessible and displaying correctly for the provisioned roles before populating the inventory.

| ✓ | Workspace Verification |
| --- | --- |
| ☐ | Verify AI Control Tower Workspace is accessible at URL path: ai-governance-workspace |
| ☐ | Verify AI Risk & Compliance Workspace is accessible for risk and compliance personas |
| ☐ | Confirm the Overview tab displays AI system counts, type breakdown, and risk classification |
| ☐ | Confirm the AI Asset Inventory tab displays the initial set of AI assets |

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

After completing role provisioning (Core Roles), use this checklist to confirm the workspaces function for the intended users. Navigate to the AI Control Tower Workspace at the URL path `ai-governance-workspace` and confirm it loads, then verify the AI Risk & Compliance Workspace is reachable by the risk and compliance personas. Within the AI Control Tower Workspace, check that the Overview tab shows AI system counts, the type breakdown, and risk classification, and that the AI Asset Inventory tab shows the initial set of AI assets. Successful verification is a prerequisite to the next step, Initial Inventory Population.
