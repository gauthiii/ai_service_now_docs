## What Is This?

Case Management Configuration covers setting up the AI Case Management module so it can handle AI-related cases and inquiries. As part of the General - Prelaunch activities, it ensures that case types and SLA definitions are configured in line with the organization's governance expectations before go-live.

---

## Overview

Configure the AI Case Management module for handling AI-related cases and inquiries. Configuration of case types and SLA definitions should align with your organization's risk and compliance governance framework.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

During prelaunch, configure the AI Case Management module to handle AI-related cases and inquiries. When defining case types and SLA definitions, align them with your organization's risk and compliance governance framework so that case handling reflects established governance expectations.
