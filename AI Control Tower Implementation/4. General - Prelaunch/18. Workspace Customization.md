## What Is This?

Workspace Customization covers tailoring the AI Control Tower workspace layouts and dashboards beyond the default configuration to meet organizational needs. It applies to the AI Control Tower Workspace and the AI Risk & Compliance Workspace, including homepage widgets, list view filters, and role-based dashboard views. This step occurs during prelaunch, after role provisioning and inventory population are complete.

---

## Workspace Customization Checklist

Customize workspace layouts and dashboards beyond the default configuration to meet organizational needs. Complete role provisioning and inventory population before customizing workspace views.

## How to Use This in Implementation

Before starting workspace customization, ensure role provisioning and inventory population have been completed, as these are prerequisites for meaningful workspace views. Configure homepage widgets on both the AI Control Tower Workspace (risk heatmaps, compliance overview, case summaries) and the AI Risk & Compliance Workspace. Set up list view filters for each workspace tab and configure role-based dashboard views as needed. Finally, verify that the Security & Privacy tab correctly displays the AI Security Score, access metrics, and AI-generated insights.
