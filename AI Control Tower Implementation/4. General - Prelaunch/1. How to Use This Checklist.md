## What Is This?

This is the orientation for the AICT prelaunch checklist, which is organized into two deployment levels. It tells implementers to begin with Foundational items to establish a working AICT deployment with core governance capabilities, then progress to Full Functionality items based on the organization's governance maturity and priorities. It sits at the start of the General - Prelaunch section as the roadmap for the rest of the checklist.

---

## Overview

This checklist is divided into two deployment levels. Begin with foundational items to establish a functioning AICT deployment with core governance capabilities. After that, progress to Full Functionality items according to your organization's governance maturity and priorities.

## Deployment Levels

| Foundational | Full Functionality |
| --- | --- |
| Minimum steps to get AICT operational with core governance capabilities. | Complete AICT capability set, built on a working Foundational deployment. |
| Stakeholder identification | Full stakeholder alignment |
| Plugin activation | Governance framework configuration |
| Core role provisioning | Hyperscaler discovery |
| Inventory intake configuration | AI case management |
| Out-of-the-box lifecycle validation | Value and adoption tracking |

## Tip

Roles should be assigned to user groups, not individual users. This simplifies ongoing access management and ensures consistent permissions across your AICT deployment.

## Foundational Level Deployment

Foundational Level Deployment Complete these steps first. It is a must-have to bring AICT operational with core governance and inventory capabilities.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Implementers should work through the Foundational column first — stakeholder identification, plugin activation, core role provisioning, inventory intake configuration, and out-of-the-box lifecycle validation — to bring AICT operational with core governance and inventory capabilities. Only after a working Foundational deployment exists should teams advance to the Full Functionality items (full stakeholder alignment, governance framework configuration, hyperscaler discovery, AI case management, and value and adoption tracking), prioritized by governance maturity. As a key gotcha, assign roles to user groups rather than individual users to simplify ongoing access management and ensure consistent permissions.
