## What Is This?

Full Functionality Deployment is the phase that follows the Foundational checklist in the AI Control Tower (AICT) prelaunch process. It guides an organization to expand its governance capabilities beyond the core, operational baseline. Implementers work through its sections according to their organization's governance maturity and priorities.

---

## Full Functionality Deployment

Complete the Foundational checklist first. Then work through these sections based on your organization's governance maturity and priorities.

> Note on preceding context: Before reaching this phase, the "Foundational Complete" milestone confirms that if all Foundational checklist items are complete, your AICT deployment is operational with core AI asset inventory, lifecycle management, and workspace access. You proceed to Full Functionality when your organization is ready to expand governance capabilities.

The first section under Full Functionality Deployment is **Full Stakeholder Alignment** (heading only on this page; content continues on subsequent pages).

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Treat this as a gate: do not begin Full Functionality Deployment until the Foundational checklist (Initial Inventory and Basic Lifecycle Validation) is complete and the deployment is operational. Once foundational capabilities are confirmed, sequence the Full Functionality sections—starting with Full Stakeholder Alignment—according to your organization's governance maturity and priorities rather than completing them all at once.
