## What Is This?

Full Stakeholder Alignment is the first section within the Full Functionality Deployment of the AI Control Tower implementation checklist. It is reached after the Foundational checklist is complete, as the organization begins to expand its governance capabilities. On this page it appears as a section heading introduced by the Full Functionality Deployment guidance.

---

## Context: Full Functionality Deployment

Before Full Stakeholder Alignment, the guide directs implementers to complete the Foundational checklist first, then work through the Full Functionality sections based on the organization's governance maturity and priorities.

The preceding "Foundational Complete" note states: If all Foundational checklist items are complete, your AICT deployment is operational with core AI asset inventory, lifecycle management, and workspace access. Proceed to Full Functionality when your organization is ready to expand governance capabilities.
