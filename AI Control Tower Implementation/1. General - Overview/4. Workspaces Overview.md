## What Is This?

AICT provides **two primary workspaces** that serve different personas and governance functions, each designed to deliver **role-appropriate visibility and action**. This subtopic introduces the two workspaces; the following subtopics detail each one and its tabs.

---

## The Two Workspaces

| Workspace | Primary Personas | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **AI Control Tower Workspace** | AI Stewards, AI Product Owners | Centralized visibility into all AI assets and related activity across the organization. |
| **AI Risk & Compliance Workspace** | AI Risk & Compliance Managers | Visibility into the risk and compliance posture of all AI assets, including systems, models, and datasets. |

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

The split into two workspaces reflects a **separation of duties** between operational AI ownership (Stewards/Product Owners) and risk/compliance oversight (Risk & Compliance Managers). During implementation, provision each persona to the correct workspace and configure tab-level access accordingly (covered in *Access Control Implementation* later in the guide).
