## What Is This?

The **AI Risk & Compliance Workspace** provides **AI Risk & Compliance Managers** with visibility into the **risk and compliance posture of all AI assets**, including systems, models, and datasets. It is the oversight counterpart to the operational AI Control Tower Workspace. This subtopic documents each tab.

---

## Workspace Tabs

| Tab | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Risk and Compliance** | Displays the risk classification of the AI asset inventory and compliance posture based on authority documents (EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF) and internal policies. Includes risk overview, risk heatmap, and compliance scoring. |
| **Operations** | Shows AI systems segmented by state and department, with visibility into risk assessments, AI assessments, control assurance, issues, policy exceptions, and AI cases. |
| **AI Cases** | Provides cases and inquiries dashboards with trends, tracking, action tasks, and issue management. |

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

This workspace is provisioned for the **risk and compliance persona** and is driven by the Govern functional area. Its **Risk and Compliance** tab depends on authority documents (EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF) and internal policies being loaded and mapped, and on risk classifications being assigned to inventory assets. During implementation, ensure the pre-built regulatory content is activated and that assessments/controls have run so the heatmap and compliance scoring populate.
