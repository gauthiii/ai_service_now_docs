## What Is This?

Value Management is a capability in AI Control Tower (AICT) that enables organizations to quantify and visualize the business impact of AI assets across environments. It surfaces standardized dashboards on the Value tab and applies Value Templates to compute metrics for AI assets. It fits in the Measure phase of AICT implementation, where teams track productivity, engagement, utilization, and ROI of deployed AI.

---

## Overview

The Value Management capability in AI Control Tower (AICT) enables organizations to quantify and visualize the business impact of AI assets across environments.

The Value tab provides standardized dashboards that measure:

- Productivity gains
- User engagement
- System utilization
- Return on investment (ROI)

Value insights are driven by Value Templates, which define the formulas and metrics applied to AI assets, including:

- AI Skills
- AI Agents
- Agentic Workflows
- Third-party integrations

## Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Description |
| --- | --- |
| AI Steward (`sn_ai_governance.ai_steward`) | Oversees execution of AICT initiatives, ensures adherence to governance policies, and manages cross-functional coordination |
| AI Asset Owner (`sn_ai_governance.ai_asset_owner`) | Owns lifecycle management of AI assets and ensures value measurement alignment |

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

To apply Value Management, navigate to the Value tab in AICT to access the standardized dashboards measuring productivity gains, user engagement, system utilization, and ROI. Configure Value Templates to define the formulas and metrics applied to your AI Skills, AI Agents, Agentic Workflows, and third-party integrations. Confirm your ServiceNow release version (May, July, or December) against the Release Compatibility table to ensure the capability is available in your environment. Assign the `sn_ai_governance.ai_steward` and `sn_ai_governance.ai_asset_owner` roles so the AI Steward can coordinate AICT execution and the AI Asset Owner can align value measurement with asset lifecycle management.
