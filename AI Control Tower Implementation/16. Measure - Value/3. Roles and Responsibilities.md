## What Is This?

This subtopic identifies the key roles involved in Value Management within AI Control Tower (AICT) and the responsibilities each role carries. It maps each role to its corresponding ServiceNow governance role identifier. These roles ensure that AICT initiatives are executed under governance policies and that value measurement stays aligned with AI asset lifecycle management.

---

## Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Description |
| --- | --- |
| AI Steward (`sn_ai_governance.ai_steward`) | Oversees execution of AICT initiatives, ensures adherence to governance policies, and manages cross-functional coordination |
| AI Asset Owner (`sn_ai_governance.ai_asset_owner`) | Owns lifecycle management of AI assets and ensures value measurement alignment |

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing Value Management in AICT, assign the `sn_ai_governance.ai_steward` role to the individual responsible for governing AICT initiative execution and coordinating across functions, and the `sn_ai_governance.ai_asset_owner` role to those owning AI asset lifecycle and value measurement alignment. Granting these governance roles is a prerequisite for the respective parties to operate within the Value tab and maintain alignment between AI asset lifecycle activities and value measurement.
