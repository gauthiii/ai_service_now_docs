## What Is This?

This subtopic addresses how an organization extends the out-of-the-box (OOB) Impact Assessment in ServiceNow AICT beyond the pre-built sections and questions. Because the OOB content is primarily oriented toward fundamental rights and EU AI Act compliance, organizations typically need to add questions to existing sections and create entirely new sections to cover their full governance requirements. It fits into the Impact Assessment configuration stage of implementation.

## How to Use This in Implementation

When configuring the Impact Assessment, do not rely solely on the OOB 7 sections and 22 questions. As noted in the preceding "Important" callout, the OOB content is oriented toward fundamental rights and EU AI Act compliance and does not cover all governance requirements. Implementers should, based on their configuration design work, add questions to existing sections and create new sections for areas such as data quality, model transparency, tech stack governance, deployment geography, system failure impact, and vulnerable groups. The additions specific to your organization (shown elsewhere coded in green) should be driven by your governance requirements identified during configuration design.
