# Why can't we combine Impact Assessment and Risk Classification?

---

## What Is This?

This subtopic explains why the Impact Assessment and the Risk Classification cannot be merged into a single step in the AI Control Tower governance process. It clarifies that, despite their related purpose, the two are built on distinct underlying technologies. This is relevant during implementation when stakeholders question why the two activities remain separate.

---

## Why They Cannot Be Combined

It uses two different back-end functions. They are completely separate technologies. At a product functional level, they are separate items.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing AICT governance, do not attempt to consolidate the Impact Assessment and Risk Classification into one form or process. Because each relies on a different back-end function and represents a separate product item, they must be configured and run as independent steps. Set stakeholder expectations accordingly so the separation is understood rather than treated as redundancy.
