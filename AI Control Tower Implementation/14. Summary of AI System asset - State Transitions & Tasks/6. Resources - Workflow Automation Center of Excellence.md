## What Is This?

This subtopic is a curated list of reference resources from the Workflow Automation Center of Excellence. It points implementers to supporting material for building and customizing the AI Asset Onboarding workflow, organized into Playbook resources and Flows and Sub-Flows. It fits at the end of the AI System asset state transitions and tasks implementation guidance, providing links for further learning.

## How to Use This in Implementation

Implementers customizing the AI Asset Onboarding playbook and its underlying flows can consult these resources for guidance. Use the Playbook Resources (best practices, getting started, Playbooks Academy, and ServiceNow Trainings) when designing or extending playbook phases and tasks, and the Flows and Sub-Flows resources (Flow Designer best practices, Getting Started with Flows, and Recommendations) when building the automation behind those phases.
