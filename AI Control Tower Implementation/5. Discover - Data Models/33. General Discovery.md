## What Is This?

"General Discovery" is the first group of pre-implementation discovery questions used when planning an AI Asset Inventory in AI Control Tower. These questions help an implementer surface where AI exists across the organization before populating the inventory. It sits at the start of the Pre-Implementation Discovery Questions, ahead of Risk and Compliance Discovery.

---

## Pre-Implementation Discovery Questions: General Discovery

General Discovery questions to ask before implementation:

- Where is AI currently deployed?
- Are third-party AI tools in use?
- Are employees using unsanctioned GenAI tools?
- Which business units are developing AI solutions?
- How are AI assets onboarded and validated?

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Use this checklist of questions during the discovery phase, prior to defining and populating the AI Asset Inventory. Answering them helps identify deployed AI, shadow/unsanctioned GenAI usage, and the teams building AI solutions, so that scope, onboarding, and validation processes can be defined accurately. Follow this set with the Risk and Compliance Discovery questions to complete the pre-implementation assessment.
