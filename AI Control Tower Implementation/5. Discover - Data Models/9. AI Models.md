## What Is This?

AI Models is one of the Core AI Asset Types defined in the AI Control Tower data model. AI models are the computational engines powering AI systems. Within the Discover data model, tracking AI models provides the governance foundation for monitoring model behavior and risk.

---

## AI Models

AI models are the computational engines powering AI systems. They are trained on datasets to perform specific tasks such as classification, prediction, or generation.

### Common Training Approaches

- Supervised learning
- Unsupervised learning
- Reinforcement learning

### Governance Value

Tracking models enables monitoring of:

- Accuracy and performance
- Bias and fairness
- Model-specific risk exposure

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When mapping AI assets in AI Control Tower, register each AI model that powers an AI system. Capture its training approach (supervised, unsupervised, or reinforcement learning) so governance teams can monitor accuracy and performance, assess bias and fairness, and evaluate model-specific risk exposure. Since models are trained on datasets, model records connect to the AI Datasets asset type and to Model Lineage (which tracks model evolution, training datasets, and evaluation history) to give full provenance and accountability.
