## How to Use This in Implementation

When configuring the AI Asset Inventory, use industry- and use-case-specific scenarios (such as Financial Services loan decisioning) as templates to map how AI Systems decompose into Models, Datasets, MCP Servers, Prompts, Inputs, and Outputs. Begin with the scenario context (e.g., a retail bank automating home loan approvals) to identify which asset types apply, then represent each component faithfully in the inventory to ensure complete coverage across domains and AI types.
