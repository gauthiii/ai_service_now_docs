## What Is This?

The Example Intake Workflow illustrates the concrete steps an AI Asset Owner follows to manually submit AI assets into the AI Control Tower. It demonstrates how the manual intake capabilities (Record Producers and the AI Control Tower Workspace) come together in a single end-to-end flow, showing the role, navigation path, and the asset types that can be submitted.

---

## Example Intake Workflow

- **Role:** AI Asset Owner
- **Navigate:** Employee Center → Technology Services → AI Assets
- **Submit request for:**
  - AI System
  - AI Model
  - Dataset

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

To execute this workflow, an implementer ensures users have the AI Asset Owner role and access to the Employee Center. The asset owner navigates through Employee Center → Technology Services → AI Assets and submits a request for the relevant asset type (AI System, AI Model, or Dataset). This relies on the manual intake capabilities described in the surrounding section: Record Producers (which provide simplified data entry and automatically create backend records and relationships) and the AI Control Tower Workspace as the operational interface.
