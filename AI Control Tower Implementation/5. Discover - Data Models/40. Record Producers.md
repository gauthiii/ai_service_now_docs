## What Is This?

Record Producers are a ServiceNow capability that supports manual intake of AI assets into the AI Control Tower. They provide a simplified data-entry interface that lets AI Asset Owners submit AI assets without working directly with backend tables. Record Producers fit into the Discover phase as the mechanism for onboarding AI solutions when automated discovery is insufficient.

---

## Record Producers

Record Producers, listed under "ServiceNow Capabilities Supporting Manual Intake," provide:

- Provides simplified data entry for AI asset creation
- Accessible via Employee Center
- Automatically creates backend records and relationships

### Usage Context

- AI Asset Owners submit:
  - AI Systems
  - AI Models
  - Datasets

### Context: When Manual Intake Applies

The surrounding section indicates manual intake (and these supporting capabilities) is relevant when:

- Discovery mechanisms are incomplete
- Governance maturity requires controlled onboarding
- External or custom AI solutions exist

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

To enable manual AI asset intake, implementers expose Record Producers through the Employee Center so that AI Asset Owners can submit AI Systems, AI Models, and Datasets. Submitting through a Record Producer automatically creates the corresponding backend records and their relationships, so no direct backend table manipulation is needed. This approach is appropriate when automated discovery is incomplete, governance maturity requires controlled onboarding, or external/custom AI solutions need to be captured.
