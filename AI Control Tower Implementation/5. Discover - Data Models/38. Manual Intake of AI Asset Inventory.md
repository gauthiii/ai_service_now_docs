---

## What Is This?

Manual Intake of AI Asset Inventory is one of the methods for populating the AI Asset Inventory, complementing Auto Discovery and API-Based Integration (a hybrid approach is recommended). It enables AI Asset Owners to manually submit AI assets through guided ServiceNow interfaces. This approach is used where automated discovery is insufficient or where governance maturity requires controlled, human-driven onboarding of assets.

---

## When to Use Manual Intake

Manual intake is recommended when:

- Discovery mechanisms are incomplete
- Governance maturity requires controlled onboarding
- External or custom AI solutions exist

---

## ServiceNow Capabilities Supporting Manual Intake

### Record Producers

- Provides simplified data entry for AI asset creation
- Accessible via Employee Center
- Automatically creates backend records and relationships

**Usage Context:**

- AI Asset Owners submit:
  - AI Systems
  - AI Models
  - Datasets

### Workspace (AI Control Tower)

- Provides a centralized operational interface
- Built using Next Experience UI
- Enables efficient asset management and governance workflows

---

## Example Intake Workflow

- Role: AI Asset Owner
- Navigate: Employee Center → Technology Services → AI Assets
- Submit request for:
  - AI System
  - AI Model
  - Dataset

### Diagram/Screenshot: Navigation Path

The workflow describes a navigation breadcrumb path within ServiceNow:

```
Employee Center → Technology Services → AI Assets → Submit request
```

An AI Asset Owner starts in the Employee Center, moves into the Technology Services catalog, opens the AI Assets area, and submits a request for an AI System, AI Model, or Dataset.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

To implement manual intake, configure Record Producers so AI Asset Owners can enter AI Systems, AI Models, and Datasets via the Employee Center; these record producers automatically create backend records and the relationships between them. Provide the AI Control Tower Workspace (built on Next Experience UI) as the centralized operational interface for managing and governing those assets. Assign the AI Asset Owner role to users responsible for submission, and direct them through the Employee Center → Technology Services → AI Assets path. Use this method to fill gaps left by Auto Discovery and API-Based Integration, particularly for external or custom AI solutions and where controlled, governance-driven onboarding is required.
