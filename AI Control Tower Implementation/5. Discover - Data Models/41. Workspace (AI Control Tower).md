## What Is This?

The AI Control Tower Workspace is one of the ServiceNow capabilities that supports manual intake of AI assets. It provides a centralized operational interface where AI assets are managed and governance workflows are carried out. It fits into the Discover phase as the primary working surface for asset management and governance once assets have been onboarded.

---

## Workspace (AI Control Tower)

- Provides a centralized operational interface
- Built using Next Experience UI
- Enables efficient asset management and governance workflows

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Implementers use the AI Control Tower Workspace as the central operational interface for managing AI assets and running governance workflows. Because it is built on the Next Experience UI, it serves as the unified surface that complements other manual intake capabilities (such as Record Producers accessible via Employee Center) for onboarding and operating on AI Systems, AI Models, and Datasets.
