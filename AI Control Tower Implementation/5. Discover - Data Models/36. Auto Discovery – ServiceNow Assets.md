## What Is This?

Auto Discovery – ServiceNow Assets is one of the methods used to populate the AI Asset Inventory in AI Control Tower. It relies on a built-in scheduled job that automatically detects and syncs AI assets that already exist within the ServiceNow platform. It fits into the broader "Discovery and Population of AI Assets" workflow, where a hybrid approach combining multiple population methods is recommended.

---

## Auto Discovery – ServiceNow Assets

This method discovers and populates AI assets automatically from within ServiceNow using a scheduled job.

- Scheduled job: Sync Now Assist AI Assets
- Runs periodically (hourly)
- Discovers:
  - AI models
  - Datasets
  - Prompts
  - Agentic AI assets
- Covers both out-of-box and custom AI skills

### Context: Discovery and Population of AI Assets

AI Asset Inventory can be populated using multiple methods. A hybrid approach is recommended. Auto Discovery – ServiceNow Assets is one such method, alongside API-Based Integration and Manual Intake of AI Asset Inventory (covered in neighboring sections).

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

To use auto discovery, ensure the "Sync Now Assist AI Assets" scheduled job is active so it can run hourly and automatically populate the AI Asset Inventory. This method captures AI models, datasets, prompts, and agentic AI assets, including both out-of-box and custom AI skills, without manual effort. As part of a recommended hybrid approach, combine this automatic discovery with other population methods (such as API-based integration or manual intake) to achieve complete inventory coverage.
