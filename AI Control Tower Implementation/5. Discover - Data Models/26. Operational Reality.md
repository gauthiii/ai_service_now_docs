## What Is This?

"Operational Reality" describes the current, real-world state of how AI asset data exists in most organizations before a centralized AI Asset Inventory is adopted. It highlights the fragmentation and informality of AI asset tracking that motivates building a single source of truth within the AI Control Tower. This sets the practical context for why discovery and centralized data models are needed in implementation.

---

## The Operational Reality of AI Asset Data

AI asset data is often:

- Distributed across multiple tools and platforms
- Siloed across teams (IT, Data Science, Risk, Legal)
- Stored in informal repositories (spreadsheets, email, shared drives)

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing the AI Asset Inventory, an implementer should expect that AI asset information is not centralized at the outset. Because the data is distributed across tools/platforms, siloed across IT, Data Science, Risk, and Legal teams, and held in informal repositories like spreadsheets, email, and shared drives, the discovery effort must consolidate these scattered sources into the centralized inventory. Recognizing this fragmentation is the starting point for establishing a single source of truth for AI assets.
