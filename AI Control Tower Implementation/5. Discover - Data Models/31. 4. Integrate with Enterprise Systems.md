## What Is This?

This subtopic is the fourth step in determining the scope of an AI Asset Inventory within ServiceNow AI Control Tower. It addresses ensuring that the AI asset inventory aligns with the organization's existing enterprise systems. This integration step helps close governance gaps and avoid the incomplete or outdated asset records that result from data fragmentation.

---

## Integrate with Enterprise Systems

As part of the structured, governance-driven approach to defining AI asset inventory scope, this step requires ensuring alignment with enterprise systems.

Ensure alignment with:

- CRM, ITSM, HR systems
- Vendor and procurement systems
- Software asset management tools

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When defining the scope of your AI Asset Inventory, an implementer should ensure the inventory aligns with the organization's core enterprise systems so asset records remain complete and current. This step follows the prior scoping steps (Define AI Assets, Classify AI Use Cases as AI Systems, and Establish Inventory Scope) and depends on connecting to CRM, ITSM, and HR systems, vendor and procurement systems, and software asset management tools. Proper alignment helps mitigate the fragmentation risks noted earlier, namely incomplete or outdated asset records, manual and time-intensive discovery processes, and increased risk of governance gaps and audit failure.
