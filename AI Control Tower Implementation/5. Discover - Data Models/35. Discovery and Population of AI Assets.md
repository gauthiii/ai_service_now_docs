## What Is This?

This subtopic explains how the AI Asset Inventory is populated within the AI Control Tower. It describes the multiple methods available for discovering and recording AI assets, with a hybrid approach recommended. It fits into the Discover phase of implementation, where AI assets across the organization are identified and brought under governance.

---

## Overview

AI Asset Inventory can be populated using multiple methods. A hybrid approach is recommended.

---

## Auto Discovery – ServiceNow Assets

- Scheduled job: Sync Now Assist AI Assets
- Runs periodically (hourly)
- Discovers:
  - AI models
  - Datasets
  - Prompts
  - Agentic AI assets
- Covers both out-of-box and custom AI skills

---

## API-Based Integration

Used for large-scale environments requiring automation.

Capabilities:

- Create, update, and retrieve AI asset records
- Integrate with external systems
- Maintain real-time inventory accuracy

Requirements:

- Appropriate roles (asset, model_manager)
- Asset Classes plugin enabled

## How to Use This in Implementation

To populate the AI Asset Inventory, implementers can combine methods (a hybrid approach is recommended). For ServiceNow-native assets, enable and rely on the scheduled job "Sync Now Assist AI Assets," which runs hourly to discover AI models, datasets, prompts, and agentic AI assets across both out-of-box and custom AI skills. For large-scale or externally integrated environments, use API-based integration to create, update, and retrieve AI asset records, integrate with external systems, and maintain real-time accuracy — ensuring the appropriate roles (asset, model_manager) are granted and the Asset Classes plugin is enabled. Manual intake is reserved for cases that auto discovery and API integration do not cover.
