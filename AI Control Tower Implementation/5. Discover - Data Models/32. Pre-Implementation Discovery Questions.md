## What Is This?

This subtopic introduces the set of discovery questions to ask before implementing AI asset inventory and governance in AI Control Tower. These questions help establish where AI exists across the organization and inform how the inventory scope and data models are defined. It begins with a "General Discovery" category of questions.

---

## Pre-Implementation Discovery Questions

### General Discovery

- Where is AI currently deployed?

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Use these discovery questions as a starting checklist when planning an AICT implementation. The "General Discovery" question — "Where is AI currently deployed?" — helps an implementer establish a baseline understanding of existing AI footprint before defining asset inventory scope, classifying AI use cases as AI Systems, and integrating with enterprise systems. (Note: on this page only the "General Discovery" category and its first question are present; additional discovery questions continue beyond this page.)
