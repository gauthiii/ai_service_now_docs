---

## What Is This?

AI Prompts is one of the data models discovered and tracked within ServiceNow AI Control Tower. Prompts are the structured inputs or instructions provided to AI systems to generate outputs, and they are particularly critical in generative AI use cases. Tracking prompts as part of the discovery data model gives governance teams visibility into how AI systems are being instructed and used.

---

## Definition

Prompts are structured inputs or instructions provided to AI systems to generate outputs.

They are particularly critical in generative AI use cases.

---

## Governance Value

Governance Value - Tracking prompts enables:

- Understanding user interaction patterns
- Monitoring output quality and appropriateness

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

As part of the Discover - Data Models stage in AI Control Tower, implementers map AI Prompts alongside related data models (such as AI Models, AI Datasets, and MCP Servers). Capturing prompts provides governance visibility into user interaction patterns and the quality and appropriateness of generated outputs, which is especially important for generative AI use cases. This supports ongoing monitoring and oversight of how AI systems are instructed and behave.
