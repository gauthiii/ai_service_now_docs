## What Is This?

Risk and Compliance Discovery is part of the Discover phase of an AI Control Tower implementation. It frames the key discovery questions an organization should ask to understand the risk and regulatory posture of its AI usage. These questions guide implementers in establishing risk categorization, regulatory coverage, and clear ownership for AI systems before they are governed.

---

## Discovery Questions

The following questions should be asked during Risk and Compliance Discovery:

- What risks are associated with AI usage?
- Which regulatory frameworks must be supported?
- Are AI systems categorized by risk level?
- Is ownership clearly defined for each system?

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Implementers use these questions to scope the risk and compliance requirements of an AI program. By answering them, an organization identifies the risks tied to AI usage, determines which regulatory frameworks must be supported, confirms whether AI systems are categorized by risk level, and verifies that ownership is clearly assigned for each system. The answers inform how AI assets are subsequently discovered, categorized, and governed within AI Control Tower.
