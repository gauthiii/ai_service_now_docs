---

## What Is This?

AI Datasets are one of the core data model entities tracked in the Discover phase of AI Control Tower. A dataset is a collection of structured or unstructured data used to train, test, or evaluate AI models. Mapping datasets within AICT establishes the data foundation for governance, providing visibility into where AI data comes from and who is responsible for it.

---

## Definition and Composition

Datasets are collections of structured or unstructured data used to train, test, or evaluate AI models.

They may include:

- Tabular data
- Images, audio, or video
- Text corpora

---

## Governance Value

Mapping datasets ensures visibility into:

- Data provenance
- Ownership and stewardship
- Regulatory and privacy implications

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When building out the Discover data model in AI Control Tower, register the datasets associated with each AI model. For every dataset, capture its type (tabular, media, or text corpora) and link it to the models it trains, tests, or evaluates. Recording provenance and assigning ownership/stewardship supports downstream governance, particularly for assessing regulatory and privacy implications of the data feeding AI systems.
