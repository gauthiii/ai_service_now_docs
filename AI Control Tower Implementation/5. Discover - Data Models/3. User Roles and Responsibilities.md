---

## What Is This?

This subtopic defines the personas, their corresponding ServiceNow roles, and the responsibilities each holds when working with the AI Asset Inventory data model in AI Control Tower. It maps the stakeholders who own, govern, and oversee AI assets to specific role identifiers used within AICT and AI Risk and Compliance. It establishes accountability for maintaining asset records and enforcing governance across the AI lifecycle.

---

## Roles and Responsibilities Matrix

| Persona | Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|---|
| AI Product / Asset Owners | AI Asset Owner `[sn_ai_asset_mgmt.ai_asset_owner]` | Maintain accurate, up-to-date AI asset records across lifecycle stages |
| AI Stewards / AI CoE | AI Steward `[sn_ai_governance.ai_steward]` | Enforce governance standards and ensure alignment with AICT policies |
| AI Risk & Compliance Managers | AI Risk & Compliance Manager `[sn_grc_ai_gov.ai_risk_and_compliance_manager]` | Oversee risk identification, compliance mapping, and governance controls |

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When configuring the AI Asset Inventory in AI Control Tower, assign the appropriate role to each persona to enforce accountability:

- Grant `sn_ai_asset_mgmt.ai_asset_owner` to AI Product / Asset Owners so they can maintain asset records throughout the lifecycle.
- Grant `sn_ai_governance.ai_steward` to AI Stewards / AI CoE members responsible for enforcing governance standards aligned with AICT policies.
- Grant `sn_grc_ai_gov.ai_risk_and_compliance_manager` to AI Risk & Compliance Managers responsible for risk identification, compliance mapping, and governance controls.

Aligning these roles to the corresponding personas ensures clear ownership of the AI asset data model and supports traceability, risk management, and lifecycle governance across AI initiatives.
