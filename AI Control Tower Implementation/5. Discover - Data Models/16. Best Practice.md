## What Is This?

This subtopic defines the recommended best practice for mapping AI data model interdependencies within AI Control Tower (AICT). It provides a structured, repeatable procedure for documenting each AI capability so that AI systems, models, and datasets are linked together. It fits into the Discover phase, ensuring end-to-end traceability and governance coverage.

---

## Best Practice for Mapping AI Capabilities

For each AI capability:

1. Inventory the AI system
2. Identify associated models
3. Document datasets used for training and evaluation

This structured mapping ensures end-to-end traceability and governance coverage.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When onboarding or discovering an AI capability in AICT, apply this three-step procedure to every capability: first inventory the AI system, then identify the models associated with it, and finally document the datasets used for training and evaluation. Following this structured mapping consistently ensures the relationships between AI systems, models, and datasets are explicitly captured, supporting traceability and complete governance coverage.
