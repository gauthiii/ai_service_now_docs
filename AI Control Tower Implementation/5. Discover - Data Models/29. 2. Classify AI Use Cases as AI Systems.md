# 2. Classify AI Use Cases as AI Systems

> ServiceNow AI Control Tower (AICT) — Implementation Guide
> Section: Discover - Data Models → 2. Classify AI Use Cases as AI Systems
> Source: AI-Control-Tower.pdf, p.49

---

## What Is This?

This is the second step in the structured, governance-driven approach for determining the scope of an AI asset inventory. It involves mapping each AI use case to an AI System, so that discovered AI activity is organized into well-defined, governable system records. This step fits between defining AI assets and establishing the inventory scope during the Discover phase.

---

## Classify AI Use Cases as AI Systems

Each use case should be mapped to an AI System based on:

- Business purpose
- Criticality and impact
- Regulatory exposure

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing the AI asset inventory, take the AI use cases that have been identified and map each one to an AI System record. Use the three criteria above—business purpose, criticality and impact, and regulatory exposure—as the basis for this classification. This step depends on first having defined clear AI asset criteria (Step 1) and feeds into establishing the overall inventory scope (Step 3).
