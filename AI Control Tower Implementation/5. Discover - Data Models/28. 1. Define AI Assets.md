---

## What Is This?

"Define AI Assets" is the first step in determining the scope of an AI asset inventory, which requires a structured, governance-driven approach. It establishes the criteria used to decide what qualifies as an AI asset to be tracked. This sets the foundation for classifying use cases as AI Systems, establishing inventory scope, and integrating with enterprise systems.

---

## Define AI Assets

Establish clear criteria based on:

- AI technology type (ML, NLP, GenAI, Agentic AI)
- Organizational standards
- Regulatory requirements

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

As part of determining the scope of the AI asset inventory, implementers first define what counts as an AI asset by setting clear criteria. Use the AI technology type (ML, NLP, GenAI, Agentic AI), organizational standards, and regulatory requirements to bound which assets must be tracked. This definition then feeds the subsequent steps: classifying AI use cases as AI Systems, establishing inventory scope, and integrating with enterprise systems.
