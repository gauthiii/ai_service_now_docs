## What Is This?

"Additional Plugins" describes the optional plugins that may be present in a ServiceNow instance beyond the required AI Control Tower Core activation plugin. These plugins extend AI Control Tower with capabilities that depend on your specific implementation scope. They fit into the layered plugin architecture as extensions on top of the foundational governance infrastructure.

---

## Additional Plugins

Depending on your implementation scope, additional plugins may be present in your ServiceNow instance. These extend AI Control Tower with capabilities for:

- Security and privacy monitoring
- AI asset discovery
- Now Assist integration
- Performance dashboards (value, health, and engagement)
- The AI Lens reporting module

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Implementers should review their instance to determine which of these additional plugins are present, since availability depends on the implementation scope. These plugins build on the required AI Control Tower Core plugin (com.sn_aict) and extend the platform with security/privacy monitoring, AI asset discovery, Now Assist integration, performance dashboards, and the AI Lens reporting module. As with all plugins, confirm version compatibility with your ServiceNow release before activating.
