## What Is This?

The Operations tab is one of the views within the AI Risk and Compliance workspace, providing the most detailed operational view in the Risk and Compliance workspace. This subtopic introduces the set of widgets available on that tab for configuration. It fits into implementation as the operational, day-to-day monitoring surface that complements the summary-level information in the main AICT workspace.

### Context: Operations Within the Risk and Compliance Workspace

The AI Risk and Compliance workspace is organized into three primary tabs. As surfaced on this page, the Operations tab provides:

- Overview of AI systems by state and department.
- Surfaces risk assessments, AI assessments, control assurance, issues by priority, policy exceptions by risk rating, and AI cases by priority.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Implementers configure the widgets on the Operations tab to give the AI risk and compliance manager a detailed operational view of the AI asset inventory. As a prerequisite, ensure both the AI Risk and Compliance workspace and the main AICT workspace are configured before conducting risk reviews, since AI risk and compliance details are also accessible under the Risk and Compliance tab in the AICT workspace.
