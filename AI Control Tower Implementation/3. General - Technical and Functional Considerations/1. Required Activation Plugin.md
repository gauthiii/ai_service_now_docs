## What Is This?

The Required Activation Plugin identifies the foundational plugin that must be activated to enable the AI Control Tower ecosystem. The AI Control Tower ecosystem is built on a layered plugin architecture — each plugin extends the platform's capabilities while depending on the ones activated before it. This subtopic specifies the core plugin that anchors that activation sequence, providing the base for management, governance, security, risk, and value measurement of AI investments.

---

## Activation Context

The activation sequence reflects both technical dependencies and a logical progression from foundational governance infrastructure to advanced risk, compliance, and value management.

**Caution:** Activating plugins out of order can result in missing data relationships or incomplete workspace functionality. Plugins 1 through 7 represent the required activation sequence. Plugin 6 (AI Risk and Compliance Content) is optional and may be skipped if your organization is not yet ready to align to a regulatory framework such as the EU AI Act or NIST AI RMF.

## Required Activation Plugin

| Plugin Name | Scope ID | Purpose |
|-------------|----------|---------|
| AI Control Tower Core | com.sn_aict | Enables management, optimization, governance, security, and value measurement of AI investments with embedded risk and compliance lifecycle management. |

**Important:** Confirm version compatibility with your ServiceNow release before activating. Always validate against the current store listing for the version available on your instance.

---

## Additional Plugins

Depending on your implementation scope, additional plugins may be present in your ServiceNow instance. These extend AI Control Tower with capabilities for security and privacy monitoring, AI asset discovery, Now Assist integration, performance dashboards (value, health, and engagement), and the AI Lens reporting module.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

To stand up AI Control Tower, activate the **AI Control Tower Core** plugin (scope `com.sn_aict`) as the foundational component before any dependent plugins. Follow the required activation sequence (plugins 1 through 7) in order to avoid missing data relationships or incomplete workspace functionality. Before activating, confirm version compatibility with your ServiceNow release and validate against the current store listing on your instance. Plugin 6 (AI Risk and Compliance Content) may be skipped if regulatory framework alignment (EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF) is not yet required. Additional plugins may also be present depending on implementation scope.
