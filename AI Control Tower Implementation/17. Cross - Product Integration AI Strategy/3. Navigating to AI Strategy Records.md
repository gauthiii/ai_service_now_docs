## What Is This?

This subtopic explains how to navigate to the AI strategy records within the AI Control Tower (AICT) application. From the AICT application, you navigate to a list view that surfaces the record types used to build out an AI strategy. It is the entry point for working with strategies, goals, and execution plans for AI initiatives.

---

## Navigating to AI Strategy Records

From the AICT application, navigate to list. This section surfaces three record types used to build an AI strategy:

- **AI Strategies** — top-level strategic priorities for AI
- **Goals** — measurable objectives aligned to each strategy
- **Strategic Plans** — execution roadmaps tied to goals

## How to Use This in Implementation

To begin building an AI strategy, open the AICT application and navigate to the list of AI strategies and goals. Use the "AI strategies and goals" navigation group to access the three related record types — AI Strategies (strategic priorities), Goals, and Strategic Plans — which together define an organization's AI strategy. Implementers start from the Strategic priorities list (where strategy records such as STR0001001 appear) and can create new records with the New button, then drill into a strategy to define associated goals and plans.
