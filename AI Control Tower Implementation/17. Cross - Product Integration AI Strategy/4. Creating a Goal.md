## What Is This?

Creating a Goal is the step in the AICT AI Strategy process where a measurable objective is defined and aligned to a Strategic Priority (AI Strategy). A goal captures qualitative and quantitative targets, can optionally be tied to an existing AI system, and supports targets that are tracked over time. It fits between top-level AI Strategies and execution-level Strategic Plans as one of the three record types used to build an AI strategy.

---

## Creating a Goal from a Strategic Priority

Users can open a Strategic Priority and select **New Goal** to define a goal aligned with their AI strategy.

Within the goal record:

- (Optional) The **Category** field may be set to **Artificial Intelligence**.
- Both qualitative and quantitative targets can be defined to measure success.

Optionally, the goal can be linked to an existing AI system:

- Set **Assigned Entity** to **Product Model**.
- Select the relevant AI system.

## How to Use This in Implementation

To create a goal, open the relevant Strategic Priority in AICT and select **New Goal**. Optionally set the goal's **Category** to Artificial Intelligence, then define qualitative and quantitative targets to measure success. To tie the goal to an existing AI system, set the **Assigned entity type** to **Product Model** and select the relevant AI system (e.g., a ServiceNow Case summarization product model). Add targets via the related list under the goal record. After goals and targets are defined, AI Stewards track progress from the Dashboard's AI Strategy tab, which visualizes goal status and targets by goal and by strategic priority.
