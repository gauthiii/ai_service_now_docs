## What Is This?

Defining AI Strategies is the subtopic that introduces how AI strategies, goals, and targets are established within AI Control Tower (AICT). These artifacts serve as the north star for all AI investment decisions across the organization. This section covers the setup and configuration of those strategies.

## Overview

AI strategies, goals, and targets are managed within AICT and serve as the north star for all AI investment decisions. This section covers setup and configuration.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Use AICT as the central place to define AI strategies and align goals and targets before making AI investment decisions. Per the broader integration context on this page, the Goals Framework (included for all customers) is used to create and align strategic goals with qualitative/quantitative targets, and the AICT License unlocks AI strategy management, goals, targets, and the AI Steward dashboard. Without Strategic Planning (SPM Pro), organizations can still define AI strategies and monitor goals in AICT, but cannot manage intake or execution within the same platform.
