## What Is This?

This subtopic identifies the plugins and licenses required to integrate AI Control Tower (AICT) with Strategic Portfolio Management (SPM) so that AI strategy, intake, and execution can be managed on a single platform. It maps each plugin to the license it needs and the capability it unlocks. This is a prerequisite step for implementing the cross-product AI strategy integration, ensuring the right entitlements are in place before defining AI strategies, goals, and targets.

---

## Required Plugins and Licensing

This integration requires the following plugins. Capabilities expanded as additional licenses are activated.

| Plugin | License Required | Capability Unlocked |
|--------|-----------------|---------------------|
| AI Control Tower | AICT License | AI strategy management, goals, targets, AI Steward dashboard |
| Goals Framework | Included — all customers | Create and align strategic goals with qualitative/quantitative targets |
| Strategic Planning | SPM Pro License | AI demand intake, project execution, portfolio planning, capacity and financial analysis |

### Note

Without Strategic Planning (SPM Pro), organizations can define AI strategies and monitor goals in AICT, but cannot manage intake or execution within the same platform.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

To enable the full cross-product AI strategy integration, ensure all three plugins are activated with their corresponding licenses. The AICT License unlocks AI strategy management, goals, targets, and the AI Steward dashboard. The Goals Framework is included for all customers and supports creating and aligning strategic goals with qualitative/quantitative targets. The Strategic Planning plugin requires the SPM Pro License and unlocks AI demand intake, project execution, portfolio planning, and capacity/financial analysis. As a key dependency and gotcha: if SPM Pro is not licensed, you can still define AI strategies and monitor goals in AICT, but you will not be able to manage intake or execution within the same platform.
