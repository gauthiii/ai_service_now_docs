---

## What Is This?

"Connection Outcome" describes what AI Control Tower can do once the AI Service Graph Connector for Google Cloud Platform (GCP) Vertex AI connection has been successfully created. It marks the result of completing the connector setup in the Discover/Discovery flow, enabling discovery of Vertex AI assets and execution data into AI Control Tower.

---

## Connection Outcome

After the GCP Vertex AI connection is created, AI Control Tower can discover Vertex AI assets and related execution data for inventory, lifecycle visibility, and value dashboard reporting.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

This outcome applies after an implementer has completed the prerequisites and created the AI Service Graph Connector for GCP Vertex AI connection. Once established, the connection enables AI Control Tower to ingest Vertex AI assets (AI systems, models, prompts, tools, system subcomponents) and execution data, which feed inventory, lifecycle visibility, and the AI Control Tower value dashboard. No additional action is described for this step beyond confirming the connection has been created.
