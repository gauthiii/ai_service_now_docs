## What Is This?

The Connection Outcome describes what happens after a LangGraph connection is created using the AI Service Graph Connector for LangGraph. It explains the result of establishing the connection in terms of what AI Control Tower can discover and how that discovered data is mapped into ServiceNow tables. This fits into the discovery phase of AI Control Tower implementation.

---

## Connection Outcome

After the LangGraph connection is created, AI Control Tower can discover LangGraph agents and usage data, then map those records into AI digital asset, usage, and CMDB tables.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

After completing the prerequisites and creating the LangGraph connection (via the AI Service Graph Connector for LangGraph), implementers can expect AI Control Tower to discover LangGraph agents and usage data. The discovered records are then mapped into AI digital asset tables, usage tables, and CMDB tables. The specifics of how this data is mapped are detailed in the LangGraph Data Mapping and LangGraph Target Tables sections that follow.
