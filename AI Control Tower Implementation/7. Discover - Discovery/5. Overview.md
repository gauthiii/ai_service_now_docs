## What Is This?

This is the overview of AI Service Graph Connectors for AI Control Tower. These connectors enable AI Control Tower to discover AI assets and related usage data from external AI platforms. They are the discovery mechanism that populates the AI inventory with assets sourced from outside ServiceNow.

---

## AI Service Graph Connectors for AI Control Tower

AI Service Graph Connectors enable AI Control Tower to discover AI assets and related usage data from external AI platforms. These connectors help populate the AI inventory with AI systems, agents, models, prompts, tools, subcomponents, and execution or usage data.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing discovery for AI Control Tower, use AI Service Graph Connectors to pull AI assets and their usage data from external AI platforms into the AI inventory. Identify which platform(s) host your AI systems, agents, models, prompts, tools, subcomponents, and execution/usage data, then configure the relevant connector so AICT can populate the inventory automatically rather than relying solely on assets created within ServiceNow.
