## What Is This?

This subtopic describes the purpose of the AI Service Graph Connector for Google Cloud Platform (GCP) Vertex AI. The connector discovers AI assets and usage data from Google Cloud Vertex AI and brings them into ServiceNow AI Control Tower. It is part of the discovery capability that populates inventory and powers value reporting in AICT.

---

## Purpose of the AI Service Graph Connector for GCP Vertex AI

The AI Service Graph Connector for GCP Vertex AI is used to discover AI assets and usage data from Google Cloud Vertex AI into ServiceNow AI Control Tower. Supported assets include:

- AI systems
- Models
- Prompts
- Tools
- System subcomponents
- Execution data

Usage information imported through the connector is consumed by the AI Control Tower value dashboard.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Use this connector when an organization runs AI workloads on Google Cloud Vertex AI and needs that inventory and usage visible inside AI Control Tower. Once configured, discovered Vertex AI assets (systems, models, prompts, tools, subcomponents) and execution data flow into AICT, where the usage data feeds the value dashboard for lifecycle visibility and value reporting. Prerequisites and required roles for setting up the connection are covered in the related subtopics within this section.
