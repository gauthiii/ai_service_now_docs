## What Is This?

This subtopic describes the purpose of the AI Service Graph Connector for LangGraph. The connector is used to discover AI assets and usage data from LangGraph and LangSmith into ServiceNow AI Control Tower. It fits into the Discovery phase of implementation, where external AI tooling is connected so its assets and usage can be tracked in AICT.

---

## Purpose

The AI Service Graph Connector for LangGraph is used to discover AI assets and usage data from LangGraph and LangSmith into ServiceNow AI Control Tower. Supported assets include AI systems, models, prompts, tools, agents, and usage records.

Usage information imported through this connector is consumed by the AI Control Tower value dashboard.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

During the Discovery phase, an implementer configures the AI Service Graph Connector for LangGraph to bring LangGraph and LangSmith AI assets (AI systems, models, prompts, tools, agents) and usage records into AI Control Tower. Once imported, the usage data feeds the AI Control Tower value dashboard, enabling visibility into LangGraph-based AI activity. This subtopic establishes the connector's intent; subsequent subtopics on the same connector cover required roles, prerequisites, connection outcome, and data mapping.
