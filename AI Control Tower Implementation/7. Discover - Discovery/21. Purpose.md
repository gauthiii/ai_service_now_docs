## What Is This?

This subtopic describes the purpose of the AI Service Graph Connector for Microsoft within ServiceNow AI Control Tower discovery. It explains what the connector discovers and which Microsoft AI sources it draws from. It fits into the discovery phase of an AICT implementation, where AI assets and usage data are brought into ServiceNow.

---

## Purpose of the AI Service Graph Connector for Microsoft

The AI Service Graph Connector for Microsoft is used to discover AI assets and usage data from Azure Foundry and Microsoft Copilot into ServiceNow AI Control Tower. Supported assets may include AI systems, agents, models, prompts, tools, subcomponents, and execution data.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing discovery from Microsoft sources, configure the AI Service Graph Connector for Microsoft to pull AI assets and usage data from Azure Foundry and Microsoft Copilot into AI Control Tower. Use it where you need ServiceNow to inventory Microsoft-originated AI systems, agents, models, prompts, tools, subcomponents, and execution data. The Required Roles and Azure Foundry Connection setup (covered in adjacent subtopics) are prerequisites for using this connector.
