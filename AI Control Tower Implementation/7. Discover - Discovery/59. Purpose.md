## What Is This?

This subtopic describes the purpose of the AI Service Graph Connector for n8n within AI Control Tower discovery. The connector is used to discover AI workflow assets and execution data from n8n into ServiceNow AI Control Tower. It establishes how n8n-based AI workflow information is brought into ServiceNow for visibility and governance.

---

## Purpose of the AI Service Graph Connector for n8n

The AI Service Graph Connector for n8n is used to discover AI workflow assets and execution data from n8n into ServiceNow AI Control Tower. Supported assets include:

- AI systems
- Models
- Tools
- Prompts
- System subcomponents
- Execution data

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Implementers use the AI Service Graph Connector for n8n when they need AI Control Tower to discover and import AI workflow assets and execution data originating from n8n. Before relying on the connector, ensure the n8n connection is established so that the supported asset types (AI systems, models, tools, prompts, system subcomponents, and execution data) can be discovered into ServiceNow.
