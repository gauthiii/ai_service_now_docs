## What Is This?

This subtopic defines the purpose of the AI Service Graph Connector for Amazon within ServiceNow AI Control Tower discovery. It explains why the connector exists and what categories of AWS data it brings into the platform. It is the entry point for understanding how Amazon AI assets and usage are discovered and surfaced in AI Control Tower.

---

## Purpose of the AI Service Graph Connector for Amazon

The AI Service Graph Connector for Amazon is used to discover AI assets and usage data from AWS services into ServiceNow AI Control Tower. Supported assets may include AI systems, agents, models, tools, prompts, and usage records.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

An implementer uses this connector to populate AI Control Tower with Amazon-sourced AI assets and usage data, enabling visibility into AI activity. Establishing the connector is the prerequisite step before discovered AWS assets (systems, agents, models, tools, prompts, and usage records) can appear in AI Control Tower; subsequent setup involves the required roles, AWS prerequisites, and IAM permissions covered in the following subtopics.
