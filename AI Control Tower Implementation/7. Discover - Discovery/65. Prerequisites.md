---

## What Is This?

This subtopic lists the prerequisites that must be confirmed before creating a Salesforce connection using the AI Service Graph Connector for Salesforce. The connector discovers AI assets and usage data from Salesforce into ServiceNow AI Control Tower. Confirming these details up front ensures the connection can be established and the relevant Salesforce AI data can be discovered.

---

## Prerequisites

Before creating a Salesforce connection, confirm that the organization has the required Salesforce connection details and OAuth credentials, including:

- Salesforce connection URL.
- OAuth Client ID.
- OAuth token URL.
- Required access to Salesforce AI agent, Einstein model, prompt, tool, and usage data.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Before configuring the AI Service Graph Connector for Salesforce, an implementer should gather and verify all four prerequisites: the Salesforce connection URL, the OAuth Client ID, the OAuth token URL, and the required access to Salesforce AI agent, Einstein model, prompt, tool, and usage data. Without these connection details and OAuth credentials in place, the Salesforce connection cannot be created and AI Control Tower will be unable to discover the associated Salesforce AI assets and usage information.
