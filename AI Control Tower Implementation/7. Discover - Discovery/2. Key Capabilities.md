## What Is This?

The Key Capabilities of Enterprise AI Discovery describe the core functions that AI Control Tower provides to find, aggregate, and catalog AI assets across an enterprise. These capabilities enable AI stewards to build a unified registry of AI systems, agents, models, prompts, and tools sourced from hyperscalers, AI apps, and Agentic AI frameworks. They form the foundation of effective AI governance, security, compliance, and operational visibility.

---

## Key Capabilities

- **Unified Registry:** Aggregate AI assets from hyperscalars, AI apps, and Agentic AI frameworks such as AWS Bedrock, Azure AI Foundry, Copilot Studio, Google Cloud Platform (GCP) Vertex AI, ServiceNow agents, Salesforce, Databricks, n8n and more.

> **Note**
>
> Identify Assets and CIs (Configuration Item) using their unique source IDs, such as ARN (Amazon Resource Names) for AWS and Product Models are identified by the associated asset.

- **Automatic Discovery:** Seamlessly discover and incorporate agents, models, prompts, and tools into the AI Control Tower's AI asset inventory.

- **Comprehensive Meta data:** Capture detailed information for each asset, including relationships, versions, and operational status.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing Enterprise AI Discovery, configure Service Graph Connectors (SGC) and integrations to the relevant providers (AWS Bedrock, Azure AI Foundry, Copilot Studio, GCP Vertex AI, ServiceNow agents, Salesforce, Databricks, n8n, and others) so their AI assets are aggregated into a single unified registry. Ensure assets and Configuration Items (CIs) are matched on their unique source IDs—such as ARNs for AWS—so duplicates are avoided and Product Models correctly tie back to their associated asset. Rely on automatic discovery to keep the AI asset inventory current with agents, models, prompts, and tools, and capture comprehensive metadata (relationships, versions, operational status) to support downstream governance, security, and compliance workflows.
