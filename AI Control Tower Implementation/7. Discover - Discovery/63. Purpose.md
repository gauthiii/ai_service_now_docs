## What Is This?

This subtopic describes the purpose of the AI Service Graph Connector for Salesforce. The connector is the discovery mechanism that brings AI assets and usage data from Salesforce into ServiceNow AI Control Tower. It enables AI Control Tower to gain visibility into Salesforce-side AI systems as part of the broader discovery capability.

---

## Purpose of the AI Service Graph Connector for Salesforce

The AI Service Graph Connector for Salesforce is used to discover AI assets and usage data from Salesforce into ServiceNow AI Control Tower. Supported assets include AI systems, models, prompts, tools, and AI agent usage data.

Usage information imported through this connector is consumed by the AI Control Tower value dashboard.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Implementers use this connector to populate AI Control Tower with Salesforce-sourced AI assets (AI systems, models, prompts, tools) and AI agent usage data. Once imported, the usage information feeds the AI Control Tower value dashboard, giving teams visibility into Salesforce AI usage alongside other discovered AI systems. This is the starting point of the Salesforce discovery flow; subsequent steps (required roles, prerequisites, connection setup) build on the goals defined here.
