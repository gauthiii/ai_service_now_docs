# Connection Outcome

---

## What Is This?

Connection Outcome describes the discovery results that AI Control Tower obtains once a Salesforce connection is successfully created through the AI Service Graph Connector for Salesforce. It defines what AI assets and usage data become discoverable after the connection is established. This subtopic fits into the Discover phase of AICT implementation, specifically the Salesforce connector workflow.

---

## Connection Outcome

After the Salesforce connection is created, AI Control Tower can discover Salesforce AI agents, Einstein models, tools, prompts, and usage information.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Once an implementer has confirmed the prerequisites and created the Salesforce connection (using the required roles `sn_ai_disc.discovery_admin` and `sn_cmdb_int_util.sgc_admin`, plus the Salesforce connection URL, OAuth Client ID, OAuth token URL, and required access to Salesforce AI agent, Einstein model, prompt, tool, and usage data), AI Control Tower automatically discovers the Salesforce AI agents, Einstein models, tools, prompts, and usage information. This discovered usage information is consumed by the AI Control Tower value dashboard, so a successful connection outcome is the prerequisite for populating Salesforce AI asset and usage insights in AICT.
