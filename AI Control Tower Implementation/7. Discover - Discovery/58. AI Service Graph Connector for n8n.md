# AI Service Graph Connector for n8n

> ServiceNow AI Control Tower (AICT) — Implementation Guide
> Section: Discover - Discovery → AI Service Graph Connector for n8n
> Source: AI-Control-Tower.pdf, p.77

---

## What Is This?

The AI Service Graph Connector for n8n discovers AI workflow assets and execution data from n8n into ServiceNow AI Control Tower. It enables AICT to import n8n AI workflow records into AI digital asset, CMDB, entity, and usage tables. It fits into the Discover phase, extending AICT's visibility to AI assets managed within n8n.

---

## Purpose

The AI Service Graph Connector for n8n is used to discover AI workflow assets and execution data from n8n into ServiceNow AI Control Tower. Supported assets include AI systems, models, tools, prompts, system subcomponents, and execution data.

---

## Connection Outcome

After the n8n connection is established, AI Control Tower can import n8n AI workflow records into AI digital asset, CMDB, entity, and usage tables.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

To use the n8n connector, establish the n8n connection so AI Control Tower can begin importing n8n AI workflow records. Once connected, AICT discovers AI systems, models, tools, prompts, system subcomponents, and execution data from n8n. Each data source flows from its staging table (in the `sn_n8n_integ_*` namespace) into the corresponding target tables across AI digital asset, CMDB product model, entity (`sn_ent_*`), and usage tables. Implementers should ensure the n8n integration plugin/scoped tables are present and that the target CMDB, digital asset, and usage tables are available so discovered records map correctly.
