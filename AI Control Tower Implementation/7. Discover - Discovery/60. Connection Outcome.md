## What Is This?

This subtopic describes what AI Control Tower can do once the n8n connection (via the AI Service Graph Connector for n8n) has been established. It defines the result of a successful connection: the ability to import n8n AI workflow records into ServiceNow tables. It fits within the discovery phase, where AI workflow assets and execution data from n8n are brought into AI Control Tower.

---

## Connection Outcome

After the n8n connection is established, AI Control Tower can import n8n AI workflow records into AI digital asset, CMDB, entity, and usage tables.

### Context: AI Service Graph Connector for n8n

The AI Service Graph Connector for n8n is used to discover AI workflow assets and execution data from n8n into ServiceNow AI Control Tower. Supported assets include AI systems, models, tools, prompts, system subcomponents, and execution data.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Establish the n8n connection using the AI Service Graph Connector for n8n before expecting any data to appear. Once the connection is in place, AI Control Tower automatically imports n8n AI workflow records into the AI digital asset, CMDB, entity, and usage tables. Verify successful import by confirming that the expected n8n assets (AI systems, models, tools, prompts, system subcomponents, and execution data) are present in these target tables.
