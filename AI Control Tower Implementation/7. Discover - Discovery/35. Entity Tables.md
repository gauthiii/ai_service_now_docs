# Entity Tables

> ServiceNow AI Control Tower (AICT) — Implementation Guide
> Section: Discover - Discovery → Entity Tables
> Source: AI-Control-Tower.pdf, p.73

---

## What Is This?

The Entity Tables are the data tables that AI Control Tower's discovery process uses to store the AI entities it finds. They hold records of discovered AI tools and the relationships between AI system subcomponents. These tables underpin the persistence layer for discovered AI assets during the Discover stage of implementation.

---

## Entity Tables

| Table | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| `sn_ent_ai_tool` | Stores AI tools |
| `sn_ent_ai_system_subcomponent_m2m` | Stores AI system subcomponent relationships |

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing AI discovery, these tables are where discovered AI entities are persisted. Use `sn_ent_ai_tool` to locate stored AI tool records and `sn_ent_ai_system_subcomponent_m2m` to inspect the many-to-many relationships between AI system subcomponents. These entity tables work alongside the discovery usage table (which stores AI usage and execution data) to provide a complete view of discovered AI assets.
