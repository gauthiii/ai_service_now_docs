# Digital Asset Tables

> Section: Discover - Discovery → Digital Asset Tables

---

## What Is This?

Digital Asset Tables are a set of shared Microsoft target tables that store the AI digital assets discovered through AI Control Tower. They are part of the "Shared Microsoft Target Tables" used by both Azure Foundry and Copilot. These tables persist discovered AI systems, prompts, and models as digital assets within AI Control Tower.

---

## Digital Asset Tables

Both Azure Foundry and Copilot use the following target tables.

| Table | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| `alm_ai_system_digital_asset` | Stores AI system digital assets |
| `alm_ai_prompt_digital_asset` | Stores AI prompt digital assets |
| `alm_ai_model_digital_asset` | Stores AI model digital assets |

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

These tables are the destination ("Target Table") for digital assets surfaced during Microsoft Azure Foundry and Copilot discovery. Implementers do not populate them manually; they are filled by the discovery and execution import processes that map staging tables to these shared Microsoft target tables. When validating discovery results, confirm that discovered AI systems, prompts, and models appear in `alm_ai_system_digital_asset`, `alm_ai_prompt_digital_asset`, and `alm_ai_model_digital_asset` respectively.
