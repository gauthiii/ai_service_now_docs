---

## What Is This?

This subtopic lists the roles required to configure and operate the AI Service Graph Connector for Microsoft, which is used to discover AI assets and usage data from Azure Foundry and Microsoft Copilot into ServiceNow AI Control Tower. These roles grant the necessary permissions for discovery administration and Service Graph Connector administration during implementation.

---

## Required Roles

The AI Service Graph Connector for Microsoft requires the following roles:

- `sn_ai_disc.discovery_admin`
- `sn_cmdb_int_util.sgc_admin`

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When setting up the AI Service Graph Connector for Microsoft, assign the `sn_ai_disc.discovery_admin` and `sn_cmdb_int_util.sgc_admin` roles to the implementing user. These roles are prerequisites for configuring the connector to discover AI systems, agents, models, prompts, tools, subcomponents, and execution data from Azure Foundry and Microsoft Copilot into AI Control Tower.
