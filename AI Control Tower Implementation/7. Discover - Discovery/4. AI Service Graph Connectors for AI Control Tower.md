## What Is This?

AI Service Graph Connectors enable AI Control Tower to discover AI assets and related usage data from external AI platforms. They are the integration mechanism that feeds the AI inventory in AI Control Tower with data sourced from outside ServiceNow. This subtopic sits within the Discover/Discovery area of the implementation, where AICT aggregates AI assets across platforms.

---

## Overview

AI Service Graph Connectors enable AI Control Tower to discover AI assets and related usage data from external AI platforms. These connectors help populate the AI inventory with AI systems, agents, models, prompts, tools, subcomponents, and execution or usage data.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Use AI Service Graph Connectors as the entry point for bringing AI assets from external AI platforms into AI Control Tower's inventory. When configured, the connectors discover and populate AI systems, agents, models, prompts, tools, subcomponents, and execution/usage data, giving implementers a consolidated view of AI assets that originate outside ServiceNow.
