## What Is This?

Automated AI Asset Synchronization is the capability by which AI Control Tower (AICT) keeps its AI inventory accurate by continuously syncing AI assets directly from a live Now Assist configuration. It eliminates error-prone, quickly-stale manual asset tracking by auto-discovering and registering AI Models, Datasets, Prompts, and Agentic AI assets used by Now Assist skills. This synchronization is what ensures the AICT inventory remains current and aligned with deployed AI functionality.

---

## Overview

When ServiceNow Now Assist is implemented within your organization, AI Control Tower (AICT) can automatically discover and register AI assets used by Now Assist skills.

This capability eliminates the need for manual asset entry and ensures that the AI inventory remains accurate, current, and aligned with deployed AI functionality.

> **Important:** Manual AI asset tracking is error-prone and quickly becomes stale. AICT's auto-discovery eliminates that risk by continuously syncing AI Models, Datasets, Prompts, and Agentic AI assets directly from your live Now Assist configuration.

---

## Automated AI Asset Synchronization

AICT maintains inventory accuracy through a scheduled job titled "Sync Now Assist AI Assets."

This job performs continuous synchronization by identifying and mapping AI assets generated or utilized by Now Assist skills into the AICT inventory.

### High-Level Process Flow

At a high level, the synchronization process:

1. Iterates through Now Assist configuration tables
2. Identifies AI assets and associated metadata
3. Determines whether corresponding AICT digital assets exist
4. Creates or updates AICT digital assets
5. Synchronizes associated Product Models (CMDB records)
6. Establishes relationships across AI Systems, Models, Prompts, and Use Cases

### 1. Sync Model Assets

**Source**

- *Generative AI Model Configurations* `$sys_generative_ai_model_config$`

**Process**

- Iterate through all model configurations
- For each configuration:
  - Identify or create an AI Model Digital Asset `$alm_ai_model_digital_asset$`
  - Prepare model metadata using *NAAssetsStaticContent*
  - Update or create the associated AI Model Product Model `$cmdb_ai_model_product_model$`

## How to Use This in Implementation

To apply this in an implementation, ensure Now Assist is implemented in the organization so that AICT has a live configuration to discover assets from. The synchronization runs via the scheduled job "Sync Now Assist AI Assets," which iterates through Now Assist configuration tables and maps discovered assets into the AICT inventory. As part of this, the Sync Model Assets step reads from the Generative AI Model Configurations table (`sys_generative_ai_model_config`) and, for each configuration, identifies or creates an AI Model Digital Asset (`alm_ai_model_digital_asset`), prepares model metadata using NAAssetsStaticContent, and updates or creates the associated AI Model Product Model (`cmdb_ai_model_product_model`). Relying on this automation avoids manual asset entry and keeps the inventory aligned with deployed functionality; the synchronization also establishes relationships across AI Systems, Models, Prompts, and Use Cases.
