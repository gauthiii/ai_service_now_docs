## What Is This?

Default Configuration describes the out-of-the-box state of the Sync Now Assist AI Assets scheduled job within AI Control Tower's Now Assist governance. It explains that AI asset inventory synchronization works automatically without any additional setup for standard Now Assist deployments. This is the starting point for the broader "Configuration and Customization" area of Now Assist governance.

---

## Default Configuration

The Sync Now Assist AI Assets scheduled job is enabled by default. No additional setup is required for standard Now Assist deployments. The job runs automatically and maintains continuous inventory sync.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

For standard Now Assist deployments, no implementer action is required to enable AI asset inventory sync — the Sync Now Assist AI Assets scheduled job is already active and runs automatically to keep the inventory continuously up to date. Implementers should verify that the job remains enabled, since continuous sync underpins an accurate, current AI inventory for governance. Customization or deactivation of this job is only needed for non-standard scenarios, which are covered in the neighboring sections (Disabling Auto-Discovery, Customizing Sync Behavior).
