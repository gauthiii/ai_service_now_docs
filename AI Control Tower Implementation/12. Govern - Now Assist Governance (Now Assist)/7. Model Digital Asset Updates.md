> Section: Govern - Now Assist Governance (Now Assist) → Model Digital Asset Updates

---

## What Is This?

Model Digital Asset Updates identifies the pieces of model information that are updated and mapped onto an AI model's digital asset during Now Assist governance synchronization. It is part of mapping configuration and model information to the digital asset, ensuring that the governed model's supporting documentation and infrastructure details are captured in AI Control Tower.

---

## Items Updated on the Model Digital Asset

When updating a model's digital asset, the following items are updated:

- Model Card
- Required Infrastructure
- Evaluation Metrics Report

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

During synchronization that maps configuration and model information to a model's digital asset, an implementer ensures the digital asset reflects the current Model Card, Required Infrastructure, and Evaluation Metrics Report. Keep these three elements aligned with the source model so the governed digital asset stays accurate within AI Control Tower.
