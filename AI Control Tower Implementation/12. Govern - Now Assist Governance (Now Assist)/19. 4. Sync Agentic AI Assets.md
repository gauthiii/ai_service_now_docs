## What Is This?

This subtopic describes how agentic AI assets are synchronized as part of Now Assist governance. Agentic AI synchronization includes AI Tools, AI Agents, and AI Use Cases derived from Now Assist configurations. It fits into the broader asset sync process that brings Now Assist artifacts into the AI Control Tower governance model.

---

## Sync Agentic AI Assets

Agentic AI synchronization includes AI Tools, AI Agents, and AI Use Cases derived from Now Assist configurations.

### Sync AI Tools

- **Source:** `$sn_aia_tool$`
- **Target:** `$sn_ent_ai_tool$`

**Mapped Attributes**

- Name
- Description
- Type
- Active Status
- Topic

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing agentic AI governance, configure the synchronization to pull AI Tools from the source table `$sn_aia_tool$` into the target table `$sn_ent_ai_tool$`, mapping the listed attributes (Name, Description, Type, Active Status, Topic). This AI Tools sync is one of three agentic AI synchronizations (alongside AI Agents and AI Use Cases) that derive governance assets from Now Assist configurations. Ensure the source Now Assist tool configurations exist before running the sync so the mapped attributes populate correctly.
