## What Is This?

Relationship Mapping is part of the "Sync Prompt Assets" step within Now Assist governance synchronization. It defines the relationships that connect a synced prompt asset to other AI assets in the CMDB. Establishing these relationships ensures that prompts are correctly associated with the AI models and AI systems they belong to.

---

## Relationship Mapping

For prompt assets, the following relationships are mapped:

- AI Model Digital Asset
- Associated AI System Digital Assets

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

This mapping is applied during the Sync Prompt Assets process, after AI Prompt Digital Assets (`$alm_ai_prompt_digital_asset$`) and AI Prompt Product Models (`$cmdb_ai_prompt_product_model$`) are created or updated. When synchronizing prompts, link each prompt to its AI Model Digital Asset and to the Associated AI System Digital Assets so that the governance data reflects how prompts relate to the broader set of AI models and systems. Ensure the corresponding model and system digital assets already exist so the relationships can be established.
