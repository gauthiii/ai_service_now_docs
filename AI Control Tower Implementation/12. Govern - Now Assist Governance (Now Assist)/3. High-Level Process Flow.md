## What Is This?

This subtopic describes, at a high level, what the "Sync Now Assist AI Assets" scheduled job does when AI Control Tower (AICT) automatically synchronizes AI assets from Now Assist. It outlines the sequence of operations the synchronization process follows to discover, register, and relate AI assets in the AICT inventory. It fits within the broader Now Assist Governance flow, where AICT continuously keeps the AI inventory aligned with deployed Now Assist functionality.

---

## High-Level Process Flow

At a high level, the synchronization process:

1. Iterates through Now Assist configuration tables
2. Identifies AI assets and associated metadata
3. Determines whether corresponding AICT digital assets exist
4. Creates or updates AICT digital assets
5. Synchronizes associated Product Models (CMDB records)
6. Establishes relationships across AI Systems, Models, Prompts, and Use Cases

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

An implementer applies this by relying on the "Sync Now Assist AI Assets" scheduled job rather than entering AI assets manually. Understanding this six-step flow helps explain how AICT keeps its inventory accurate: the job reads Now Assist configuration tables, matches them to existing AICT digital assets (creating or updating as needed), keeps related CMDB Product Models in sync, and wires up the relationships between AI Systems, Models, Prompts, and Use Cases. Subsequent subtopics (for example, "Sync Model Assets") detail the specific source tables and processing steps for each asset type.
