## What Is This?

Sync Prompt Assets is a synchronization step in the Now Assist governance process that takes prompt configurations defined in Generative AI Configurations and creates or updates the corresponding AI Prompt Digital Assets and AI Prompt Product Models. It ensures prompt templates are represented as governed digital assets and related to the relevant AI systems and models within AI Control Tower.

---

## Source

- *Generative AI Configurations* `$sys_generative_ai_config$` (Prompt Templates)

---

## Process

- Iterate through prompt configurations
- For each prompt:
  - Identify or create an AI Prompt Digital Asset `$alm_ai_prompt_digital_asset$`
  - Update or create the associated AI Prompt Product Model `$cmdb_ai_prompt_product_model$`
  - Associate prompts with relevant AI systems and models

---

## Relationship Mapping

- AI Model Digital Asset
- Associated AI System Digital Assets

---

## Product Model Attributes

- Name
- Version
- Model Category

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

To implement prompt asset synchronization, read prompt template configurations from the `sys_generative_ai_config` table and, for each prompt, ensure an AI Prompt Digital Asset record exists in `alm_ai_prompt_digital_asset` and a matching AI Prompt Product Model exists in `cmdb_ai_prompt_product_model`. Populate the product model with the Name, Version, and Model Category attributes, and establish relationships to the AI Model Digital Asset and associated AI System Digital Assets so prompts are linked to the AI systems and models they belong to. This step depends on prompt configurations already being defined in Generative AI Configurations and feeds the broader Now Assist asset governance inventory.
