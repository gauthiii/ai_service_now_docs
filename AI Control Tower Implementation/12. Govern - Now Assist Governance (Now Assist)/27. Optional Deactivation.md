---

## What Is This?

Optional Deactivation is part of the Configuration Guidance for the AICT asset synchronization scheduled job. While the default recommendation is to keep the scheduled job enabled, this subtopic explains the alternative path: turning off automatic discovery. It clarifies the trade-off an implementer accepts if the job is deactivated.

---

## Optional Deactivation

If automatic discovery is not desired:

- The scheduled job may be deactivated
- Manual asset management will be required to maintain inventory completeness

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

If your organization does not want automatic discovery of AI assets, you can deactivate the scheduled synchronization job. Be aware that doing so shifts responsibility to manual asset management to keep the AICT inventory complete. By contrast, the default recommendation is to keep the scheduled job enabled to ensure continuous inventory accuracy, alignment between deployed AI capabilities and governance records, and reduced operational overhead.
