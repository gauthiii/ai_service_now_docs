## What Is This?

Prompt Sync is one of the AICT governance sync operations for Now Assist. It iterates over Generative AI Configurations (which contain Prompt Templates) and maps them to AI Prompt Digital Assets. After syncing the asset, AICT links the Prompt to the relevant AI Systems via the AI Model Digital Asset and AI System Digital Asset references, establishing governance visibility over prompts in use.

---

## What Prompt Sync Does

Iterates Generative AI Configurations (which contain Prompt Templates) and maps them to AI Prompt Digital Assets. After syncing the asset, AICT links the Prompt to the relevant AI Systems via the AI Model Digital Asset and AI System Digital Asset references.

### Records Updated and Fields Synced

| Record Updated | Fields Synced |
|---|---|
| AI Prompt Product Model | Name, Version, Model Category |
| AI System linkage | Updates AI Systems in AICT with associated Prompts using AI Model Digital Asset and AI System Digital Asset references |

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When Prompt Sync runs, ensure your Generative AI Configurations and their Prompt Templates are correctly defined, since these are the source of the AI Prompt Digital Assets. The sync populates the AI Prompt Product Model (Name, Version, Model Category) and then links prompts back to AI Systems through the AI Model Digital Asset and AI System Digital Asset references. This depends on the corresponding AI Model and AI System Digital Assets already existing so the linkage can resolve correctly, giving governance visibility into which prompts are associated with which AI Systems.
