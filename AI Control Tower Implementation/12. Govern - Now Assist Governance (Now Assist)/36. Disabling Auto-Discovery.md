---

## What Is This?

This subtopic explains how to deactivate the **Sync Now Assist AI Assets** scheduled job that drives automatic AI inventory discovery in AI Control Tower. It applies when an organization does not use Now Assist or maintains a separate inventory management process. It sits within the Configuration and Customization aspects of Now Assist governance and carries a strong recommendation against disabling.

---

## Disabling the Sync Now Assist AI Assets Scheduled Job

You may deactivate the scheduled job if your organization does not use Now Assist or has a separate inventory management process. To disable:

- Navigate to **System Definition > Scheduled Jobs**
- Locate **Sync Now Assist AI Assets**
- Set the job status to **Inactive**

**Recommendation:** Keep the scheduled job active. Disabling it means your AI inventory will no longer reflect deployed Now Assist assets, creating governance gaps and potential compliance risk.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

By default, the **Sync Now Assist AI Assets** scheduled job is enabled and maintains continuous inventory sync with no additional setup. Only disable auto-discovery when Now Assist is unused or inventory is managed elsewhere, since deactivation stops the AI inventory from reflecting deployed Now Assist assets and introduces governance gaps and compliance risk. If non-standard sync behavior is needed instead of full disablement, prefer customizing sync logic (extending `NowAssistAIAssetsUtil`) rather than turning the job off.
