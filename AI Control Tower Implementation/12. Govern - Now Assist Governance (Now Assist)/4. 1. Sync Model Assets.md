## What Is This?

Sync Model Assets is the first step of AICT's automated synchronization of Now Assist AI assets. As part of the "Sync Now Assist AI Assets" scheduled job, it discovers Now Assist Generative AI model configurations and registers them as AI Model Digital Assets and associated AI Model Product Models in the AICT inventory. This eliminates manual model-asset entry and keeps the inventory current with deployed Now Assist functionality.

---

## Sync Model Assets

### Source

- *Generative AI Model Configurations* `$sys_generative_ai_model_config$`

### Process

- Iterate through all model configurations
- For each configuration:
  - Identify or create an AI Model Digital Asset `$alm_ai_model_digital_asset$`
  - Prepare model metadata using *NAAssetsStaticContent*
  - Update or create the associated AI Model Product Model `$cmdb_ai_model_product_model$`

### Context: Automated AI Asset Synchronization

This subtopic operates within AICT's automated synchronization capability. When ServiceNow Now Assist is implemented, AICT can automatically discover and register AI assets used by Now Assist skills, eliminating manual asset entry and keeping the AI inventory accurate, current, and aligned with deployed AI functionality.

AICT maintains inventory accuracy through a scheduled job titled "Sync Now Assist AI Assets," which performs continuous synchronization by identifying and mapping AI assets generated or utilized by Now Assist skills into the AICT inventory. At a high level, the synchronization process:

1. Iterates through Now Assist configuration tables
2. Identifies AI assets and associated metadata
3. Determines whether corresponding AICT digital assets exist
4. Creates or updates AICT digital assets
5. Synchronizes associated Product Models (CMDB records)
6. Establishes relationships across AI Systems, Models, Prompts, and Use Cases

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

To apply this step, ensure Now Assist is implemented so that Generative AI model configurations exist in `sys_generative_ai_model_config`. The "Sync Now Assist AI Assets" scheduled job then iterates through these configurations and, for each one, creates or updates an AI Model Digital Asset (`alm_ai_model_digital_asset`), prepares model metadata via *NAAssetsStaticContent*, and creates or updates the linked AI Model Product Model (`cmdb_ai_model_product_model`). No manual model entry is required; verify that these tables are populated after the job runs to confirm models were synced correctly.
