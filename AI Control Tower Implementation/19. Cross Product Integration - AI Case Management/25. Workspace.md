## What Is This?

The Workspace for AI case management is the AI Risk and Compliance Workspace, where analysts monitor and manage AI inquiries. It provides a structured, dashboard-driven view of the AI inquiry workflow so that AI Risk and Compliance analysts and AI Stewards can track inquiries across their lifecycle. This is where the inquiry management process surfaces operationally inside ServiceNow.

---

## AI Risk and Compliance Workspace

The workspace is presented as the **AI Risk and Compliance Workspace** within ServiceNow. It is titled **AI risk and compliance management**, with the description: "Monitor and manage risks and compliance of multiple types of AI in your enterprise."

The workspace provides this dashboard to give a structured view of the AI inquiry workflow.

## How to Use This in Implementation

Implementers and analysts access the AI Risk and Compliance Workspace and navigate to the **AI cases → Inquiries** view to monitor and track AI inquiries. The dashboard surfaces inquiry volume broken down by state (Approved, In progress, New, Triage) and by priority (Critical through Planning), enabling analysts and AI Stewards to gauge workload and prioritize work. Use the **Create inquiry** action to add new inquiries, and rely on the state/priority breakdowns to manage inquiries through the workflow.
