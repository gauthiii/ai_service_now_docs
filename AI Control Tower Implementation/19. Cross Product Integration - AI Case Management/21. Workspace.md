## What Is This?

The Workspace is the AI Risk and Compliance Workspace used to monitor and manage AI risks and compliance across multiple types of AI in the enterprise. It provides dashboards with key metrics, trends, and actionable tasks for tracking AI risks, case activities, compliance tracking, and governance enforcement. Within AI Case Management, it surfaces the status, lists, and trends of AI cases.

---

## AI Risk and Compliance Workspace Dashboards

The workspace is titled **AI Risk and Compliance Workspace** and presents the **AI risk and compliance management** experience: "Monitor and manage risks and compliance of multiple types of AI in your enterprise." It is organized into the tabs **Risk & compliance**, **Operations**, and **AI cases**.

This dashboard provides the key metrics, trends, and actionable tasks that you can use to track your AI risks, case activities, compliance tracking, and governance enforcement.

## How to Use This in Implementation

Implementers use the AI Risk and Compliance Workspace as the operational front end for AI Case Management. Navigate to the **AI cases** tab to monitor case status by state and priority, review the summarized list of reported AI cases (with sub-type, case analyst, and priority), and create new AI cases via **Create AI case**. Use the summary tiles (All, Overdue, Due in 7 days, Unassigned) to triage workload, and the **Trends** view to analyze case subtypes (grouped by business dimension and filterable) and open/closed case patterns over time for compliance tracking and governance enforcement. All activity is captured in a full audit trail to support regulatory compliance.
