## Assessment Templates Overview

Assessment templates provide a structured, repeatable way to gather information during the investigation of reported AI cases. They ensure consistent data collection, support comprehensive analysis, and help maintain compliance with governance and regulatory standards. Standardized formats enable investigators to identify root causes, assess risks, and recommend corrective actions while keeping documentation audit-ready.

The AI Risk and Compliance content accelerator includes a pre-defined template — the **AI Case Assessment Questionnaire** — designed to support thorough investigation of reported AI cases. Additional examples include:

- **AI Bias Investigation Questionnaire** — captures data sources, bias detection methods, and remediation steps.
- **Model Performance Deviation Assessment** — evaluates causes of accuracy drops, drift, or unexpected outputs.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

To apply assessment templates, leverage the AI Risk and Compliance content accelerator, which ships with the pre-defined AI Case Assessment Questionnaire to support investigation of reported AI cases. Investigators use these standardized templates to collect consistent data, identify root causes, assess risks, and recommend corrective actions. For specialized investigations, additional templates such as the AI Bias Investigation Questionnaire and the Model Performance Deviation Assessment provide structured formats for capturing bias-related data and evaluating model accuracy or drift, ensuring documentation remains audit-ready and compliant with governance and regulatory standards.
