## Overview of AI Case/ Inquiry

AI Case Management provides a centralized system for managing AI-related cases and inquiries — from initial submission through investigation, resolution, and regulatory reporting. The solution is designed to improve response times, strengthen oversight, and ensure issues are resolved at their root cause.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Implementers should treat this overview as the framing definition for the AI Case Management solution: a single, centralized system covering the full case/inquiry lifecycle (submission, investigation, resolution, and regulatory reporting). Use it to set expectations for the solution's goals — faster response times, stronger oversight, and root-cause resolution — before moving on to the detailed key capabilities, roles and responsibilities, and solution architecture covered in the surrounding sections.
