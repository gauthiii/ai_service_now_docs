## What Is This?

This subtopic identifies who the AI Case Management Implementation Guide is written for. It frames the broader guide, which provides implementers with a structured, step-by-step approach to configure and operationalize an AI Case Management solution that supports effective AI governance. Knowing the intended audience helps the right stakeholders engage with the installation, configuration, and governance oversight of the solution.

---

## Intended Audience

The intended audience for the AI Case Management Implementation Guide would be:

- **Implementation Engineers / Solution Integrators** – responsible for installing and configuring the solution
- **AI Stewards** – overseeing solution deployment in alignment with organizational AI governance strategy

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Use this subtopic to confirm role alignment before starting the AI Case Management implementation. Implementation Engineers / Solution Integrators should own the hands-on installation and configuration tasks, while AI Stewards should be engaged to ensure the deployment aligns with the organization's AI governance strategy. Establishing these responsibilities early supports effective collaboration and sustained operational ownership of the solution.
