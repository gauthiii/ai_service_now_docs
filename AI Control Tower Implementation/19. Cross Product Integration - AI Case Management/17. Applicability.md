## What Is This?

Applicability configuration identifies the impacted and related areas that can be associated with a reported AI case or inquiry. These associations provide broader context for an AI case and support impact analysis. They should be configured according to enterprise preferences as part of AI Case Management setup.

---

## Applicability Configuration

Applicability configuration identifies the impacted and related areas that can be associated with a reported AI case or inquiry. These associations provide broader context, support impact analysis, and should be configured according to enterprise preferences.

The associations are grouped as follows:

| Association Type | Areas |
|------------------|-------|
| Impacted areas | User, Company, Entity, Location, and AI Asset |
| Related areas | Control, Citation, Policy, Control Objective, and Risk Event |
| Cause-related associations | Department, Entity, and User |

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When configuring AI Case Management, define the impacted and related areas that can be linked to a reported AI case or inquiry based on your enterprise preferences. Use the impacted areas (User, Company, Entity, Location, AI Asset) and related areas (Control, Citation, Policy, Control Objective, Risk Event) to give cases broader context and to enable impact analysis. Cause-related associations can additionally be linked to Department, Entity, and User to connect a case to its underlying causes.
