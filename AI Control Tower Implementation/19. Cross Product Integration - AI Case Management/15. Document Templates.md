## What Is This?

Document Templates are standardized, predefined document formats used within AI Case Management to ensure consistent data extraction for regulatory and audit reporting. They reduce manual effort, improve accuracy, and accelerate compliance submissions. Within the implementation, they provide reusable reporting formats that align with specific regulatory requirements and audit scopes.

---

## Document Templates

Standardized document templates ensure consistent data extraction for regulatory and audit reporting. They reduce manual effort, improve accuracy, and accelerate compliance submissions. Predefined formats align reports with specific regulatory requirements and audit scopes, and can be reused across similar reporting needs for greater efficiency.

Examples include a Regulatory Compliance Report Template such as EU AI Act conformity evidence, and an AI Model Bias Assessment Summary Template.

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing AI Case Management, configure document templates to standardize the documents generated for regulatory and audit reporting. Define predefined formats that map to specific regulatory requirements (for example, EU AI Act conformity evidence) and audit scopes so reports are consistent and reusable across similar reporting needs. Reusing these templates reduces manual effort, improves data extraction accuracy, and accelerates compliance submissions.
