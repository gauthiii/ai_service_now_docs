## What Is This?

This subtopic summarizes the core capabilities of the AI Case Management solution within the broader Cross Product Integration for AI governance. It outlines how AI-related cases and inquiries are intaken, investigated, tracked, and resolved. It fits early in the implementation as part of understanding the solution before configuration.

---

## Key Capabilities

The AI Case Management solution includes:

- A single intake point for all AI cases and inquiries submitted through the Employee Center Portal.
- Structured workflows for investigations and assessments.
- Evidence and observation collection.
- Regulatory violation tracking from initial report through to lodgement.
- Identification and resolution of issues in affected areas.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When planning an AI Case Management deployment, use these capabilities as the functional scope to configure: ensure the Employee Center Portal is set up as the single intake point, build the structured investigation and assessment workflows, enable evidence and observation collection, and establish regulatory violation tracking from initial report through lodgement so that issues in affected areas can be identified and resolved at their root cause.
