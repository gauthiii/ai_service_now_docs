## What Is This?

Inquiry Type is a configuration within AI Case Management that defines AI inquiry types to organize incoming inquiries into clear categories. It improves tracking, reporting, and routing of AI governance questions, and sits alongside Case Types as part of the Configurations area of the cross-product AI Case Management integration.

---

## Defining AI Inquiry Types

Defining AI inquiry types organizes incoming inquiries into clear categories for efficient tracking and resolution. It improves reporting accuracy, helps identify patterns in AI governance issues, and ensures inquiries are routed to the right teams with appropriate prioritization. This structure supports compliance monitoring, promotes consistent handling across AI-related questions, and adapts as regulatory landscapes evolve.

Common examples include Regulatory Requests, Data Privacy Clarification, and Bias Testing Documentation.

## How to Use This in Implementation

When implementing AI Case Management, configure inquiry types as a categorization taxonomy for incoming AI governance inquiries, in parallel with Case Types. Establish categories such as Regulatory Requests, Data Privacy Clarification, and Bias Testing Documentation so that inquiries can be tracked, reported on, prioritized, and routed to the correct teams. Maintaining well-defined inquiry types supports compliance monitoring and consistent handling, and should be revisited as regulatory requirements change. Follow the linked configuration steps to create and manage the inquiry type records.
