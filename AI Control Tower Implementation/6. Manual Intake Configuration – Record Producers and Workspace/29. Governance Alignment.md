---

## What Is This?

Governance Alignment is a configuration consideration within the manual intake setup for record producers and workspaces. It ensures that the required fields captured during AI intake align with governance, risk, and compliance expectations and that data remains consistent across the related AI asset records. This anchors the intake experience to broader AI Risk & Compliance and regulatory requirements.

---

## Governance Alignment Requirements

- Ensure required fields align with:
  - AI Risk & Compliance requirements
  - Regulatory frameworks (EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF)
- Maintain consistency across:
  - Product Model
  - Digital Asset
  - AI System of record

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When configuring record producers and workspace intake forms, map the required fields so they satisfy AI Risk & Compliance requirements and align with regulatory frameworks such as the EU AI Act and NIST AI RMF. Validate that the field values stay consistent across the Product Model, Digital Asset, and AI System of record so that the same governance data is represented faithfully across all linked records.
