## What Is This?

This subtopic describes the capabilities available through the AI Control Tower Workspace as part of Option II: Workspace-Based Intake. The workspace provides a direct, role-based interface for asset creation and management, and these capabilities define what asset types users can add and manage directly within it.

---

## Workspace Capabilities

Add and manage:

- AI Systems
- AI Models
- AI Datasets

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Within the AI Control Tower Workspace (Option II: Workspace-Based Intake), users with the required roles use these capabilities to directly add and manage AI Systems, AI Models, and AI Datasets. This requires the roles called out for workspace-based intake — AI Asset Owner (`sn_ai_asset_mgmt.ai_asset_owner`) and AI Steward / AI CoE (`sn_ai_governance.ai_steward`) — to access and operate the workspace interface.
