## What Is This?

Form 1: Model Form is the first of three primary form components that make up the Intake Form Architecture in AI Control Tower. It captures the information required to create Product Models for AI assets. Together with the Asset Form and the Related Assets Form, it is orchestrated through a stepper-based intake experience.

---

## Intake Form Architecture Context

The Intake-form is composed of three primary Form components, orchestrated through a stepper-based experience. A single configurable page supports multiple asset types through parameter-driven rendering, ensuring scalability and maintainability.

The three Form Components are:

- Form 1: Model Form
- Form 2: Asset Form
- Form 3: Related Assets Form

---

## Form 1: Model Form

Captures information required to create Product Models:

| Product Model | Table |
| --- | --- |
| AI System Component Product Model | `cmdb_ai_system_component_product_model` |
| AI Model Product Model | `cmdb_ai_model_product_model` |
| AI Dataset Product Model | `cmdb_ai_dataset_product_model` |
| AI Prompt Product Model | `cmdb_ai_prompt_product_model` |

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When configuring manual intake, the Model Form is used to capture the Product Model layer of each AI asset. Implementers map intake fields to the four product model tables (`cmdb_ai_system_component_product_model`, `cmdb_ai_model_product_model`, `cmdb_ai_dataset_product_model`, `cmdb_ai_prompt_product_model`). Because the intake page is parameter-driven, the same configurable page renders the correct model form per asset type. In the stepper experience, the Model Form is combined with the Asset Form within the Details Step, so it should be configured in coordination with Form 2 (Asset Form) and Form 3 (Related Assets Form).
