---

## What Is This?

This subtopic is one of the Implementation Considerations for the AI Asset Inventory (AICT). It defines how AI assets and their relationships should be structured so that systems, models, datasets, and prompts are explicitly tracked and consistently mapped. It ensures the inventory data model supports lineage, governance separation, and alignment with the CMDB.

---

## Data Model and Asset Relationships

- Enforce structured mapping between:
  - AI System → AI Model → Dataset
- Maintain explicit relationship tracking:
  - System ↔ Models
- Models ↔ Datasets
  - Systems ↔ Prompts
- Enable model lineage tracking (training, evaluation datasets, versions)
- Maintain separation between:
  - Digital Assets (inventory layer)
  - AI System of Record (governance layer)
- Align with CMDB product model architecture for consistency

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When configuring the AI Asset Inventory, model your records so that AI Systems link down to AI Models and Datasets, and maintain bidirectional relationships (System ↔ Models, Models ↔ Datasets, Systems ↔ Prompts). Capture model lineage—training and evaluation datasets and versions—to support traceability. Keep a clear separation between the inventory layer (Digital Assets) and the governance layer (AI System of Record), and align the data model with the CMDB product model architecture so relationships stay consistent across the platform.
