## What Is This?

This subtopic defines the discovery and integration strategy for populating the AI Control Tower inventory. It combines multiple discovery mechanisms with enterprise system integration to ensure AI assets, their metadata, and relationships are captured comprehensively. It complements manual intake by providing automated and connector-based coverage.

---

## Discovery and Integration

- Combine:
  - Auto-discovery (Now Assist, AI Agents)
  - Service Graph Connectors (external platforms)
  - Manual intake (gap coverage)
- Integrate with enterprise systems:
  - ITSM, CRM, HR, Vendor Management
- Ensure discovery captures:
  - Metadata
  - Relationships

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Implementers should adopt a layered discovery approach: rely on auto-discovery for Now Assist and AI Agents, use Service Graph Connectors to pull in assets from external platforms, and apply manual intake to cover any remaining gaps. Integration with enterprise systems (ITSM, CRM, HR, Vendor Management) extends coverage across the organization. Ensure that discovery mechanisms capture both asset metadata and the relationships between assets so the inventory remains complete and connected.
