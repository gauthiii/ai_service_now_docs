## What Is This?

This subtopic is one of the best-practice / guidance items in the Manual Intake Configuration section, focused on enforcing lifecycle and governance discipline across AI assets. It ensures that every AI asset moves through defined lifecycle stages, is automatically governed, and is properly linked to risk, compliance, and case records. It fits into implementation as the governance enforcement layer that keeps manually intaked AI assets audit-ready and compliant.

---

## Lifecycle and Governance Enforcement

- Align all assets to lifecycle stages:
  - Assess → Build → Test → Deploy → Monitor → Retire
- Ensure AI Asset Governance Details are automatically created
- Link assets to:
  - Risk assessments
  - Compliance controls
  - AI cases and inquiries
- Enforce state transitions through governance workflows
- Ensure readiness for:
  - Audit
  - Risk reviews
  - Regulatory reporting

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

To apply this, configure governance so that AI assets are mapped to the six lifecycle stages (Assess, Build, Test, Deploy, Monitor, Retire) and ensure AI Asset Governance Details records are generated automatically rather than manually. Establish relationships from each asset to its risk assessments, compliance controls, and related AI cases and inquiries. Use governance workflows to enforce valid state transitions between lifecycle stages, which in turn keeps the assets ready for audit, risk reviews, and regulatory reporting.
