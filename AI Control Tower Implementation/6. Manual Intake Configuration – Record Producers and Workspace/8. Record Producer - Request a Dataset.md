---

## What Is This?

This intake form captures datasets used in training and evaluation. It is one of the manual intake record producers in AI Control Tower, enabling users to register AI datasets along with their digital asset, product model, and governance details. It establishes the dataset's lifecycle and traceability without creating a Configuration Item (CI).

---

## Key System Actions

- **Creates** AI Dataset Digital Asset
  ```
  [alm_ai_dataset_digital_asset]
  ```
  - Defaults category to AI Dataset
  - Captures dataset relationships

- **Creates** AI Dataset Product Model
  ```
  [cmdb_ai_dataset_product_model]
  ```
  - Establishes dataset attributes and lifecycle state
  - Links product model to digital asset

- **Creates** AI Asset Governance Details
  - Enables lifecycle governance and traceability

> **Note:** No Configuration Item (CI) is created during intake

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Use this record producer to onboard datasets used in training and evaluation into AI Control Tower. Submitting the form creates the AI Dataset Digital Asset (`alm_ai_dataset_digital_asset`, defaulted to the AI Dataset category) and the AI Dataset Product Model (`cmdb_ai_dataset_product_model`), linking the product model to the digital asset and setting dataset attributes and lifecycle state. It also creates AI Asset Governance Details to enable lifecycle governance and traceability. Note that intake does not create a Configuration Item (CI), so implementers should not expect a CMDB CI record from this flow.
