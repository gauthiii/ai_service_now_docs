---

## What Is This?

The "Request an AI Model" record producer is a manual intake form within AI Control Tower (AICT) used to create and govern AI models independently of AI systems. Submitting it generates the supporting digital asset, product model, and governance records that enable lifecycle tracking of an AI model within AICT.

---

## Purpose

This intake form creates and governs AI models independently of systems.

---

## Key System Actions

- **Creates** AI Model Digital Asset

  ```
  [alm_ai_model_digital_asset]
  ```

  - Defaults category to AI Model
  - Captures datasets (training and evaluation)

- **Creates** AI Model Product Model

  ```
  [cmdb_ai_model_product_model]
  ```

  - Establishes model attributes and lifecycle state
  - Links product model to digital asset (many-to-one relationship)

- **Creates** AI Asset Governance Details
  - Enables lifecycle tracking within AICT

---

## Note

- No Configuration Item (CI) is created during intake

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When an implementer needs to onboard an AI model into governance separately from an AI system, use this record producer. On submission it automatically provisions an AI Model Digital Asset (`alm_ai_model_digital_asset`, defaulted to the AI Model category and capturing training/evaluation datasets), an AI Model Product Model (`cmdb_ai_model_product_model`, holding model attributes and lifecycle state and linked many-to-one to the digital asset), and AI Asset Governance Details to enable lifecycle tracking within AICT. Note that no Configuration Item (CI) is created as part of this intake, so do not expect a CMDB CI to appear from this flow.
