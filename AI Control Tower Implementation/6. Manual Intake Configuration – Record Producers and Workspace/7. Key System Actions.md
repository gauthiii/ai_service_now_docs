## What Is This?

This subtopic describes the key system actions triggered by the "Record Producer: Request an AI Model" intake form. This intake form creates and governs AI models independently of systems. The actions establish the records and governance details needed to track AI models within AICT.

---

## Record Producer: Request an AI Model

This intake form creates and governs AI models independently of systems.

### Key System Actions

- **Creates** — AI Model Digital Asset

  ```
  [alm_ai_model_digital_asset]
  ```

  - Defaults category to AI Model
  - Captures datasets (training and evaluation)

- **Creates** — AI Model Product Model

  ```
  [cmdb_ai_model_product_model]
  ```

  - Establishes model attributes and lifecycle state
  - Links product model to digital asset (many-to-one relationship)

- **Creates** — AI Asset Governance Details

  - Enables lifecycle tracking within AICT

### Note

No Configuration Item (CI) is created during intake

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When an implementer configures the "Request an AI Model" record producer, submitting the intake form automatically creates three records: an AI Model Digital Asset (`alm_ai_model_digital_asset`, defaulted to the AI Model category and capturing training/evaluation datasets), an AI Model Product Model (`cmdb_ai_model_product_model`, establishing model attributes and lifecycle state with a many-to-one link to the digital asset), and AI Asset Governance Details that enable lifecycle tracking within AICT. Note that no Configuration Item (CI) is created during this intake, so do not expect a CI to govern AI models created through this form.
