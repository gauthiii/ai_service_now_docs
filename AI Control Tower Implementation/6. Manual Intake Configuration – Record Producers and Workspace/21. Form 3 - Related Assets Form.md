## What Is This?

Form 3 (the Related Assets Form) is the third of three primary form components that make up the manual Intake-form experience, orchestrated through a stepper-based experience. It captures the relationships between AI assets, linking together the Product Models and Digital Assets created in Form 1 and Form 2. It fits into the intake architecture as the component responsible for establishing how AI systems, models, datasets, and prompts relate to one another.

---

## Related Assets Form

Form 3: Related Assets Form captures relationships between AI assets:

- AI Systems → associated Models and Datasets
- AI Models → associated Datasets
- Datasets → base datasets
- AI Systems of record → associated Models, Datasets, and Prompts

### Context Within Intake Form Architecture

The Intake-form is composed of three primary Form components, orchestrated through a stepper-based experience. The Related Assets Form (Form 3) is the third of these components, following:

- **Form 1: Model Form** — Captures information required to create Product Models.
- **Form 2: Asset Form** — Captures information required to create Digital Assets.

A single configurable page supports multiple asset types through parameter-driven rendering, ensuring scalability and maintainability.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When configuring the manual intake experience, implement the Related Assets Form as the third form component in the stepper. It depends on the records produced by Form 1 (Product Models) and Form 2 (Digital Assets), since it defines the relationships among those AI assets. Use it to wire up the four relationship types listed above so that AI systems, models, datasets, and prompts are correctly associated for downstream governance and tracking.
