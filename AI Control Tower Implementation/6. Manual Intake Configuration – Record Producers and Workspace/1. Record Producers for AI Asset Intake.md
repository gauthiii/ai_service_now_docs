## What Is This?

Record Producers are Service Catalog items that enable controlled, manual onboarding of AI assets into the AI Control Tower AI Asset Inventory. They facilitate standardized intake of AI assets via the Service Catalog under the Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) category. This subtopic sits within the broader Manual Intake Configuration capability, which combines Record Producers with Workspace-based intake mechanisms to support structured ingestion of AI Systems, Models, Datasets, and Prompts.

---

## Manual Intake Configuration Context

To support controlled onboarding of AI assets, AI Control Tower provides Record Producers (Service Catalog) and Workspace-based intake mechanisms. These capabilities enable structured ingestion of AI Systems, Models, Datasets, and Prompts into the AI Asset Inventory.

## How to Use This in Implementation

To enable manual intake of AI assets, implementers leverage the predefined Record Producers available in the Service Catalog under the Governance, Risk, and Compliance category. Direct users to the **AI assets** catalog page (reached via Home > Technology services > AI assets) where they can select the appropriate Record Producer to request AI use cases, raise inquiries, report AI cases, request datasets, or request AI models. These Record Producers standardize the structured ingestion of AI Systems, Models, Datasets, and Prompts into the AI Asset Inventory, ensuring controlled and governed onboarding alongside Workspace-based intake mechanisms.
