---

## What Is This?

Reporting and Observability defines how AI Control Tower surfaces visibility into the AI assets captured through manual intake. It covers enabling dashboards, filtering, export and reporting, and end-to-end traceability so that governance, compliance, and audit teams can monitor the AI estate. It fits at the stage where intake data is being consumed and reported on across the organization.

---

## Reporting and Observability Requirements

**Enable dashboards for:**

- AI system inventory
- Risk exposure
- Model usage and performance

**Provide filtering by:**

- Business unit
- Risk level
- AI type

**Support export and reporting for:**

- Regulatory compliance
- Internal audits

**Ensure traceability from:**

- Input → Model → Output → Decision

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

Implementers should configure dashboards that report on the AI system inventory, risk exposure, and model usage and performance captured through intake. Enable filtering across business unit, risk level, and AI type so stakeholders can scope views to their needs. Provide export and reporting capabilities to satisfy regulatory compliance and internal audit requirements, and ensure the data model preserves traceability across the full Input → Model → Output → Decision chain.
