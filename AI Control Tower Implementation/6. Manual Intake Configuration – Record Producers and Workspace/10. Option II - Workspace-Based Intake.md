## What Is This?

Option II is the second approach to manual intake of AI assets in AI Control Tower. The AI Control Tower Workspace provides a direct, role-based interface for asset creation and management. Unlike the Record Producer (Service Catalog) approach, this option lets authorized users add and manage AI assets directly within the workspace.

---

## AI Control Tower Workspace

The AI Control Tower Workspace provides a direct, role-based interface for asset creation and management.

### Roles Required

- **AI Asset Owner** `[sn_ai_asset_mgmt.ai_asset_owner]`
- **AI Steward / AI CoE** `[sn_ai_governance.ai_steward]`

### Capabilities

- Add and manage:
  - AI Systems
  - AI Models
  - AI Datasets

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

To enable this intake path, grant users the required roles: AI Asset Owner (`sn_ai_asset_mgmt.ai_asset_owner`) for asset ownership, and AI Steward / AI CoE (`sn_ai_governance.ai_steward`) for governance oversight. Once roles are assigned, authorized users can add and manage AI Systems, AI Models, and AI Datasets directly through the AI Control Tower Workspace rather than via the Service Catalog record producers described in Option I.
