---

## What Is This?

This subtopic lists the roles required to use Option II: Workspace-Based Intake. The AI Control Tower Workspace provides a direct, role-based interface for asset creation and management. To create and manage assets through this workspace, a user must hold the appropriate AI Asset Owner or AI Steward / AI CoE role.

---

## Roles Required

Option II (Workspace-Based Intake) requires the following roles:

| Role | Role Identifier |
|------|-----------------|
| AI Asset Owner | `sn_ai_asset_mgmt.ai_asset_owner` |
| AI Steward / AI CoE | `sn_ai_governance.ai_steward` |

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When enabling the AI Control Tower Workspace as a direct, role-based interface for asset creation and management, assign the `sn_ai_asset_mgmt.ai_asset_owner` role to AI Asset Owners and the `sn_ai_governance.ai_steward` role to AI Stewards / AI CoE members. Users holding these roles can then add and manage assets directly through the workspace rather than through a record producer intake form.
