## What Is This?

Data Quality and Standardization is a best-practice item within the Manual Intake Configuration guidance for AI Control Tower. It defines how implementers keep AI asset records consistent, well-categorized, and free of bad data when assets are captured through the manual intake (Record Producers and Workspace) experience. It ensures intake data remains reliable for downstream governance, lifecycle, and reporting processes.

---

## Data Quality and Standardization Requirements

- Enforce consistent:
  - Naming conventions
  - Versioning standards
  - Provider attribution
- Standardize model categories and asset classifications
- Implement validation rules to prevent:
  - Duplicate assets
  - Incomplete records

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When configuring manual intake, define and enforce standardized naming conventions, versioning standards, and provider attribution so that all captured AI assets are consistent. Standardize the model categories and asset classifications used across intake forms, and add validation rules to the intake configuration that block duplicate assets and reject incomplete records before they are saved. Applying these controls at intake keeps the AI asset inventory clean and dependable for the lifecycle, governance, and regulatory processes that consume it.
