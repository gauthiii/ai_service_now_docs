## What Is This?

This subtopic introduces the set of Record Producers that AI Control Tower exposes through the Service Catalog for standardized intake of AI assets. Record Producers facilitate intake of AI assets via the Service Catalog under the Governance, Risk, and Compliance category. They provide the catalog request entry points that an implementer uses to onboard AI Systems, Models, Datasets, and related requests into the AI Asset Inventory.

---

## Available Record Producers

Record Producers facilitate standardized intake of AI assets via the Service Catalog (Governance, Risk, and Compliance category).

The "AI assets" catalog page is the entry point for these requests. It is described as: "Use this page to request AI assets, seek guidance, or report concerns."

The following Record Producers (catalog requests) are available on the AI assets page:

| Record Producer | Description |
|-----------------|-------------|
| Request an AI use case | Request the development or procurement of an AI system |
| Raise an AI inquiry | Submit general inquiry to AI Center of Excellence team |
| Report an AI case | Report an AI case to address issues, improve accuracy, or ensure compliance |
| Request a dataset | Request a dataset to analyze trends, train models, or drive data-driven decisions |
| Request an AI model | Request the development or procurement of an AI model |

## How to Use This in Implementation

An implementer locates these Record Producers in the Service Catalog under the Governance, Risk, and Compliance category, surfaced on the "AI assets" catalog page within the Technology services portal. Each producer corresponds to a specific intake action — requesting an AI use case, raising an AI inquiry, reporting an AI case, requesting a dataset, or requesting an AI model — and channels structured submissions into the AI Asset Inventory and to the AI Center of Excellence team. Use the filter and sort controls on the page to navigate the available requests, and select the appropriate card based on the asset or concern being onboarded.
