## What Is This?

Form 2: Asset Form is one of the three primary form components that make up the manual Intake-form, orchestrated through a stepper-based experience. It captures the information required to create Digital Assets for the AI assets being onboarded. It complements Form 1 (Model Form) and Form 3 (Related Assets Form) within the intake architecture.

---

## Asset Form Overview

The Asset Form captures information required to create **Digital Assets**.

The digital asset types and their backing tables are:

| Digital Asset | Table |
|---|---|
| AI System Digital Asset | `alm_ai_system_digital_asset` |
| AI Model Digital Asset | `alm_ai_model_digital_asset` |
| AI Dataset Digital Asset | `alm_ai_dataset_digital_asset` |
| AI Prompt Digital Asset | `alm_ai_prompt_digital_asset` |

### Context Within the Intake Form Architecture

The Intake-form is composed of three primary Form components, orchestrated through a stepper-based experience:

- **Form 1: Model Form** — Captures information required to create Product Models (e.g., `cmdb_ai_system_component_product_model`, `cmdb_ai_model_product_model`, `cmdb_ai_dataset_product_model`, `cmdb_ai_prompt_product_model`).
- **Form 2: Asset Form** — Captures information required to create Digital Assets (this subtopic).
- **Form 3: Related Assets Form** — Captures relationships between AI assets.

In the Stepper-Based User Experience, the **Details Step** combines the Model Form and Asset Form.

A single configurable page supports multiple asset types through parameter-driven rendering, ensuring scalability and maintainability.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When configuring manual intake, implementers use the Asset Form to capture the data needed to create Digital Asset records across the four AI digital asset tables (`alm_ai_system_digital_asset`, `alm_ai_model_digital_asset`, `alm_ai_dataset_digital_asset`, `alm_ai_prompt_digital_asset`). Because the page is parameter-driven, the same configurable page renders the appropriate asset type, so the form does not need to be rebuilt per type. The Asset Form is presented alongside the Model Form within the Details Step of the stepper-based intake experience.
