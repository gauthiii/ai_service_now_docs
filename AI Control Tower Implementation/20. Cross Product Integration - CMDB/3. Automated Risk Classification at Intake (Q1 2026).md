## What Is This?

Automated Risk Classification at Intake is a Q1 2026 capability that embeds risk assessment directly into the AI asset intake form. It auto-flags high-risk or non-compliant submissions for review so that configuration items (CIs) entering the CMDB carry an initial risk classification from the moment they are created. It fits into the CMDB intake/onboarding flow as a way to apply governance at the point of entry rather than as a later, separate step.

---

## Automated Risk Classification at Intake

Risk assessment questions are now embedded in the intake form. High-risk or non-compliant submissions are auto-flagged for AI Steward review, ensuring CIs entering the CMDB carry an initial risk classification from day one—without requiring a separate triage step.

---

## How to Use This in Implementation

When configuring AI asset intake (the form-based Manual Intake path through the AICT Workspace), ensure the embedded risk assessment questions are completed so submissions can be evaluated automatically. Submissions identified as high-risk or non-compliant are routed to an AI Steward for review. The benefit for implementers is that newly created CIs arrive in the CMDB pre-classified with an initial risk rating, removing the need for a separate, manual triage step before governance can begin.
